why ccg?
growth. collaboration. appreciation. at catalyst consulting group, inc. we are pleased to maintain a diverse team of technologists and creatives, and though each member of our team fills a role, any of us are happy to tell you we are not defined by our job titles. working in small groups offers each of us the opportunity to branch out and take on multiple responsibilities. it also allows for us to consistently learn, grow and expand our own skill sets. bouncing ideas off one another and learning from each other is something that happens in the catalyst office every day. and while developing authentic connections with our clients is the foundation of our business, we would not be the company we are today without the strong connections we have built internally.
we offer great benefits including medical, dental and vision; 401k, short and long term disability, and life insurance; paid vacation, sick pay and paid holidays; company events.
role specifics
we have an immediate opening for a jr. wordpress & php developer to join our dev team that builds and maintains client websites. the ideal candidate has a strong analytical sense and attention to detail, as well as a strong work ethic. he or she should be organized and able to follow directions, problem-solve, and work in a fun team environment.  
you will research, design, build, test, deploy and maintain the company's customer facing production websites. your knowledge of web development combined with programming skill to assist in optimizing the company's current sites, implementing new features and fixing bugs. 
key responsibilities

use front-end coding (html / css / javascript) to replicate provided website design comps, with close attention to detail 
managing client websites and keeping them up-to-date
respond to client requests for website features and support 
innovate new ideas with our team 

 position requirements:

wordpress general concepts and structure
good understanding of php frameworks
experience using jira (or similar project tracking software)
experience with html, css (sass knowledge is a plus)
familiarity with managing dns and web hosting
2+ years css, css3, html, html5, javascript
using developer tools in the browser
media queries and responsive design
general concepts and structures of grid systems
how to research and solve css puzzles

 bonus skills

wordpress theme and plugin development 
familiarity with the linux command line
experience working with aws

knowledge of the following a plus:

jira administration
linux systems administration skills
source control (cvs, subversion, git)
agile/scrum development
mysql / other databases
sql
adobe creative suite

*us citizens and those authorized to work in the u.s. are encouraged to apply. we are unable to sponsor at this time.
