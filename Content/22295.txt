tech staff, software engineer – data path
apply now
job id r24752 date posted 12/05/2017 location hopkinton,  massachusetts
why work at dell?endless challenges and rewards. opportunities on six continents. a team of colleagues fueled by collaboration. all this, and a company deeply committed to integrity and responsibility.the dell emc storage business is the industry leader in enterprise and midrange storage systems.  our products and solutions are developed in deep collaboration with our dell technology family members including vmware and cloud foundry, with our family of dell emc storage businesses and with strategically aligned partners and vendors.the midrange organization is focused on innovating storage and compute solutions that enable our customers to leverage the benefits of key emerging technologies and industry trends, including nvme, nvme over fabric, and storage class memories.  our customers are driving towards flexible consumption models that converge block, file, compute, and networking; while leveraging cloud based analytics and machine learning to drive optimizations and low-touch management.our innovative products are delivered using reality-based agile methodology and test-driven development with continuous integration and delivery.  we use modern tools such as git, artificatory, jenkins, and slack for collaboration.  we integrate, contribute, and leverage oss components and interfaces at the edge of our product architectures so our engineers can focus on core ip.  this approach allows us to deploy our assets in a software defined way.  our assets are all containerized to enable us to quickly extend product features and capabilities, and gives us the ability to instantiate our solutions on cots servers, custom hw, and in cloud environments.we are looking for a storage systems data path developers to join our next gen data path team.   we are looking for candidates with experience in one or more areas that include;thin storage designio stack and filesystem experiencecaching architectures leveraging emerging media including nvme and storage class memorieshigh availability and cache coherency protocolsdata reduction techniquesdevelopment experience10-15 years experience with data path storage systemsmulticore developmentlinux user space
