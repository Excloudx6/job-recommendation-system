data engineer

        at marqeta
      
(view all jobs)

        oakland, california
      


we are seeking a data engineer that wakes up every morning thinking about how to make data use and data solutions more efficient, thrives on leveraging open source tools and enjoys working in a team environment. the right person for this role has a passion for all things data, and has begun to develop the technical skills to follow that passion. a data engineer in the data insights, visualization and analytics team is expected to be a problem solver for marqeta’s growing data needs, be able to contribute to projects in a timely manner while juggling multiple priorities, and proactively ideate with the broader team.
 
responsibilities:
help develop a data environment consistent with industry best practices and cutting edge approaches. develop, maintain and support a nimble data access solution able to both provide ready access to existing data features and incorporate new features in a timely manner. operate in time-sensitive environment with competing deadlines, by appropriately prioritizing the short and long term paths based on an agile development process.
 
our ideal candidate has:

2+ years experience designing, implementing and maintaining relational / data warehousing environments (custom or structured etl, preferably working directly with large data environments)
familiarity with data warehousing concepts (e.g. dimensional data modeling) and schema design (e.g. star, snowflake, etc.)
experience building and launching efficient & reliable data pipelines to move data (both large and small amounts) throughout data warehouse.
experience in redshift sql(postgresql) or mysql, with ability to optimize queries for high volume environments
experience in building large-scale, distributed systems using python, java or scala.
experience with git, github, and the pull request workflow
experience with shell scripting/crontab
positive outlook towards work, strong work ethic and ability to work in a team environment

 
bonus points for:

experience in big data frameworks (such as hadoop, hive, kafka, spark is a plus)
experience in data modeling for complicated business problems, designing etl / data pipeline solutions (such as talend/python preferred), and working in columnar databases (amazon redshift preferred)
experience in payments (good domain knowledge on financial services industry)
previous work with nosql dbs such as dynamodb, cassandra, mongodb, hbase

 
perks:

be an early member of a team driven data usage and analytics in a create startup
leverage modern tools and languages to further your career growth
competitive salary and benefits package with meaningful equity
great oakland location close to public transportation
casual work environment
a ready supply of drinks and snacks (cold brew coffee on tap!!)
