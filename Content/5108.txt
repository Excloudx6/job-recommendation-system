time series data scientist

        at datarobot
      
(view all jobs)

        boston
      


we are looking for a data scientist to lead the expansion of time-series capabilities in our disruptive predictive analytics platform. you will understand the needs of customers in industries like finance and manufacturing,. you will then collaborate to design solutions with our team of world-class data scientists, software engineers, and an executive team that is passionate about data science. you will gather product vision, ideas, and requirements from both external and internal sources, create and maintain a dynamic roadmap, and work with an enthusiastic development team to deliver our customers’ dream product.
the technical product manager is expected to:

work directly with the executive team to define the product strategy and roadmap for time series applications
solicit and capture product ideas from both outside and inside the company
deliver product requirement documents with prioritized features and corresponding justification
act as an effective liaison between engineering/data science and marketing/sales teams
maintain the product roadmap for time series capabilities and deliver to clients and sales teams as necessary
be an expert with respect to the competition
act as a thought leader within the company

requirements

deep experience building predictive models for time series problems using both classical statistical techniques (e.g. arima) and machine learning (e.g. xgboost)
4+ years programming experience in python or r
excellent teamwork and written/verbal communication skills
technical background in software and/or data analysis
proven ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority

plus

experience with deep learning models for time series modeling
experience with applications to the internet of things (iot) or finance
experience operating on large, high-frequency datasets in frameworks like spark.
experience as product manager for big data, hadoop, data integration, and/or predictive modeling space
advanced degrees in a relevant field (such as statistics or computer science) or mba
experience in agile product management using jira and confluence
experience in a fast paced startup environment
