hedis analyst

mcci medical group
     -
san antonio, tx


duties:works directly and report to national director of stars, hedis, mra to plan, organize, coach, and provide overall support for hedis quality initiatives and any projects required.under minimal supervision, assist or directly oversees and monitors the development, implementation, and coordination of meetings or committees to ensure progress toward goals and overall objectives are being met.coordinating and completing hedis quality specific projects ensuring consistency with mcci’s strategy, commitments, and goals.serves as a consultant, educator, and policy changes in the use of hedis data and the quality improvement process.performs studies as related to trends identified via hedis results, utilization review activities, utilization patterns, audits, etc.develops performance improvement targets for quality and efficiency measures, and makes recommendations to medical offices, affiliated and wholly-owned.proactively seek resources to identify gaps on existing or new projects as they arise.audit patient charts at provider offices, onsite or remotely as needed.analyze trend reports and performs other analyses to assist with documentation.periodically examines provider records to confirm compliance with hedis and ncqa standards.regularly monitors, follows-up, and documents case information.assists in development of training materials, templates, processes, and procedures related to the project.serves as resource and liaison for multiple associates to complete assignments.collaborates with teams regarding work flow process improvement, document management, and abstraction education.major responsibilities:collect and organizing information obtained from various sources to recognize trends in hedis and other quality data, performing and managing tasks in all phases of a project including research, data collection, data analysis and production of draft proposals and reports.provide analytical and managerial support for various projects as needed.required to have extensive familiarity with key elements:serve as the expert in hedis and stars for the expansion marketprovide regular, comprehensive literature and job aids of findings and national guidelines that will assist in the development and support of programs.complete ad hoc reports as required for development of trend report and metrics to support hedis quality programs and initiatives of obtaining the highest possible star rating.skills:must be able to work independently on multiple projects in coordination with other internal and external project team members.ability to present comprehensive analytic results orally and in writing to medical offices, plans, and providers incorporating descriptions of measures, relevant data, and other background material to provide context for the results.experience analyzing large datasets, particularly health-related data including claims data.must have excel skills in the intermediate to advanced range (pivot tables, v lookup, h lookup, conditional formatting.detail oriented, proficient computer skills with experience in database, powerpoint, excel, word and other ms office applications. sql skills preferred. public speaking and group presentation skills required. bachelor’s degree in health services is preferred. bilingual is a plus.travel to offices within mcci’s expansion market required, up to 25%-50% or as business needs arise.job type: full-timejob location:san antonio, txrequired experience:hedis & stars: 1 year
