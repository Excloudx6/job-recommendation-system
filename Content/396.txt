overviewas a software engineering intern, you’ll work alongside our experienced software engineers on the development, delivery, support and enhancements of our trading systems and infrastructure. you will have the chance to work on challenging projects that will be pushed into production. in addition, throughout the summer you’ll meet with senior technologists to learn about sig technology and how it impacts trading.our interns have:created a suite of tools to improve system efficiency using  performance dataimproved the visual display of market data in python/c#/f#contributed to the development of high performance low level c++ code for handling the first steps of our market data processesconducted technology research projects related to scale and performance with open source toolscontributed to studies of equity trading and markets by developing and using large sets of trade and price datawhat we're looking forstudents pursuing a bs or ms in computer science, computer engineering or a similar majorstrong software development skills in any object oriented language (we use c++ and c# the most)visa sponsorship is available for this position
