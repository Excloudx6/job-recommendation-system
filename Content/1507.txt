about usbeatport is responsible for the digital accessibility of electronic music and its associated culture. we have built the largest electronic music store in the world; standing atop colossal catalogue of inventory, containing millions of tracks catering to the tastes of over a million weekly users from around the globe.we are looking to hire a driven and talented database engineer to join our team and help us transform the platform into the digital record store of tomorrow. an ideal candidate will have a strong background in database systems, software development, creative problem solving and working with transactional data.this role is located in denver, colorado and requires eligibility to work in the unites states without need for visa sponsorship.  applicants who are outside the denver metro area and ready to relocate are welcome to apply, but local candidates are preferred.you will

write efficient db queries and lookups
design effective data models
utilize your python skills to analyze and solve problems
develop highly available and scalable web applications using django
learn complex systems through self discovery
utilize modern day distributed cloud db technologies i.e. google spanner
work with docker and kubernetes

you are

proficient with sql and nosql
have a solid understanding of db table indexes
have significant data modelling experience
experienced with acid compliant database design
a proven python developer with experience in a production level environment
a purpose driven, problem solver with tremendous thirst for learning
comfortable in a unix/linux environment
passionate about electronic music and culture

if this sounds like you, we would love to chat.benefitswe offer a competitive compensation package, plenty of vacation days, vip access to select music events, and monthly beatport.com credits to keep your library fresh.we hire people who love what they do and we make sure they have plenty of room for growth. you will work with a team that values collaboration and mentorship. we are always helping one another and sharing empirical knowledge about our stack.how to applytell us about your involvement in edm culture and why we should meet you.please include your resume, links to code you are proud of and anything else you feel is relevant.
