drone application developer intern
job descriptionintel designs and builds sophisticated drones and drone applications. we are constantly developing new technologies to improve the way drones navigate, communicate, and analyze their environment. as a drone application developer in the intel unmanned aerial systems (uas) team, you will work in a small interdisciplinary team to develop, integrate, and test uav traffic management capabilities in line with standards established in the u.s. by nasa the faa.you must possess minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates. experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your school work/classes/research and/or relevant previous job and/or internship experiences. this is an entry level position and will be compensated accordingly.qualificationsminimum requirementsthe candidate must be pursuing a bs degree in computer science, electrical engineering,  or related field.the candidate must be able meet the requirements to apply for faa part 107 certification (https://www.faa.gov/uas/getting_started/fly_for_work_business/becoming_a_pilot/)minimum 1 year experience in:java script programmingpythonlinux scriptingpreferred qualifications:familiarity with ubunto, yocto and/or dockerdrone piloting skills manual flight: e.g. flying multi-copters.ability to work effectively with abstract requirements.robotics, avionics, aeronautical/astronautical engineering.control systems.  responsibilities may be quite diverse of a technical nature. candidate experience and education requirements will vary significantly depending on the unique needs of the job. inside this business groupintel labs, the new business initiatives group (nbi), the perceptual computing group and the new devices group (ndg). additionally, new devices group and the new business initiatives group were combined into the new business group (nbg) which nests into ntg.other locationscalifornia, santa clara; posting statement. intel prohibits discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, national origin, age, disability, veteran status, marital status, pregnancy, gender expression or identity, sexual orientation or any other legally protected status.
