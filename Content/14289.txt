software engineer
description
at pearson, we’re committed to a world that’s always learning and to our talented team who makes it all possible. from bringing lectures vividly to life to turning textbooks into laptop lessons, we are always re-examining the way people learn best, whether it’s one child in our own backyard or an education community across the globe.we are bold thinkers and standout innovators who motivate each other to explore new frontiers in an environment that supports and inspires us to always be better. by pushing the boundaries of technology — and each other to surpass these boundaries — we create seeds of learning that become the catalyst for the world’s innovations, personal and global, large and small.
qualifications
description
pearson api management team is currently seeking a  software engineer who will be responsible for the core software engineering tasks in a project team. the selected individual will be required to work with dynamic teams that are driven by project delivery goals also he/she should possess the drive to learn and continuously improve on work performances and assist software engineers in writing quality code.  the api management team is a highly visible and fast paced team building critical integrations across pearson’s services and applications.
essential duties and responsibilities

play a key role in the software development process by participating in the conceptualization, design, planning, specification, and development
continue to acquire specialized functional knowledge, more complex skills and operating practices
remain flexible and responsive to changing priorities and multiple, concurrent responsibilities
responsible for analysis of current programs including performance issues and incorrect application behavior
make optimum utilization of engineering tools and technologies to maintain a high productivity level in the team

qualifications

master’s or bachelor’s degree in computer science, with strong fundamentals and problem solving skills, or equivalent work experience
4 years’ experience in software development
experience in distributed architecture; knowledgeable/experience with enterprise architecture, database design
experience in service integration and api design (rest, esb, streaming pub/sub)
experience in service security (jwt, oauth2)
experience with software lifecycle (jenkins, git, containerization)
experience working in agile development environment

additional plus

experience with apigee, nginx
experience with nodejs
experience with api gateways
experience with drupal
experience in global solutions in public and private cloud (aws)
