design automation engineer layout backend
job descriptiondevelops and tests engineering design automation tools, creates flows/scripts to analyze and test design methodologies. specifies materials, equipment and supplies required for completion of projects. evaluates vendor practical capabilities to provide required products or services. responsible for designing, deploying and testing efficiency of tools to utilize in achieving design goals and collaborating with design teams on methodology development. design automation engineers are advocates of applying design methodologies to help execute projects effectively and successfully with high quality.qualificationseducation:  candidate should possess a bachelor of science degree with at least 5 years additional experience or a master of science degree with at least 3 years additional experience, in computer science or computer engineering. minimum requirements:3+ years of experience in ic design or computer aided design cad2+ years of experience in setup/use/support of data management tools integrated with virtuoso platform2+ years of experience in scripting skills, especially tcl and perl python a plus2+ years of experience in cadence virtuoso layout xl/space-based router, cadence schematic editor, skill programming development and p-cell automation additional requirements:basic understanding of cmos vlsi, device models, device formation, semiconductor layersstrong problem solving skills, ability to multi-task and prioritize tasksproficient in virtuoso platform - schematic editor, layout editor, ade environment, tech file, streaminability to work in a dynamic environment and highly self-motivated.must have good interpersonal skills and customer support.self-motivated technical leader.inside this business groupnon-volatile solutions memory group:  the non-volatile memory solutions group is a worldwide organization that delivers nand flash memory products for use in solid state drives (ssds), portable memory storage devices, digital camera memory cards, and other devices.  the group is responsible for nvm technology design and development, complete solid state drive (ssd) system hardware and firmware development, as well as wafer and ssd manufacturing.other locationscalifornia, santa clara; posting statement. intel prohibits discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, national origin, age, disability, veteran status, marital status, pregnancy, gender expression or identity, sexual orientation or any other legally protected status.
