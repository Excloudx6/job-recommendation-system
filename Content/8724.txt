firstkey homes is an active investor and operator of single-family rental homes across the united states. we’ve already invested over $1b in approximately 8,000 homes and we’re continuing to acquire more properties every month. at firstkey, we aim to be best-in-class within our industry by providing high quality homes coupled with an unmatched customer experience for our residents. this role will provide the right candidate with an opportunity to grow with a dynamic company as we continue to increase our market size.summary of responsibilities:the diligence data analyst is primarily responsible for reviewing and validating data related to real estate acquisitions. this data tracking will encompass pre-acquisition due diligence, funding readiness, final certification of accuracy and transition of data to support post acquisition operations. this position will be a key partner to the acquisition integration team, accounting team and business leaders throughout the organization.essential dutiesfollow acquisitions through all steps in the due diligence process while maintaining accurate data in data warehouse.prepare and maintain detailed data tapes for multi-property and single property acquisitions to include financial data, property characteristics and tenant data. distribute data in a timely manner as required.reconcile incoming updates to existing data tape and consolidate.identifying and resolving data anomalies, errors and omissions.resolve data exceptions in timely manner and track the progress of exceptions through resolution.coordinate with funding coordinators to ensure that data tape documents are delivered timely and in order of priority.consolidate financial data, property characteristics and tenant data into data tapes for single property acquisitions.manage upload and integration of data into data warehouse.review and prepare lease abstracts.consolidate operational data (including documentation and financial data) into end-user format.this brief summary is not an all-inclusive description of job duties.other job duties and responsibilities may also be assigned by the incumbent’s manager at any
