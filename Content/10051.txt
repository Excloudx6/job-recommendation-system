about datacamp
datacamp is building the future of data science education. our students get real hands-on experience by completing self-paced, interactive data science courses from the best instructors in the world, right in the browser. in fact, over 1.8 million students around the world have completed nearly 80 million datacamp exercises to date!
the growth team at datacamp is a small agile team with the sole mandate of driving full funnel company growth. as a data scientist on the growth team, you will be part of a multidisciplinary team, consisting of a growth marketer, a designer, and a full stack engineer. your focus will be evaluating results from growth experiments and conducting research for new areas of possible improvements. 
responsibilities:

using data to provide actionable insights for potential high impact projects
communicating results of growth projects produced from a high tempo growth team
building dashboards to track team goals
further developing and integrating our analytics and marketing automation
ensure new projects are tracked properly throughout their lifecycle

our ideal candidate:

has 2 years experience as a data scientist or data analyst
is highly skilled with r and/or python + sql
has experience working with various marketing apis (facebook, adwords, etc.)
is a creative problem solver
is an extremely effective communicator
is detail oriented

what’s in it for you:

a competitive salary and stock options at an early stage start-up
flexible hours
100% of medical & dental insurance premiums paid
international company retreats every 6 months
work with a great team (everyone says this, but we’re serious—we’re pretty great)
