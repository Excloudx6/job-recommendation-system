                  description
                

career evolutions is seeking out a jr. database developer for an exciting and growing nationally recognized healthcare company in phoenix, az. this is an excellent career opportunity with a compensation up to $80,000.
as a key member of the technology team, you will aid the team in all database development related tasks. you will use your strong sql server understanding to best allow for the development and maintenance of company databases. you will be responsible for delivering reports utilizing ssrs.  
if you’re an up and coming database developer/bi professional who is ready to implement your skills into an exciting workplace environment; if you want to find yourself on an engaging and dynamic team in an stimulating and challenging field; and if you feel a need to take ownership of your what you do and see your work translate directly to organizational growth – then this is the position!
typical duties will include

you will be responsible for the development, testing, and maintain maintenance mysql
work with the application support team to spearhead all maintenance activities for the application suite. including overseeing fixes, troubleshooting, monitoring, and basic enhancements.
various etl programming, basic data modeling, design, development, and documentation based on industry best practices.

requirements

a bachelor's degree in computer science, information systems, or other related field (or equivalent work experience).
knowledge of mysql and analytical and reporting tools.
strong understanding of database design concepts (both oltp and olap).
ability to effectively collaborate with customers and analysts to translate business needs/requests into functional and/or technical requirements.
ability to analyze and optimize sql queries.
1-3 years of experience in a bi/database developer or analyst role.
1-3 years of experience of hands-on knowledge of sql.
ability to build strong relationships with internal team, demonstrate excellent customer service skills, including the ability to work with customers on the phone and web meetings.
experience in developing test plans and specification to ensure bug fixes and code updates resolve the issue identified and do not introduce other errors.
