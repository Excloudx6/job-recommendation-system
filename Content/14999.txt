the team 
our information services team is a highly collaborative group of individuals who are focused on supporting business operations through modernizing internal applications and establishing a supportive and scalable architecture. 
what you do 
harness your in-depth knowledge of software development to establish architecture that facilitates the freedom of data exchange between our enterprise applications in order to support pinnacol assurance’s strategic projects.  
strategic outcomes                                                                                                                                

successfully accomplish the creation of middle/service layer for easy maintenance and scalability
clear understanding of business initiatives in order to focus on continuous improvement within our applications, while maintaining architectural standards
established member of the team, who is actively enhancing company through a strong understanding of the market place and if/how new technologies can be incorporated to enhance our processes

the day to day

document and demonstrate solution by developing documentation, flowcharts, layouts, diagrams, charts, code comments and clear code
prepare and install solutions by determining and designing system specification, standards, and programming
identify system or program problems efficiently and accurately and propose pragmatic solutions. write software that satisfies business requirements and follows applicable coding standards
create design documents that satisfy business requirements, follow adopted methodologies, and lead to efficient, easy to maintain, and reliable systems
maintain code to enhance functionality, improve efficiency, and replace malfunctioning or obsolete code, following industry standards
test programs to ensure they meet business requirements and functionally work well, following testing standards
participate in deployment and configuration management processes as necessary
ensure all code is version controlled according to industry standards and best practices


leverage internal and external resources for learning and focus on what is pertinent to organization’s needs
remain current on knowledge and skills necessary for software development, including programming tools, development techniques and related technologies.
perform additional duties as assigned              

what we are looking for

you have expert knowledge of the software development life cycle with 3+ years of software development experience, programming in ruby or java.
you have experience in an agile environment
you have proven experience in systems integrations, writing api’s, restful web services, and with continuous integration & continuous deployment
you are understand the larger picture in reference to design and development
you’re collaborative and have the ability to work across teams
you are passionate about learning
