e1073:software engineer sr


job description : 
advanced programs rf (aprf) is seeking a software engineer on the coralring team to develop simulation and ground software for a domain specific cognitive system.  engineer will be member of agile software development team focused on all aspects of the software development required to design, implement and test a high fidelity simulation system, as well as operational ground software.  the ideal candidate will have experience working on agile developments teams, have a broad technical skillset, experience interacting with end-users, and demonstrated ability to deliver high quality software products.while considering the system as a whole, this role focuses on machine learning and data science technologies and tools potentially including big data and high throughput near real-time processing.  preferred candidate will have experience training machine learning algorithms, and configuring and tuning algorithms to minimize error rate and improve overall performance.in this role, the engineer will participate in proposals for future designs.  this is a high-visibility, fast-paced environment with opportunities to work a variety of mission spaces and payloads.basic qualificationsexperience with javascript, node.js, python, java or other programming languagebachelors in electrical engineering, physics, mathematics, or computer science or related fieldactive ts/sci clearance is requireddesired skillsfamiliarity with machine learning concepts and toolsexperience developing in an agile development environmentexperience with configuration management systems significant modeling and simulation experienceexperience developing for amazon web services (aws)experience with technical presentations, proposal generation, and ability to write technical papersas a leading technology innovation company, lockheed martin’s vast team works with partners around the world to bring proven performance to our customers’ toughest challenges. lockheed martin has employees based in many states throughout the u.s., and internationally, with business locations in many nations and territories.join us at lockheed martin, where we’re engineering a better tomorrow.lockheed
