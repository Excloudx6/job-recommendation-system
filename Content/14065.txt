job summary
the defense threat reduction agency (dtra) assists the combatant commanders, services and government agencies in protecting themselves against, and executing us policy regarding, chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear, and high explosives (cbrne) threats. this assistance is provided through advanced computational analysis and development of counterforce and defensive planning capabilities through analytical models and software tools. these analytical capabilities include integrated munitions effects assessment (imea) and its characterization tool, underground targeting and analysis system (utas), as well as vulnerability assessment and protection option (vapo).
