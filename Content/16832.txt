about tang tech team
the tang tech team is an efficient diverse group leading the development of multiple successful projects.  we collaborate with highly experienced investment management professionals who work with a wide range of companies to develop and grow their value and industry positions.  our goal is not to create a one-hit wonder app, but rather to shape a company with the sharpest individuals in the tech industry who can contribute to multiple successful projects. the tang tech team is constantly solving problems and working together to create innovative software solutions. with established funding from tang capital management, we can expand all of our resources and focus on our projects instead of raising capital. please see the corporate overview below for more information on tang capital management. 
position summary
as a software engineer and the 6th member of the team, you will own the development of your projects from developing mvp features for a product launch to scaling them for millions of users. the perfect candidate will demonstrate a deep expertise in javascript and its popular tools such as: react, node and angular. this individual should also have a wide range of abilities including front-end, cloud platform services, cloud back-end services, communication, cleverness and the belief that they can build anything.
essential duties and responsibilities

full-stack web/mobile application development
prototype new and redesign features
focus on ui design principles and making apps work intuitively
contribute best-in-class programming skills to develop highly innovative-facing mobile and web products
build and maintain software for multiple projects
work with user data to help build and analyze dashboards and reports

qualifications

minimum 4+ years of mobile technology experience required
minimum 4+ years of html/css3/javascript experience required
minimum 2+ years of professional work experience required
must have a deep understanding of the javascript language
must be proficient with at least 2 of the following javascript tools:


node.js
angular.js
react.js


must have a passion for learning and be self-motivated
must have excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to function within a collaborative team environment

corporate overview

tang capital management, llc (tang capital), is a life sciences-focused investment firm with a strong track record of creating and building successful biopharmaceutical companies. the principal of tang capital founded ardea biosciences, inc. in 2006 and served as its director until its acquisition by astrazeneca plc in june 2012. the principal of tang capital currently serves as chairman of heron therapeutics, inc. (nasdaq: hrtx), la jolla pharmaceutical company (nasdaq: ljpc), and as the chairman and chief executive officer of odonate therapeutics, llc. tang capital manages an investment fund that is the largest shareholder of all three of these companies. tang capital currently has offices in san diego and new york.
