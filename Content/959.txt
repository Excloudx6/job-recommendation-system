logistics done differently.
as the software engineer, you will apply technical, mathematical, and scientific techniques and knowledge to evaluate software from other sources. design and implement software components that address specific xpo logistics business requirements, resulting in systems that achieve quantifiable measures of performance, quality, affordability, maintainability, and simplicity. develop new, complex software components, and modifications to and support of existing production software. provide technical support for complex software operating environments deployed by the business.
pay, benefits and more.
we are eager to attract the best, so we offer competitive compensation and a generous benefits package, including full health insurance (medical, dental and vision), 401(k), life insurance, disability and more.
what you’ll do on a typical day:

design new software by applying techniques of computer science, engineering, and mathematical analysis that satisfies the objectives stated in business requirements
evaluate and select process and logic patterns, programming language constructs, data storage methods, and operating environment interfaces that effectively satisfy business requirements and quantifiable measures
develop appropriate programs and systems documentation according to established department standards
collaborate with architects, systems engineers, project managers, systems administrators, and operations analysts to deploy and implement solutions, and troubleshoot technical issues
deploy and implement fully-tested software solutions
follow industry best practices, techniques and xpo logistics standardized processes that deliver improved software solutions
evaluate identified technologies for inclusion in developed software solutions
certify the proper functioning and performance of software components using repeatable, reproducible, and reportable software testing methodologies
support software infrastructure by identifying and diagnosing software failures
install and upgrade software development tools
provide 24/7 on-call rotational support of software 

what you need to succeed at xpo:
at a minimum, you’ll need:

bachelor’s degree in software engineering or related technical field, or certification in software engineering from acm or ieee generally required. an equivalent combination of education, training and experience may be considered
leadership and project management training are beneficial
4+ years of experience in software solutions development preferably in medium to large corporate environments or commercial software companies
knowledge of rapid programming, agile development, software patterning, and refactoring techniques
knowledge of operating environment technologies (network, server, operating systems, database systems)
knowledge of open source projects
knowledge of the strengths and weaknesses of common programming languages (c, c++, c#, java, visual basic)
experience with data marshalling, data transformation, and data representation technologies (single-byte characters, multi-byte characters, xml, stylesheets)
experience with process communication techniques (shared memory, message queues, semaphores, mutexes)
knowledge of concurrent processing models, constructs, and techniques (threaded processes, thread synchronization, apartments, error recovery)
knowledge of sql and relational databases (db2/udb, oracle, mysql)
knowledge of application-specific technologies (image/graphics processing, character and symbology recognition, data acquisition, and others specific to business application)
knowledge of unix, java, and windows architecture and programming interfaces
experience with the industry standard ides – eclipse, visual studio, for unix, java, and windows

it’d be great if you also have:

strong written and verbal communication skills in technical settings. well-developed active listening and presentation skills
solid knowledge of it software development life cycle methodologies and processes
basic project management skills. well-developed time management and prioritization skills
demonstrated analytical capabilities
ability to apply understanding of functionality of event-driven business models

be part of something big.
xpo provides cutting-edge supply chain solutions to the world's most successful companies, including disney, pepsi, l'oréal, toyota and many others. we’re the fastest-growing transportation company on the fortune 500 list and we’re just getting started.
