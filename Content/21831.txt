summer 2018 intern, software development - undergraduate
canonsburg, pa
 
ansys is the global leader in engineering simulation software to bring ideas to life. if you’ve ever seen a rocket launch, flown on an airplane, driven a car, used a computer, touched a mobile device, crossed a bridge, or put on wearable technology, chances are you’ve used a product where ansys software played a critical role in its creation. we inspire the world’s most innovative companies deliver radically better products to their customers and solve the most complex design challenges limited by imagination.
ansys believes in developing our future engineers and technologists. we offer an enriching internship program for undergraduate students currently enrolled full-time in a four-year college or university.   
our interns work on a variety of impactful projects. they develop and test our industry-leading simulation software while gaining real experience in their field of study. interns may support any of our business units, including but not limited to the following:
• design business unit
• platform development unit
• licensing & install unit
• mechanical business unit
• electronics business unit
• release management unit
• fluids business unit
• semiconductor business unit 
this internship is a 40-hour per week paid position starting may 2018 and concluding august 2018.
responsibilities 
• work with software developers to design, implement, and debug
• engineering simulation features
• ui toolkit components
• infrastructure process and tools
• online help systems
requirements
• pursuing a bs in computer science or computer engineering with a gpa of 3.0 or higher
• must be currently enrolled in a full-time degree program and returning to the program after the completion of the internship
• exposure to c++, c#, java or python
• sharp and tenacious problem solving skills
• team player, responsible, and motivated
desired skills
• exposure to object oriented programming
• strong math skills
• web development experience
