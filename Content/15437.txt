country us
do you have what it takes to push the boundaries of game-changing technologies, deliver results while working with a diverse team of talented individuals, and challenge yourself knowing that what we do matters to the warfighter?  are you mission ready?  this job is for you!we at isr & space systems are world leaders in high altitude electro-optical sensors, employed by the u.s. and foreign military in time critical intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance operations. our passion for supporting the warfighters and their mission demands excellence in everything we do, from the talent we recruit to the highly-engineered systems we deliver. we value people, integrity, ideas and performance. this position offers an exciting opportunity to grow technically and broaden capabilities while proudly contributing to unique state of the art solutions that truly make a difference by supporting our effort to become a world class sar sensor provider.qualification: required skillsjava experiencelinux or unix experiencepreferred skillsc++ experiencedatabase design and development experiencexml experiencemessaging systems like activemqability to debug and troubleshootco-op, internship or projects experience a pluscompetenciesprogramming with multithreading including synchronization and deadlocksabstract classes and interfacegui development (swing library)working with data structures; such as hash maps, linked listsexception handlingunderstand how java handles object referencesgood verbal and written communication skillsability to work well in a team environmentability to drive tasks to closurecandidate must be a u.s. citizen with the ability to secure a dod security clearanceeducation: bs degree or currently pursuing in software engineering or related field required, w 0-2 years’ experienceunited technologies corporation is an equal opportunity/affirmative action employer.  all qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability or veteran status, age or any other federally protected class.
