data privacy engineer
apply
job id 3017978
location
    vancouver, washington
date posted 10/26/2017
the cloud solutions and operations team (cso) is looking for a talented and self-starter engineer to join our team as a data privacy engineer. this role will be part of a cross functional team to ensure compliance to data privacy rules and guidelines. daily responsibilities include assurance of clear documentation of privacy rules as well as proper execution and management. this technically focused role will support multiple products and platforms where customer generated data is stored and used to support and enhance services across hp’s portfolio of products.job responsibilities:identify how, why and where data is generated and stored and who has access to the data across a multitude of platforms and cloud accounts. verify that all data is stored in compliance with the hp informational governance board (igb) guidelines. create and maintain audit trails for data access and usage. engage with hp r&d and analytics teams to provide system and application training and privacy reviews to ensure that data collection, processing and storage complies with the igb guidelines on a continual basis and document the findings. stay current with global privacy regulations. implement privacy by design principles inside of the cso organization and its hp partners.specify and utilize tools and processes to help automate the following:audit trail creationmonitoring and alerting of personal data usagereportingdata removaltrain internal hp development teams, cyber security, and management on the proper use of personal data including collection, processing and retention.the following skills are mandatory:excellent written and oral communication skills with emphasis on engagement, alignment and follow-through. communication skills must include ability to successfully target presentations at executives or at an individual contributor leveldemonstrated ability to track data flows through cloud/datacenter based (aws, azure, etc) applications that generate telemetryexperience with structured and unstructured storage especially personal data across multiple platformsexperience in privacy engineering, global/regional privacy related certifications are a plus (such as cipp or cissp)demonstrated ability to understand legal requirements and translate to engineering/analytics processesbachelor's or master's degree in related field.these secondary skills are highly desired:history of generating and providing analytics including ways of pseudonymizing and/or equivalence due to aggregationexperience in security engineering, especially application and infrastructure securitysystem administration and/or dba experience
