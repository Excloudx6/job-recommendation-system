this highly technical role is responsible for ensuring complete and accurate web development for all marketing efforts. you'll be working in conjunction with the rest of the team to bring websites and projects to life. we want someone who will stay on top of the changes in the digital industry to optimize the company’s online footprint and help keep gorilla at the forefront of web marketing. 
tasks and responsibilities

developing, maintaining, troubleshooting, and enhancing all current and future web pages
adhering to project schedules and effectively reporting progress to meet challenging deadlines
investigating possible issues, including technical problems with websites, analytics and tracking pixels
collaborating with various team members to proactively optimize and expand sites
staying up to date with the latest standards and trends in the inbound marketing space 

requirements

proficient in php, mysql, and jquery
knowledge using ssh and the command line for server management
ability to take designs and turn them into templates through html and css
knowledge of responsive, mobile-friendly design techniques
strong problem-solving and troubleshooting abilities
availability to work from our st. louis, mo office
