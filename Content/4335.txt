job description:
the software development team at advanced geoint systems (ags) - systems integration group (sig) develops, implements, and integrates state-of-the art sensor-data processing and analytics technologies, providing compact and scalable solutions to challenging problems in areas of video analytics, sensor data processing, computer vision, and machine learning.  as a member of the software group, you will lead a highly-motivated team responsible for software development of cutting-edge desktop and web-based thin client applications for video exploitation.  you and your team will support the integration of these technologies into core bae systems products and customer platforms.  responsibilities of this position include: work with the ags product management team to develop product roadmapsarchitect next-generation video exploitation products consistent with product roadmapscollaborate with multi-site ags engineering teams to coordinate development of productsoversee implementation of next-generation video productslead initiatives for innovation in products, tools, and processes

minimum education and experience:
bachelor's degree and 8 years work experience or 12 years of experience in lieu of degree

required skills and education:
we seek a software architect with a background in web-based video distribution technologies.  other job requirements include: leadership of small teams to accomplish complex technical tasksstrong programming skills in one or more of the following languages: c++, java, javascript.thorough understanding of object-oriented programming conceptsthorough understanding of restful web services architecture and implementationthorough understanding of modern web development technologies and methodologiesextensive experience with video technologies, including:standard video containers and codecs such as mpeg-2 transport stream, mpeg-4, h.264, h.265web-based video distribution technologies and standards such as mpeg-dash, hlsvideo processing frameworks such as directshow, ffmpeg, gstreamerexperience debugging, profiling, and optimizing codeexperience integrating and troubleshooting complex hardware and software systemsexperience with software development processes, including version control, documentation, code review, and testingus citizenship and ability to obtain dod clearance

preferred skills and education:
developers with experience working with full motion video (fmv) in a department of defense or intelligence community context are preferred.  while not critical to the open position, experience with following skills are useful in our organization and will be helpful as you expand your roles and responsibilities within bae systems: experience with multi-threading and/or distributed computingcreating and maintaining cross-platform software (windows/linux)experience developing or interfacing with graphical user interfacesexperience with cloud computing and issues of database scalability

about bae systems electronic systems:
bae systems is a premier global defense and security company with approximately 90,000 employees delivering a full range of products and services for air, land and naval forces, as well as advanced electronics, security, information technology solutions and customer support and services.the electronic systems (es) sector spans the commercial and defense electronics markets with a broad portfolio of mission-critical electronic systems, including flight and engine controls; electronic warfare and night vision systems; surveillance and reconnaissance sensors; secure networked communications equipment; geospatial imagery intelligence products and systems; mission management; and power-and energy-management systems. headquartered in nashua, new hampshire, es employs approximately 13,000 people globally, with engineering and manufacturing functions primarily in the united states, united kingdom, and israel. equal opportunity employer/females/minorities/veterans/disabled/sexual orientation/gender identity/gender expression
