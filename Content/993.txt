data center uefi bios engineering intern
job descriptionjoin the exciting world of data center group dcg bios/uefi firmware engineering team and set the pace of the next fastest super-computers on earth! you will be a part of the uefi bios team that develops bios code to run in intel's latest generation sever platforms.as an intern candidate, you will be mentored by a sr. engineer in the importance and best practices of development. you will participate in the coding of new test content, updating existing test content, develop technical collaterals, develop new code and resolve technical issues in the existing code. you may need to work with engineers in the lab to analyze, root cause and debug technical problems. you may be expected to assist with developing tools and scripts for build and automation processes.the selected candidate should have the following behavior skills:good problem analysis and problem solving skillsability to own and drive resolution of technical issues requirementsqualificationsyou must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates. experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your school work/classes/research and/or relevant previous job and/or internship experiences.minimum requirements: graduate working towards a master's degree in computer science, computer engineering or related field.6+ months experience/skills in following:programming experience in python scriptingprogramming experience in c/c++ programming languageobject oriented programmingbasic understanding of computer architectureexperience working in a linux and windows environmentinside this business groupthe data
