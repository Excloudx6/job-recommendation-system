novel or complex assignments. they visualize information using an advanced knowledge of a wide range of tools (e.g., gis, r, or python), develop sophisticated scripts and algorithms, create advanced explanatory and predictive models, and lead comparative analyses to address complex problems.* apply complex statistical and mathematical analysis to identify trends, conflicts, opportunities, solve complex analytic problems, optimize data interoperability* lead collaborative efforts to develop and implement new and innovative collection and analytic methods to answer emerging intelligence intelligence challenges.band 05: data scientists oversee and direct the development and application of advanced or innovative methods to identify, collect, process, and analyze large and complex mathematical analyses to identify trends, solve analytical problems, optimize performance, and gather intelligence. they develop new ways to visualize information using extensive knowledge of a wide range of tools (e.g., gis, r, or python), develop and disseminate sophisticated scripts and algorithms, create highly advanced explanatory and predictive models, and oversee comparative analyses to
