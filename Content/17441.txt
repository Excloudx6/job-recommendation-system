web producer ii

position summarythe web producer is responsible for maintaining and enhancing f5’s public facing web sites across multiple languages, platforms, and operating systems. as a web producer, the person in this role supports and delivers best-in-class web experiences to f5 customers, partners, and prospects.the web producer will work with the marketing, business, creative, and development teams to support their online content production needs.the web producer should be customer-obsessed, detail-oriented, and comfortable working with highly technical teams and with marketing teams, as well as be able to handle multiple projects in a fast-paced environment.primary responsibilities manage, prioritize, and execute maintenance production requests.work with web team members in all functions (designers, copywriters, content strategies, seo lead).supports content deployment processes and acts as the center for production-related communications and issues tracking.support stakeholders by responding to questions and issues per an established sla and driving to rapid resolution.produce landing pages and microsites and manages content across multiple properties, including localized sites, via the dnn and adobe experience manager.proactively communicate project status to business owners and key stakeholders as needed, and manage deliverable/launch expectations with all business owners.be a brand steward and last line of defense to ensure compliancy and consistency with all campaigns and initiatives across the web properties.handle project/work activities with broad scope and functional impact.responsible for upholding f5’s business code of ethics and for promptly reporting violations of the code or other company policies.performs other related duties as assigned.the job description is intended to be a general representation of the responsibilities and requirements of the job.  however, the description may not be all-inclusive, and responsibilities and requirements are subject to change.knowledge, skills and abilitiessolid understanding and experience in htmljavascript, xml, css experience is a plusworking experience with cms systems: dnn and adobe experience managerability to juggle multiple priorities and make things happen in a fast-paced, dynamic environmentexperience with agile project managementexperience delivering e-commerce sitesstrong microsoft office skillsbasic front-end developer skills a plusweb product manager experience a plusqualifications3+ years digital production; experience should span simple page updates, single page landing experiences, to producing complex websites.3+ years general marketing experience1+ years project management experiencephysical demands and work environmentduties are performed in a normal office environment while sitting at a desk or computer table.  duties require the ability to utilize a computer, communicate over the telephone, and read printed material.duties may require being on call periodically and working outside normal working hours (evenings and weekends).f5 networks, inc. is an equal opportunity employer and strongly supports diversity in the workplace.
