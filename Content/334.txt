job description

requisition id: 00001by9 
janssen research
& development, llc., a johnson and johnson company is hiring a senior associate systems engineer, data systems to
be based out of spring house pa, malvern pa,
cork ireland, or beerse belgium. at the janssen
pharmaceutical companies of johnson & johnson, what matters most is helping
people live full and healthy lives. we focus on treating, curing and preventing
some of the most devastating and complex diseases of our time. and we pursue the
most promising science, wherever it might be found.janssen research
& development, llc discovers and develops innovative medical solutions to
address important unmet medical needs in oncology, immunology, neuroscience,
infectious diseases and vaccines, and cardiovascular and metabolic diseases.
please visit http://www.janssenrnd.com/
for more information.  we are janssen. our
mission drives us. our patients inspire us. we collaborate with the world for
the health of everyone in it. thriving on a diverse company culture,
celebrating the uniqueness of our employees and committed to inclusion. proud
to be an equal opportunity employer. position summary:systems engineer
with responsibility for the implementation and ownership of business and
technology laboratory data systems within the pharmaceutical drug development
and manufacturing sciences (pdms) for the janssen r&d operations
organization. major duties & responsibilities:responsible for the
     implementation of business and data system technology strategies with a
     view to streamline and expand our development and execution abilities
     within the pdms organization.  a liaison (specific point of
     contact) with functional pdms value stream departments getting in-depth
     knowledge of business processes and in partnership with the business
     identifying new opportunities for organizational improvements.manage knowledge management
     strategies aligned with process development/process engineering design
     methodologies, through collaborative efforts with supply chain, quality,
     quantitative sciences and other key partners throughout the enterprise.  key elements of the role include
     teamwork and partnering with international stakeholders, in both pdms, rdo
     and commercial organizations. ensure overall compliance
     with regulatory requirements and gxp, as related to knowledge management.business owner
     responsibilities for strategic laboratory and manufacturing data
     management systems within pdms. serve as a liaison between it and the
     system users in the pdms organization supporting system enhancements,
     changes, upgrades, troubleshooting, and other base activities.manage relationships with
     vendors for key laboratory, manufacturing, and data analytics systems.maintaining subject matter
     expertise in scientific and process engineering big data management
     through participation in industry meetings, workshops, and academic
     collaborations.qualificationsrequired:a minimum of a bachelor’s
     degree in a technical or scientific discipline such as engineering, life
     sciences, information technology, management information systems,
     manufacturing execution systems computer science and a minimum of four (4)
     years of related work experience is required.a minimum of two (2) years of
     expertise in pharmaceutical or related regulated industry is required.this position can be based
     out of spring house pa, malvern pa, cork ireland, or beerse belgium and requires up to 15% travel internationally and/or domestic. preferred:experience working within a
     gxp environment, implementing and maintaining high quality standards for
     all aspects of the work conducted including adherence to regulatory and
     gxp compliance, as they pertain to data management and software
     development is preferred.pharmaceutical r & d
     industry experience is preferred.basic knowledge of drug
     development process is preferred.basic knowledge of
     statistical analysis is preferred.  johnson &
johnson family of companies are equal opportunity employers, and all qualified
applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race,
color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, genetic information,
national origin, protected veteran status, disability status, or any other
characteristic protected by law. primary locationunited states-pennsylvania-spring houseother locationseurope/middle east/africa-belgium-antwerp-beerse, europe/middle east/africa-ireland-cork-cork, north america-united states-pennsylvania-malvernorganizationjanssen research & development, llc.
