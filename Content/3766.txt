machine learning infrastructure engineer


location:
new york


job code:
329


# of openings:
3



description
schrödinger, a technology leader specializing in software solutions for life science research and development, is seeking a data infrastructure engineer to help lead the development of deep learning based methods for drug discovery.  the ideal candidate is an experienced software developer who has worked with large datasets, unix environments, and distributed computing.  the candidate should have demonstrated excellent programing skills, and must have a strong interest in machine learning. 

required qualifications include:
-ability to prototype and scale pipelines for ingesting data and training models
-expert in unix/linux
-experience in parallel and distributed programming
-excellent communication skills, both written and verbal
-experience in software engineering and software architecture
-expertise in functional and objected oriented python programming
-ability to integrate complex software systems
-ability to implement new approaches from papers

pluses:
-b.s., m.s. or ph.d. in computer science, software engineering, or related field
-experience with data mining or etl technologies (eg. hadoop/spark/kafka)
-experience with machine learning and statistical methods
-general understanding of the physics underlying protein-ligand binding
-experience in statistical biophysics, including protein-ligand binding and molecular dynamics simulation analysis
-experience with tensorflow or deepchem
-experience with cuda
-peer-reviewed publications in computational chemistry or computer science
