be part of a journey with one big mission, make it enterprise automation simpler, fun and more robust with an end goal of making end users' lives easier.
 we’re looking for a full stack developer to join our growing team to help take our product to the next level!
 you will be integrating our user interface team to build amazing web user interfaces for our main product. we are looking for well-qualified candidates with a broad selection of full stack web development knowledge including mvc frameworks, server architecture, and ui development skills who care about pixels with a sense of ui design.
 the candidate should be an excellent communicator who can work collaboratively in our spread and cultural-mixed team.
  your role

work with the ui team on the re-think of the user interface for the web
design and develop beautiful and amazing responsive web user interface
implement scalable and highly efficient ways of presenting a large set of data

  your profile

solid knowledge in object oriented development and design patterns
in-depth knowledge with java and related web technologies :

html5, js, css, scss …
jee, jsp, servlet, jetty, maven …
gwt, vaadin is a plus


understands how to write tests (unit testing and mocking)
working knowledge of restful services, json, xml
sql expertise
agile development methodologies
working knowledge with ci server is appreciated (hudson/jenkins/teamcity)
love building amazing user interface, details and pixel perfect oriented
highly motivated and a true team player

 our offer

the opportunity to shape the future of our amazing user interface
a dynamic environment with a fast and agile development cycle
work environment with modern development tools and up-to-date hardware
international travel opportunities to work with distributed development teams
competitive salary and benefits package
 
 
sma solutions


founded in 1980, sma solutions is a privately held software development company located in kingwood, texas specializing in automation and analytics software solutions.  our entrepreneurial spirit has led sma solutions to become a global operation with nine offices worldwide, multiple business partners, and an international client base.  we are looking for employees who are passionate about customer service to support our continuing growth.   we pride ourselves on our culture offering a relaxed, family atmosphere to foster innovation and creativity.  the successful candidate will be someone who wants to develop professionally, have fun, and help our customers with integrating and modernizing their organizations.
