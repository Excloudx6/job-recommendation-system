company description
as market leader in enterprise application software, sap helps companies of all sizes and industries innovate through simplification. from the back office to the boardroom, warehouse to storefront, on premise to cloud, desktop to mobile device – sap empowers people and organizations to work together more efficiently and use business insight more effectively to stay ahead of the competition. sap applications and services enable customers to operate profitably, adapt continuously, and grow sustainably.
office location

contract duration

purpose & objective of position
this internship will provide exposure to delivering software in a professional environment. we use an agile software delivery methodology, which is becoming mainstream but is not normally taught in university. we are working with cutting-edge technologies on a cloud-based application, solving an interesting business problem involving event stream processing and machine learning.
 
expectations & tasks
the software developer intern will work alongside our engineering, participating in the full product delivery lifecycle from taking in requirements in the form of user stories, designing a software solution, implementing code, and delivering it from staging into production.

aptitude with core java libraries

prior computer science or stem coursework that involves coding as part of the curriculum
 
qualifications

cloud-based computing
distributed computing concerns like immutability and deadlocking
restful web services
event stream processing
data mining
data analytics


ability to quickly learn new libraries and frameworks
inquisitive nature
