business intelligence analyst (secondary markets)


location:
charlotte, nc

description
cardinal financial is redefining the standard.  we have a distinct advantage with our   game changing  infrastructure that enables fast, consistent, and high-quality experience for both the loan originator and the borrower.  we are seeking   forward thinking  people, who produce results rather than wait for them. you will be backed by one of the strongest, most supportive and technical mortgage banking leadership teams in the industry and we are all committed to the success of the enterprise.
what you will do

analyze business requirements from management and users to define the bi solution needed to produce the required output
apply your expertise in business analysis and the presentation of data to see beyond the numbers to develop a deep understanding of your area of responsibility and how it connects with the business as a whole
collect and translate data into actionable insights using a variety of systems and methods
clean data in preparation for reporting and analysis
research and analyze key business drivers, competitive environment, opportunities, trends and operating metrics based on research findings
prepare presentations and clearly communicate findings from initiatives to senior management and to the broader organization
provide support for ad-hoc data analysis
cross-train with other bi analysts to increase individual and team knowledge
explore and prepare a wide variety of new data sources for inclusion in a data warehouse

what you need

bachelor’s degree in finance, business, information systems , or equivalent experience
2-3 years data analysis or bi work experience, preferably for a financial services company bi tools experience required;  ssrs/ssis/ssas or tableau preferred
experience with database querying required; mysql or sql server syntax experience highly preferred
experience with vba (visual basic) highly preferred
ability to turn vague concepts and asks into well-documented and effective business metrics and insights
ability to initiate and drive projects to completion with minimal guidance
ability to communicate the results of analyses in a clear and effective manner
positive attitude with lots of energy
ability to work well with a team environment and take constructive criticism, coaching and feedback on a consistent basis
highly detail oriented and organized with the ability to multi-task and work in a very fast paced environment
ability to prioritize and manage large volume of tasks
ability to exercise discretion on sensitive or confidential matters
experience with amazon web services aurora or redshift

what we offer

strength, stability, and vision
an empowered culture where your ideas are important and your voice matters
opportunity for career growth
competitive compensation package
benefits that become effective the first day of the month following your start date including - medical, dental, vision, life and much more…
