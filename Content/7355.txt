analyst, delivery & analytics - 3680
analytics - usa chicago, illinois 
 
with shrinking marketing budgets and an increasingly fragmented media landscape, it's never been more important to understand the real return on marketing investment.  nielsen catalina solutions combines nielsen's rich media and purchase information with catalina marketing's expansive shopper data to provide the most comprehensive single-source measurement of advertising roi as well as tools to help consumer packaged goods marketers optimize media schedules on tv, online and mobile.
 
the delivery & analytics team serves as a trusted advisor and solutions provider to our clients.  as a member of delivery & analytics, you will start with a client problem or challenge and work backwards to create the right process and mix of solutions/product to tackle that opportunity and provide insights.
 
as an analyst, your objective is to drive client value by assisting in the analysis and preparation of client deliverables.  to do this, you will have a set of powerful analytical tools, thorough job training, a supportive peer network and experienced manager to help guide your work.
 
responsibilities:


contribute to client deliverables and deliver integrated insights and analyses 


conduct research and contribute to presentations 


provide insights to client inquiries and deliver high level of service 


attend client meetings with senior account team members 


participate in knowledge sharing across the organization 


utilize and participate in analytic communities to create and promote best practices 


mine and organize data based on client business issues 


organize data and identify trends and patterns 


utilize advanced statistical modeling to prepare analyses and make preliminary recommendations 


broaden knowledge of client business issues and needs, industry and nielsen catalina solutions services 


participation in cross functional teams/projects 


job-related and leadership training opportunities


 
qualifications: 


bachelor's degree in a quantitative field such as statistics, finance or economics 


strong academic record with minimum 3.0 cumulative gpa or equivalent 


1-3 years of analytical experience preferred 


strong working knowledge of sas.  sql knowledge a plus. 


strong analytic proficiency with the ability to analyze large and complex data sets. 


client service mentality 


proficiency in microsoft office suite 


ability to multi-task and good communication and organization skills 


permanent work authorization in the u.s.


 
about nielsen:
nielsen n.v. (nyse: nlsn) is a global performance management company that provides a comprehensive understanding of what consumers watch and buy. nielsen’s watch segment provides media and advertising clients with total audience measurement services across all devices where content — video, audio, and text — is consumed. the buy segment offers consumer packaged goods manufacturers and retailers the industry’s only global view of retail performance measurement.
by integrating information from its watch and buy segments and other data sources, nielsen provides its clients with both world-class measurement as well as analytics that help improve performance. nielsen, an s&p 500 company, has operations in over 100 countries that cover more than 90 percent of the world’s population. for more information, visit www.nielsen.com
nielsen is committed to hiring and retaining a diverse workforce. we are proud to be an equal opportunity/affirmative action-employer, making decisions without regard to race, color, religion, gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, national origin, genetics, disability status, age, marital status, protected veteran status or any other protected class.
