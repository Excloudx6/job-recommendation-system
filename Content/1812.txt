process, and analyze large amounts of data. you’ll be instrumental in establishing the scope, infrastructure requirement and design of our data products, and you’ll seek guidance from product owners and other stakeholders to ensure that your work product remains consistent with product needs. the initiatives you’ll contribute to will touch all parts of our data ecosystem (i.e. big data clusters, mpp appliances, etc.), so the ability to operate comfortably in a diverse technical environment is a must. strong collaboration and communication skills are also required in our environment. if you’re
