location(s): co - denver
 
are you looking for an exciting job where you can put your skills, talents and education to work at a company you can feel proud to be a part of? do you want a workplace that will challenge you and offer you opportunities to learn and grow?  a professional position at xcel energy could be just what you’re looking for.
 
 
is it time to see how all those college courses translate to the real world? are you ready to work alongside
experienced professionals on real projects and issues? an internship at xcel energy will give you practical experience
in the energy industry at a company that’s committed to excellence, safety and environmental stewardship.
 
the skills acquired in this internship provide an incredible insight into the energy industry and will prepare a student well beyond the classroom for the next step in a solid career within a well-established industry. 
this type of development and preparation does take time, so the student commitment expected for this position is at least 18 months - this requires that the intern maintains student status for the entirety of the internship period; therefore, preference is for a sophomore or first semester junior.
this is a paid internship position: 20 hours per week during academic periods, and up to 40 hours per week during non-academic periods including summer and school breaks. the academic school year weekly schedule can be flexible (such as 8am - 12 noon mwf and 1pm - 5pm tth).  based on the expected commitment, preference will be given to students at colleges and universities within a reasonable distance of downtown denver.  xcel energy will publish this posting at the following institutions: colorado school of mines, metro state university, regis university, university of colorado - boulder, university of colorado - denver, and university of denver. the targeted start date for this intern position will be mid to late august 2017.
 
please include a copy of college transcript(s) (unofficial transcripts are acceptable) with the submitted application/resume. please indicate expected graduation date.
 
position summary
 
compile and develop customer electric load data and sample statistics into meaningful reports for various internal and external clients. ensure that standard statistical reports are complete each month for all assigned electric load samples. assist quantitative analysts and senior analysts by designing spreadsheets, databases, procuring customer data, and producing necessary analytical reports. candidate will also learn electric industry specific software such as load analysis.
 
at xcel energy, our customers' quality of life depends on the energy we provide. every time they flip a switch or turn a dial, they tap into the energy of over 12,000 people working to make life better.  as a leading combination electricity and natural gas energy company, xcel energy offers a comprehensive portfolio of energy-related products and services to 3.4 million electricity and 1.9 million natural gas customers.
 
xcel energy has become a leader in the energy industry by demonstrating excellence in environmental performance. our environmental commitment includes improving air quality, conserving resources, harnessing renewable energy, and protecting wildlife and habitats.
 
xcel energy's vitality comes from our people. it takes professionals in engineering, customer service, linemen, marketing and sales professionals, energy traders, mechanics, finance and accounting professionals and many others to power our company.
 
minimum requirements
• current undergraduate student: sophomore status by september 1, 2017.
• pursuing a technical major such as economics, statistics, math, business, or computer science.
• must possess basic computer skills, preferably with microsoft office applications (e.g., word and excel).
• good organizational, communication, and problem solving skills.
• current gpa of 3.0 or higher
 
preferred
• candidate should have an interest in learning and using sas data management and statistical software; any prior experience is beneficial.
