about us
since 2011, rewardstyle has fueled the arrival of a new influence on the retail industry: professional content creators. by providing the innovative technologies, strategic consultancy and partnerships necessary to empower a global army of 13,000 premium content creators, rewardstyle is making a tangible impact on global e-commerce sales. in 2017, rewardstyle influencers are expected to drive approximately $1 billion in retail sales.
liketoknow.it, rewardstyle's consumer-facing, ready-to-shop content platform which makes beautiful and original influencer content actionable for millions of consumers worldwide, was launched in 2014. in march 2017, the game-changing liketoknow.it app was launched, featuring a proprietary technology that allows consumers to instantly shop their screenshots of influencer-created imagery anywhere they discover it across social media and the mobile web.
honored as one of the 50 most innovative companies in the world by fast company, rewardstyle has redirected the style publishing industry and contributed to the professionalization and financial independence of thousands of influencers worldwide, enabling them to earn meaningful revenue on their digital content, ultimately empowering them to create and grow small businesses into international brands.
today, more than 200 team members work from rewardstyle offices in dallas, london, new york, shanghai and são paulo.

title: data architect
location: dallas, texas                                   
reports to: chief architect
job purpose: 
rewardstyle is looking for a data architect who is a proven technology leader.  the successful candidate will be responsible for driving our data strategy across our saas, mobile, and business intelligence platforms.   
if your dna is dominated by technical depth, business acumen, and a desire to create delight in every customer experience; if you have a passion for driving big data decisions, creating data strategies, and influencing engineers; and if you are truly skilled in inspiring a cross-functional team along a growth journey, then this role and rewardstyle is for you!
key responsibilities and accountabilities:


lead the big data evolution of the rewardstyle platform, including mobile, saas, and business intelligence applications
drive end-to-end data architecture across rewardstyle product offerings and platform services
work with key stakeholders in multiple engineering and bi groups to drive near and long term data strategy
provide strategic and tactical direction in the areas of data management, analytics and visualization across our entire internal operations.
guide and inspire the teams in data science best practices, and evangelize strategic use of data throughout the business
assisting in translating functional and technical requirements into detailed data system architecture and design, including capacity planning
creating and enforcing database standards and best practices
designing and developing data models, etl, data governance policies and aggregation processes
lead the database technology decisions and manage database costs.
be a key contributor to ensure the product is highly secure, available, scalable, resilient, and cloud ready
bring in new innovative ideas around data practices
influence cross-organizational leaders that can drive success for the individual development teams
communicate effectively across multiple organizations to ensure that the teams are completely aligned to the objectives and can do the job effectively
build strong inter-personal relationships with peers and other key stakeholders that can help smooth execution

 technical / educational requirements:


bachelors degree in computer science, electrical engineering or related field
masters degree preferred
10+ years dba/data engineering experience with deep understanding of aws redshift, aws rds, and cassandra, as well as caching strategies.  knowledge of percona, redis, postgresql a plus
5+ years experience designing highly available, distributed, big data systems
5+ years experience with large-scale data analytics and data warehousing
demonstrated ability to drive data strategy that serve millions of people
understanding of industry-leading technology/solutions in big data and machine learning
a laser focus on outstanding business outcomes with effective and fast decision making that takes into account the interests of all four key stakeholders – employees, customers, shareholders, and partners
experience in making successful trade-offs that balance the short and long term product goals
experience using data insights for product refinement
ability to work well through ambiguity, confidence in making tough calls and leading through adversity with a sharp focus on the eventual right outcomes
strong knowledge of data structures and algorithms
deep understanding of distributed systems concepts and principles
strong understanding of system performance and scaling
experience debugging, diagnosing, and troubleshooting complex production software
