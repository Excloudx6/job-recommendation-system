software engineer, backend
 
bonsai is looking for a talented and passionate backend software engineer to join our backend services team to help build, run, and scale our ai platform. bonsai’s backend team owns the systems that tie our software together into a cloud platform: our http api, microservice and machine learning job orchestration services, cluster provisioning and monitoring, system capacity, and data storage systems. as a backend engineer at bonsai, you’d contribute to all of those pieces.
 
responsibilities
 

design, build, improve and maintain scalable, highly available microservices in python
find system performance bottlenecks and fix them
build and improve infrastructure automation
provision and manage bonsai brain cloud deployments
monitor, diagnose and fix production issues
improve engineering standards, tools and processes

 
skills and experience

at least 2 years of industry experience as a backend software engineer or similar
familiarity with docker or other containerization technologies
proficiency with a general purpose language like python or c++
experience working with linux and cloud platforms (aws, azure, etc)
a passion for high quality code

this position is based out of bonsai’s satellite office in seattle, washington.
