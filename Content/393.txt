employment opportunities
aegis’ greatest asset is people. the enthusiasm, skill, and dedication of our worldwide staff are ultimately what make us different. we are always looking for more members to join our team. we invite you to send us your information if you are interested in a career at aegis.

inside sales-account executive – high wycombe, uk
aegis software is looking for an ambitious and hard-working inside sales-account executive to join our international sales team throughout the uk and ireland. candidates from a range of backgrounds with demonstrable sales talent who wish to further develop their skills in an international environment are invited to apply.  in joining our team as an inside sales account executive, you will play a fundamental role in implementing multi-channel campaigns across the uk and ireland based out of our offices in high wycombe, uk. with the overall aim of creating demand, qualifying prospects and supporting clients in closing sales, you will also: 
·       research accounts, identify key contacts and generate interest
·       conduct telephone-based prospecting and customer contact
·       source and qualify new sales opportunities through lead follow-up activity
·       nurture and develop leads across multiple campaigns
·       maintain and expand sales funnel and ‘opportunity’ pipelines
·       achieve quarterly quotas and assigned objectives
·       develop ‘sales ready’ new business prospects
·       assist regional sales manager with quoting opportunities
requirements:
·       a record of over-achievement of quotas and/or objectives
·       excellent communications skills (written and oral)
·       an instinctively commercial approach with a positive attitude
·       you possess good it skills
·       highly organised, with a strong work-ethic
·       educated to degree level or equivalent
·       a second european language would be advantageous
benefits:
·       competitive annual base salary + commissions
·       a truly global team of outstanding professionals
·       unique software and a line of products that’s been recognized by key industry analysts
submit your resume here
business development manager – europe
aegis software is recruiting for an experienced business development manager with a strong marketing and commercial focus to support the development and growth of our value added reseller (var) and consultant markets.  reporting to the european regional director, this role will be responsible for the market analysis, development and growth of additional vars and consultants throughout europe, and overall management of our var/consultant internal team to create new revenue streams for the business unit. this role can be based at either of our sites in high wycombe, uk or erlangen, germany for the continued expansion of the company’s manufacturing execution software (mes). 
requirements:
·       educated to a degree standard in a technical and / or commercial discipline with a good level of experience in a business development, sales and/or marketing function
·       sound technical knowledge of our mes offering to identify commercial opportunities for the business
·       ability to work autonomously and a passion for new business development with a high level of account management skills.
·       ability to manage a small team of people from various disciplines within the business. 
·       experienced in the development and use of marketing techniques with a proven ability to compile business plans and assemble business cases is required
·       proficiency in german language a plus. 
benefits:
·       competitive annual base salary + commissions.
·       a truly global team of outstanding professionals.
·       unique software and a line of products that’s been recognized by key industry analysts.
submit your resume here
translator/technical documenter – erlangen, germany
aegis software is currently looking for an in-house translator and technical writer who will work primarily from english to german in an on-demand, deadline‑driven environment. this is an exciting opportunity to join a highly skilled team in a role offering excellent job security as our company continues to grow based in our erlangen, germany offices.  your primary role will involve:
·       translation of a wide range of technical and marketing texts from english to german
·       creation of technical documentation for our software products
·       revision of texts translated by other members of our organisation   
requirements:
·       at least one year experience as a translator from english to german (other languages a bonus)
·       an excellent first degree (a postgraduate qualification in translating would be an advantage)
·       an ability to meet deadlines and multi-task while working independently or as part of a team
·       a willingness to learn and apply new skills quickly and efficiently
·       strong it and online research skills
benefits:
·       competitive annual salary
·       a truly global team of outstanding professionals
·       unique software and a line of products that’s been recognized by key industry analysts
submit your resume here
sales applications engineer - north america
aegis software is seeking a talented applications engineer to join our team for the continued expansion of the company’s manufacturing execution software (mes). ideal applicants would be located in eastern/south eastern us but all locations will be considered.
responsibilities
•    provide regional support to aegis sales by communicating extensive product, technological, and practical knowledge as required during sales visits, web conferences, and teleconferences. 
•    conduct on-site manufacturing operation audits that define the customer’s ‘current state’, followed by reviews that identify opportunities for improvement
•    identify solutions to the customer’s stated needs that utilize existing aegis product functionality.  communicate product enhancement requests in a manner that identify the customer’s business need and a proposed solution when beyond the boundaries of aegis’ current product functionality.
•    draft custom engineering requests to aegis engineering that accurately describe the customer’s background, motivation, and requirements for functionality 
•    author technical response documentation in support of a sales opportunity (requests for proposals, capabilities matrices, and statements of work)
•    develop new or contribute to existing technical sales collateral such as interface/module overviews, product reference guides, and recorded demonstrations.
•    support webinars, trade shows, user group meetings, and similar events as required.
•    maintain a productive and collaborative relationship between sales applications and deployment to ensure a seamless transition for the customer.
•    support aegis in additional capacities as required, such as system installations, training sessions, and satellite office support.
•    continuous self-education on new releases including underlying technology and product capability, use, and installation.

must haves:
•    working knowledge of supporting application software, such as microsoft word, excel, powerpoint, and visio.
•    working knowledge of windows operating systems and server-based database software.
•    excellent written and oral communication skills.
•    possess the skills necessary to produce well-written summarized reports to management and colleagues. 
nice to have:
•    working knowledge of aegis mes product offering.
•    direct experience in electronics and box build assembly and test operations. 

travel required, ranging between 40-60% per month.  
submit your resume here
system deployment engineer - north america
aegis software is seeking talented deployment engineers to join our team for the continued expansion of the company’s manufacturing execution software (mes). candidate can be located anywhere in the us/canada/mexico within reasonable distance of major airport. ideal applicants would be located in canada within quebec/ontario or us west coast. as a deployment engineer you will be responsible for the successful installation, configuration and training on our software. this work includes travel to customer sites throughout north america.
responsibilities

train on and deploy aegis software products; including travel to customer facilities
manage and communicate tasks/timelines for a given deployment project
attend project status calls and meetings
prepare present and review agendas
daily trip reports documented and submitted back to aegis hq
telephone/web support for aegis software products
generation of product and reference documentation

requirements

previous experience as a software trainer or deployment engineer
good working knowledge of microsoft products, including ms office and client/server operating systems
strong verbal and written communication skills
willingness to travel domestically and internationally up to 75%

ideal candidate

previous experience with mes or erp software solutions
manufacturing experience particularly in the electronics assembly sector
located in canada within quebec/ontario or us west coast

submit your resume here
software engineer 
aegis software is seeking talented software developers to join our team for the continued development of the company’s commercial software products.  this includes enhancing, customizing and providing maintenance support for the companies’ entire product suite.
candidates should have a passion for software development using the latest and greatest technologies. our customers and market demand continual improvement through proper code structure, regular code reviews, and thorough testing on everything we produce. this ensures aegis offers the best functionality to our customers while maintaining performance in high transaction manufacturing environments.
responsibilities

strong development skills in object oriented programming language (c# preferred)
coordinating with aegis’ global service team to diagnose and correct software defects reported from the field
ability to work independently from a specification through aegis custom engineering process
enhancing the existing aegis product suite with a strong focus on the end user scenarios and requirements

requirements

bachelor’s degree in computer science or related degree
past employment with multi-tier software solution provider
working knowledge of full-stack .net development with ability to work across user interface, business logic, and data access layers
experience and/or working knowledge of one or more of the following technologies: wpf (windows presentation foundation), wcf (windows communication foundation), sql syntax, entity framework, linq, lambdas/delegates, .net remoting, visual studio 2015, tfs (team foundation server), devexpress, c++, winforms, unit testing, agile/scrum development processes
ideal candidate would also have experience in a manufacturing sector, or with a software provider involved in the manufacturing vertical

software deployment engineer - europe
aegis is currently looking for an experienced software deployment engineer to fulfil a position within our european services team. the ideal candidate would be based out of our uk office and would operate mainly within europe installing, configuring and training on our market leading manufacturing execution software (mes).
responsibilities

visit customers within europe, and occasionally globally, to install, configure and train on our mes software
carry out “in house” training programs for visiting customers
assist with product qa cycle and associated documentation
provide support for existing customers contacting us through our support network

requirements

previous experience as a professional software trainer or deployment engineer
good working knowledge of microsoft products, in particular client and server operating systems
strong working knowledge of microsoft sql
strong verbal and written communication skills
willingness to travel domestically and internationally around 50% of the time
must have full driving license

other skills

experience in electronics or other manufacturing sector a big plus
language skills, in particular german and french are desirable
