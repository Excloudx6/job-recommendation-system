job description
godaddy is looking for excited engineers who have a passion for problem solving.  this position is for our search platform team where you will play an integral part in the design and build of our next generation cloud ready search platform. this team of engineers is responsible for designing and delivering a performant search solution as well as developing automation tools to build, deploy, and monitor the performance of those systems. we are looking for passionate people with a strong agile background who want to be involved in cutting edge systems that are used regularly and are critical to the company delivering applications with a global footprint.what does the job entail?help godaddy build the next generation search platformunderstand all components of the stack in detail to build an end-to-end user experience that is sure to delight users of the platformwrite great code, innovate, lead, and contribute to technical architecture for scalable systems using open-source and cloud based solutionsbuild extensive monitoring and remediation infrastructure to validate and maintain uptime of services and ensure performanceparticipate in agile concepts such as daily stand-up meetings, task tracking boards, design and code reviews, automated testing, continuous integration and deploymentwork closely with service engineering, operations, and data users to engineer cutting-edge solutions that allow godaddy to answer challenging big data questionsdo you have what it takes?bs/ms in computer science or equivalent strong experience in python and/or javapassion to develop high-quality and extraordinary solutions to real complex distributed-computing problemsunderstanding of algorithms, data structures, and performance optimization techniqueswillingness to work in a dynamic agile environment and participate in all aspects of our agile processstrong interpersonal and communication skills, flexibility, team spirit, and positive approachhands-on experience with search and performance technologies (elasticsearch, lucene, newrelic) desiredexperience with current data technologies (spark, kafka, etc...) a plusknowledge of cassandra or other distributed data
