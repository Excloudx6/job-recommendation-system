 sr data and applied scientist









are you passionate about analyzing and modeling terabytes of customer data?  &nbsp do you consider yourself a data guru in developer’s shoes? does the thought of using data and insights to predict office usage fascinate you? want to be part of a team where the first question is always “what does the data tell us”? if so the office extensibility developer experience and analytics team (dxda) maybe the ideal job you are looking for.  &nbsp  office extensibility is creating the platform for developers to write applications for the cloud (office365) and the office client products.  &nbsp we are enabling developers to write apps for office client and server using the latest technologies such as azure, html5, javascript, sql and .net paired with efforts to integrate across mobile platforms, sharepoint, exchange, outlook, using open, modern and cloud friendly tools.  &nbsp see https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/office  &nbsp for what our developers can build today.  &nbsp our team - developer experience and analytics is a growing agile team tasked with turning our incredibly rich and big data into strategic assets that will drive business and engineering goals and efforts.  &nbsp our team is deeply focused on harnessing the business and engagement data to help drive change in our business strategy through data insights.  &nbsp as a member of our team, you will turn this data into actionable insights that will drive feature improvements to the office extensibility platform and its customers (developers and end users). our vision is to have a balanced team of data science and development skills. if you dream of being able to use data science/applied statistical techniques like: anomaly detection, machine learning/ predictive analytics and experimentation, then you should talk to us.  &nbsp if you are a thought leader in this space and want to play a key role in helping shape our strategy and execute on the same, we would really love to talk to you.  &nbsp  responsibilities  &nbsp • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp help our efforts to improve the way we gather and prepare data for analysis to help make critical business decisions and the kpi’s that matter most across a diversity of problem domains • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp understanding the data generated by experiments, and producing actionable, trustworthy conclusions from them. • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp employ machine learning to detect and correlate problems • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp build models, simulation, scalable and automated analytical systems • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp drive improvements to the product design and architecture, leading to increased customer satisfaction • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp lead and collaborate with experts from across the company to advance data science best practices  &nbsp • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp educate and train others on modern applications of data science techniques  &nbsp • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp learn how to build and sustain engagement from all levels of an organization  &nbsp basic qualifications: • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp bachelors or masters degree in a quantitative field, phd preferred. • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp 5+ years of industry work experience in sql, r, python to implement statistical models, machine learning, and analysis (prediction, classification, clustering, time series forecasting, regression models, etc.) in big data environment  &nbsp preferred qualifications: • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp advanced working knowledge of data mining using sql, etl and data warehouses  &nbsp • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp experience on large scale computing systems like cosmos, hadoop, mapreduce and/or similar systems preferred  &nbsp • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp experience with programming languages like java, c# is a plus  &nbsp • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp familiarity with deep learning toolkits, e.g. cntk, tensorflow, etc. is a plus  &nbsp • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp exceptional written and verbal communication to educate and work with cross functional teams  &nbsp •
