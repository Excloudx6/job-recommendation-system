data analyst

        at cars.com
      

        300 s. riverside
      


cars.com is one of chicago’s original tech companies. our online platform makes it easier for consumers to shop for, sell and service their cars. with our expert content, mobile app features, millions of new and used vehicle listings, a comprehensive set of research tools and the largest database of consumer reviews in the industry, cars.com offers innovative products to connect consumers with dealers across the country.
data is the driver for our future at cars. we’re searching for collaborative, analytical, and innovative engineers to help utilize the almost 20 years of data we have at our disposal. if you are passionate about using data to solve problems and build game changing products, we’d love to work with you!
required skills: 

partner with business stakeholders to develop analytical solutions
build complex queries on large data sets used to make informed decisions and demonstrate cars.com value to local and national customers
translate business needs into data visualizations
collaborate with data platform teams influencing processes and technologies used to deliver solutions
foster self-service analytic environment by providing domain and tool expertise
perform root cause analysis on reporting and data issues.

required experience: 

3-5 years in sql programming
experience with data visualization tools such as tableau, power bi or qlikview
ability to work with large databases such as oracle, teradata and/or a big data environment
solid understanding of data warehousing concepts and technologies
ability to communicate with internal and external teams both verbally and written
bachelor’s degree in computer science, mathematics, engineering or related field

preferred

experience with agile methodologies
proficiency in python or r
experience with hive or impala
experience with cloud based technologies like aws
