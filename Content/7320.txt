job description
peoplecaddie is looking for contractor/temporary business intelligence analysts to assist our clients in the chicagoland area.

the primary role of the business intelligence analyst is to leverage analytical and technical skills to innovate, build, and maintain well-managed data solutions and capabilities to tackle lending risk related problems. this role also supports forecasting, development, implementation, documentation, and reporting of key risk metrics used for lines of business, legal entities, and geographic differences for management, and regulatory reporting. 

essential functions 

develop understanding of companies business, lending services in particular.
use digital technologies to mine complex, voluminous, and different varieties of data sources and platforms
design, develop, implement and test business intelligence solutions using tableau, sql, sas and business objects including data structures, universes and reports to create new reports or converting already existing reports to tableau/business objects.

partner with various teams within lending services to provide consultancy and translate the business needs to design and develop tools, techniques, metrics, and dashboards for insights and data visualization.
drive an understanding and adherence to the principles of data quality management including metadata, lineage, and business definitions
work collaboratively with appropriate 
identify required data and work with data ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and independently and implement rapid changes in project direction.

licenses and certifications
sas/ sql/ business objects certifications a plus.
