data scientist

        at eero
      

        san francisco
      


data powers the future of eero. we rely on data to inform and drive product decisions, improve network and mesh performance, set marketing strategies, enhance factory testing, provide better customer support and much more. as a data scientist at eero, you will participate in product development by bringing analytical and statistical expertise to solve new and complex problems. working closely with our product & engineering leadership, you will dig deep into the largest data set from consumer mesh networks ever compiled to drive the understanding, growth and success of eero.
you will…

partner with product and engineering teams to develop key performance indicators, answer important questions with data and identify new product opportunities
apply your expertise in quantitative analysis, data mining, and data storytelling to build compelling stories about how our users interact with our product
develop statistical models on large data sets to better understand our business and the drivers of change
present your analysis with logical recommendations to influence product direction and communicate the state of the business
build and automate actionable analytics and reporting for key business processes
innovate to derive meaning from new data sources and devise new measurement frameworks
mentor others within eero in sql, analytics, statistics, and all things data  

currently, you…

have 3+ years of work experience in quantitative analysis with a demonstrable business impact
possess strong analytic skills and the ability to synthesize large datasets into meaningful insights.
are building statistical models to segment and predict user behavior
design and evaluate key performance indicators and experiments to validate hypotheses
build datasets from large amounts of structured and unstructured data using sql and the hadoop ecosystem (redshift, vertica, spark, hive, presto, pig, etc)
possess deep understanding statistics and application utilizing r or python pandas
have demonstrable general purpose coding experience with a dynamic language such as python, ruby, node.js, php, clojure, etc
build key data sets and compelling, intelligible visualizations using tools such as looker or tableau
have a phd or ms degree in math, physics, statistics, economics, computer science, or other quantitative fields.
are confident in your communication and presentation skills

bonus points if you…

have deployed production models to predict or classify
have coding experience in languages such as scala, java, c++, c#, or go
