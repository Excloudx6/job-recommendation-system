affinipay is looking for a senior front-end software developer to help us build our next generation user interfaces. our front-end engineers work together with our ux team to deliver modern, great looking, and easy-to-use interfaces to our user base of lawyers, cpa's, and other professionals.
responsibilities include:

owning the design and implementation of application features within our node.js/reactjs web interfaces
working to improve processes and technologies
iterating on new and existing features to improve usability, performance, and security
deploying and managing applications within aws

requirements:

bs in computer science or engineering
5+ years professional backend software development
3+ years experience with a javascript ui framework (backbone, angular, ember, or reactjs)
proficient in javascript
experience creating interfaces that are responsive, accessible, and international
experience with rails a plus
experience with react native a plus
experience with application containers a plus
knowledgeable in mysql, postgresql, or oracle db environments and orm frameworks
experience implementing and debugging large scale solutions in cloud-based environments
familiar with linux environments
experience with security-related development best practices

about affinipay
based in austin, texas, affinipay is a software-driven financial technologies company focused on creating uis and apis that make it easy for our partners to add payments and payment-related features to their applications. our custom payment technology, as well as our best-in-class customer support, has won the endorsements of the american bar association and 46 state bars, and has made us the leader in the legal industry. affinipay has been listed as one of austin business journal’s fastest growing companies for the last 3 years. check out our website at www.affinipay.com.
