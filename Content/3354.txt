software engineer ii - 3d gui
canonsburg, pa
job summary
the ansys electronic 3d gui product group is focused on developing graphical user interfaces that drive our industry-leading electrical simulation products. we are seeking a software engineer with a passion for user interface and product development. the successful candidate will perform design, development and maintenance of various components for hfss, maxwell, q3d extractor and other industry-leading ansys products; in a highly collaborative, multidisciplinary team environment.
essential functions
• perform software development activities including design, implementation, maintenance, testing, and documentation.
• employ best software practices.
• investigate problems discovered by qa or product support and develop solutions. perform bug verification and release testing.
• understand the requirements for a product, including target environment, performance criteria, and competitive issues.
   
   
job minimum requirements and qualifications
• master’s degree in computer science, engineering, or related technical field, or a bachelor’s degree with three years of professional experience.
• significant software development experience using c++ or related language.
• good knowledge of data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented design.
  
   
other information
• a master’s degree is preferred.
• experience developing and debugging user interface code on windows and/or linux.
• familiarity with the entire development process, including specification, documentation, quality assurance, and use of software engineering tools.
• strong technical knowledge in software development methodologies with experience in project planning and implementation. ability to complete high quality work on schedule.
• familiarity with electronic simulation and tools is a plus.
• good communications and interpersonal skills.
