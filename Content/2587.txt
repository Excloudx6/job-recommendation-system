internal job grade: b4
job description summary
bank of the west is looking for a software engineer to work within the digital channels team to develop and support www.bankofthewest.com as well as other in-house developed digital applications and services.  responsible for the overall design, development, modification, testing, and/or implementation of short- and long-term software solutions to meet both business and technical needs. review and translate business requirements into technology solutions. work within an agile project team to deliver digital solutions.
essential job functions
 

develop and test ui software for primary website and related services. 

develop new user-facing features and solutions to meet marketing, product, and ux/vd requirements. 

ensure the technical feasibility of ux/vd requirements.  

share recommendations and technical solutions as well as alternative solutions with the overall project team.

build reusable code and libraries for future use.

conduct a wide range of quality control tests across both desktop and mobile to ensure that developed software meets or exceeds specified standards and end-user requirements.

participate in agile sprint planning, sprint grooming and retrospectives.  

contribute to release planning and deployment guides as needed.

coordinate and communicate application support activities with key stakeholders.

provide technical support and mentoring to team members as needed.

participate the development of software policies, standards and procedure.

leads users in the planning of user acceptance testing, alpha and beta testing

creates test data files with valid and invalid records to thoroughly test program logic and verify system flow

manage environments, implement/deploy software upgrade to various environments and support post implementation.required experience
 

5+ years of experience translating wireframes and visual designs into quality code.

experience with html5, css3, backbone js, twitter bootstrap, javascript and jquery.

experience with server-side css pre-processing platforms, such as less and sass.

understanding of asynchronous request handling, partial page updates and ajax.

experience with image authoring tools.

working knowledge of cross-browser compatibility issues and relative solutions.

understanding of seo principles, analytics and web standards.

working knowledge and applied experience of ada compliance requirements.

familiarity with enterprise cms (i.e. sitecore).

experience with tfs and hp quality center.

bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience. education
