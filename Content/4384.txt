principal data engineer
                        













requisition id: 
                        
36666
                        








business unit: 
                        
corporate
                        








location: 
                        
portland, or, us, 97209
                        








logistics done differently.
as a systems engineer analyst principal, with exposure to data science, advanced data analytics, python, r, artificial intelligence, machine learning, sql, visualization tools such as tableau, fundamental statistics, data warehousing etc., you will love this job which offers you, the tools, the team, the data, the business problems and the scope to advance an enterprise in the path of data sciences and advanced analytics.   you will be part of the bi data engineering team of about 20+ dynamic engineers with diversified capabilities.  this team is responsible for end to end data management which includes, data modeling, data integration, data warehousing and providing business benefits through data exploration, data visualization and development of machine learning / artificial intelligence (ai) models.  engineers are specialized in some with hands-on capabilities on most of the tools which include, erwin, informatica, netezza, aws (redshift, ec2 and s3), oltp databases,  python, r, elastic search and iot's.   this team is self-reliant and manages the capabilities right from tool selections & poc's  to project deliveries.  the team is also self-content managing our own private repository in git and administering our own projects in jira.
pay, benefits and more.
we are eager to attract the best, so we offer competitive compensation and a generous benefits package, including full health insurance (medical, dental and vision), 401(k), life insurance, disability, and the opportunity to participate in a company incentive plan.
what you’ll do on a typical day:

establish and maintain solid business relationships and work effectively with leadership teams and senior business contacts.
gain and upkeep a system-wide, architectural view of the data solutions - you are required to have system-wide, architectural view of the deployed data solutions, and you consistently think in terms of architecture.
work with customers and business teams to identify relevant customer uses cases, customer needs and business opportunities that enhances the products we support;
develop product requirements documents to communicate data product features and decisions clearly to all stakeholders (business and technical)
translate and communicate business needs in clear technical terms to the technical resources and the technical details in simple business terms to the leadership and non-technical people
identify, evaluate and integrate the data sources and ideal solutions that would best fulfill business requirements
play a leadership role in your team
demonstrate good judgement making technical trade-offs between your group’s short term and long term business needs, and the needs of the company as a whole
collaborate closely with the engineering team to align system design decisions to drive technical product goals
provide solutions and guidance to the team members on technical and business issues facing them; where warranted, you will play a hands-on role
guide the team with clear priorities and driving effective time management of the team 
partner with enterprise data science team to prioritize product development priorities

what you need to succeed at xpo:
at a minimum, you’ll need:

master's degree in cs, mis, or equivalent is required.  
successfully completed recognized data science / advanced data analytical courses
7+ years of professional experience in bi / data warehouse development preferably in a medium to large data warehouse environment. 
hands-on experience in delivering solutions in at least two of data modeling, data integration, data analysis, sql, r/python and machine learning
demonstrated success in guiding data science projects to generate business insights and recommendations
understanding of core principles of data warehousing, data science and machine learning
hands on experience building logical data models and physical data models and normalizing techniques with 3nf
experience in mpp databases like netezza or teradata. 
well-developed written and verbal communication skills in customer settings and with internal management. well-developed active listening, facilitation and presentation skills.

it’d be great if you also have:

strong understanding of statistics to drive analysis around large structured and unstructured data sets
masters or phd in data science, statistics, computer science or related field (or equivalent work experience)
knowledge in cloud solutions (preferable in amazon suite of products)
experience in transportation / logistics industry

be part of something big.
xpo provides cutting-edge supply chain solutions to the world's most successful companies, including disney, pepsi, l'oréal, toyota and many others. we’re the fastest-growing transportation company on the fortune 500 list and we’re just getting started.
