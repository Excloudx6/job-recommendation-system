software engineer-robotics 





software
pittsburgh, pa
posted 3 weeks ago


iq inc. is dedicated to providing creative, high-quality software to our clients while supplying a rewarding, fun, collaborative, and flexible work environment to our team. we are currently in need of an ambitious software engineer with experience in c++ and python, with great attitude and aptitude, to work in the robotics field.
we are looking for software engineers with a background developing robust systems to work on adding to, maintaining, optimizing and improving our existing ros ecosystem and develop systems to enhance the robustness of the robot software.
we also need someone who is an analytical thinker, motivated by working as part of a terrific team, is great with people, and doesn’t take themselves too seriously.
you must have:

a bs or ms in engineering, sciences, mathematics (or equivalent relevant work experience)
3+ years of practical experience in writing robust, production-quality code in c++ and python
experience developing one or more of the following types of systems/algorithms: simulators, navigation, mapping, localization, or calibration
experience with ros
track record working on all stages of the software development life cycle
strong software design skills, including debugging, performance analysis, and test design
practical experience with iterative development and testing on robots
fluent with a linux development environment
strong problem solving skills and ability to learn quickly

it would be great if you also had:

experience using ansible
experience using cloud services
familiarity with containers (docker or similar)

sound interesting? send us your resume at resume@iq-inc.com today.
