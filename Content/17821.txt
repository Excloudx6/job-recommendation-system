					intern - software developer
				


						tracking code
					

						1932-236 
					

						job description
					

vml is all about putting everything you are into everything you do. we're a full-service advertising agency that thrives on moving brands forward by inspiring a human connection.
 the softwared developer internship is for someone who’s innovative and provides creative, new concepts and industry knowledge. you must contribute to the successful completion of major projects by investigating, analyzing and resolving complex application problems; designs and codes and compiles and tests complex websites and applications; employs standards, security methods and tools related to building enterprise web applications; works with project management and leadership to provide time estimates and schedules for projects; communicates regularly with department management and functional users on project requirements, activities and status; participates in technical reviews and feasibility studies and interprets documentation; and follows software engineering standards, processes and techniques.
 responsibilities:

working with the technical director, technical architect, app/web developer, database administrator, system administrator, project management, account management and creative to create best-in-class websites and applications, completing all requirements and meeting usability expectations
developing and/or maintaining a sustainable front-end framework and coordinating with other team members
partnering with technology personnel to develop creative marketing technology solutions in support of meeting client expectations
estimating timelines for assigned projects and ensuring that work is completed within the estimated timeline
participating in the development and implementation of short- and long-term technology plans and related policies and procedures
following standards that will facilitate a quality it infrastructure across vml and all clients

 as part of the wpp group, vml offers one of the best overall compensation packages in the business. vml is headquartered in kansas city.


						required skills
					


junior, senior or nontraditional student in college or recent graduate with coursework or experience in a related field
review creative work and identify issues with layout, consistency, browser compatibility, etc.
have a strong understanding of client-side and server-side, pros/cons of using
possess superior analytical and problem-solving abilities
have excellent organizational, interpersonal, presentation and communication skills
able to anticipate and independently provide solutions for complex problems and issues, recommending upgrades, enhancements, priorities and strategic technology directions based on independent research, consultation and analysis
can rapidly absorb complex technical and conceptual information, identifying key issues and details, and present understandable alternatives to both technical and nontechnical individuals at all levels of the organization
able to work on multiple complex tasks against deadlines and shifting priorities and are comfortable dealing with uncertainty, ambiguity and change
can identify the practical implications of new technologies




						required experience
					

experience with the most updated versions of front-end technologies and highly proven proficiency working with the following:

html5, css3, javascript and javascript libraries (jquery, backbone, angular, etc.), ajax, json, build tools (grunt, sass, less, etc.), responsive and mobile-optimized development
adobe photoshop and creating "pixel perfect" html/css from .psd files
strong understanding of seo best practices
strong understanding of web architecture concepts (http request/response, session, cache, cookies)
client-side performance optimization
facebook integration
