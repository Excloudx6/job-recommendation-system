descriptionintroduction
as our company slogan
states, the staples’ supply chain is making
things happen for our customers every day through our network of more than
30 warehouses, 1300 1200+ stores, and hundreds of delivery trucks.
and even though we
already operate the best supply chain in our industry, we need talented
individuals like you to help us leverage advanced analytics and optimization
algorithms to achieve breakthrough performance levels.
position
summary
the scientist is responsible for applying
advanced analytical methods to drive business-critical decisions at staples.  using mathematical optimization, statistical
analysis and machine learning the individual plays a key role in supporting the
development of state-of-the-art solutions to complex decision-making problems.
these problems will
come from all parts of the staples supply chain and will include challenges
like:
developing and implementing vehicle routing
algorithms to optimize last mile parcel delivery and build optimization models
to support the development of an international transportation network using
multiple modes including truck load, ltl and ocean freightdeveloping demand prediction models to estimate
transportation and labor capacity and build scheduling tools to support
improvements in delivery speed and customer service while driving growth and increase
profitability building and deploying algorithms that reduce
order fulfilment costs across the staples warehouse and transportation network
through robotic automation, process control, and capacity optimization
primary responsibilities
work
closely with key business partners to understand critical, complex, data-driven
business and operations challenges, and then apply analytical methods to solve
them – resulting in a significant, positive impact on the staples’ bottom-line.research,
design, and implement data models and innovative algorithms to analyze diverse
sources of data to realize actionable resultscommunicate
results to executive leadership
qualificationsbasic qualifications
ms
in computer science, statistics, operations research, applied mathematics, applied
physics or related field, plus 3-5 years industry experiencephd
in computer science, statistics, operations research, applied mathematics, applied
physics or related field, plus 0-3 years industry experiencehands-on
experience with mathematical modeling, and mathematical optimization techniques
and technologies as well as advanced machine learning, data mining, statistical
inferencehands-on
experience with querying databases in order to drive required analysis
independently and swiftlyexcellent
ability to communicate complex quantitative analysis in a clear, precise, and
actionable manner with both technical and non-technical stakeholders.keen
attention to detail, and ability to dive deep
 
preferred qualifications
experience
developing large scale systems using parallel and distributed computingexperience
working with development toolsets such as spark, hadoop, python, c++, java, r,
h2o, shiny, docker, mysql, teradata, nosql, etc.experience
using deterministic and non-deterministic mathematical programming, constraint
satisfaction, advanced supervised and unsupervised learning techniques, natural
language processing, robotics, markov decision process, and control theory experience
with various areas of the supply chain such as customer order, inventory,
warehouse, and transportation managementexperience
with financial modelling
