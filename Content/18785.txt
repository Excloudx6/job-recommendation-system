software developer intern

                    xero internships (check out xero.com/grads) | san francisco, usa
                





check out our new site! xero.com/gradssoftware developer intern 
our purpose
small business makes the world go round – it’s the heart of the global economy. at xero we want millions of small businesses to thrive through beautiful software, advice and connections. we aim to make being a small business more efficient and profitable, and more enjoyable too. 
how you’ll make an impact
the role of the developer intern is to undertake, under the guidance of a mentor, software application development on applications in our suite of products (including new ones), contributing to new or improved experiences for our customers, internal and external.if you love writing code, our team of engineers could be the place for you. we show our customers love by creating great new features and fixing the occasional bug, and we do it all at speed.
what you'll do
our dev interns join a wide range of small product teams, including those that build new strategic applications, make our existing applications even more beautiful, focus on our mobile platform, and ensure that our apps remain highly-available, secure, scalable, robust and high-performing.
step into our world of full stack developers, front-end and back-end specialists, and quality assurance engineers. you see your code make a difference in the lives of xero users after each release. you'll work across front-end and back-end components for web, mobile and apis that build on top of .net, open-source web frameworks, and android and ios – all deployed and managed through aws.
requirements
our teams are comprised of proactive communicators who take initiative and come up with great ideas. we have an agile, highly diverse team of people who are passionate about what they do and motivated to take it to the next level. we’re eager to see applications from students with a wide range of qualifications. ideally, you will:
have a passion for writing code and also appreciate the customer experience.be able to prove your technical chops by showing us what you’ve been working on outside of university. you may have played around with a new framework, learned a new language, built (or partially built) something, been active on stack overflow, or have a great github profile.be a great communicator who likes to work closely in a team environment, and be able to take a logical, analytical approach to your work, cope with change and be eager to learn.
xero is an equal opportunity employer.
