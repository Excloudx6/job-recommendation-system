software developer
description
at pearson, we’re committed to a world that’s always learning and to our talented team who makes it all possible. from bringing lectures vividly to life to turning textbooks into laptop lessons, we are always re-examining the way people learn best, whether it’s one child in our own backyard or an education community across the globe. we are bold thinkers and standout innovators who motivate each other to explore new frontiers in an environment that supports and inspires us to always be better.
by pushing the boundaries of technology — and each other to surpass these boundaries — we create seeds of learning that become the catalyst for the world’s innovations, personal and global, large and small.
overview:
the software developer will participate in all phases of the software development lifecycle, working independently and as a part of a small team implementing and maintaining existing moderately complex software products. this position is responsible for coding, testing and debugging new software or making enhancements to existing software.
the developer manages the full software development lifecycle, which includes testing, implementation and auditing. you will also aid in the creation of technical documents and providing the necessary technical information to support the software. receives very minimal supervision. draws on diverse, multi-year experience to propose and/or implement constructive methods and procedures.
primary responsibilities:

design, develop, implement and maintain moderately complex software products
code, debug and make recommendations on software enhancements
participate in requirements gathering, definition and documentation
conduct unit and integration testing on software for functionality and limits
analyze old and current code for problem resolution
develop high-quality code, establishing development standards and practice by example
serve as a technical resource and mentor

qualifications
education & experience:

associates degree/bachelors of arts/bachelors of science in computer science or a computer related discipline or the equivalent experience in computer software development
at least 2 years of experience in a software development environment preferred
development experience using xslt, java and xml
development experience using rest, html or javascript preferred
experience in quarkxpress helpful

skills, knowledge & abilities:

demonstrated programming skills including design, code review, implementation, testing, and debugging
solid knowledge/experience of test automation tools, defect-tracking, and/or change management tools
some understanding of database concepts, including sql and db schemas
excellent verbal and written communication skills to describe challenges and solutions in both technical and layman’s terms
great attention to detail, passionate about quality of deliverables, meeting deadlines and client satisfaction
effective relationship, time management, and prioritization skills
creative problem-solving, strong technical and tactical thinking, demonstrated by ability to troubleshoot
ability to learn new products and technical concepts quickly
self-motivated and pro-active with strong curiosity and desire for self-development
