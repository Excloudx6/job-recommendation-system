biziq is growing!
this is an in-office position. no telecommuting or freelance regardless of how spectacular your skills are. you must be local to the metropolitan phoenix area for immediate consideration.
we're looking for a positive, energetic, enthusiastic, experienced front end  web developer/designer to join our winning team!
to apply:
email your resume with the title of this posting along with a brief cover letter explaining why you have applied and how you are the best candidate for the position. also please include your direct email address. many job boards do not forward it when applying through the job board. please also include a link to your website or portfolio.
or apply on our careers page at http://bit.ly/2luhx7c
position details:
our company is based in the *arcadia / biltmore* area of phoenix, az . our sales representatives are crushing their sales goals and we need more front end web developers/designers to support the influx of new sales, so we've made a commitment to expanding our development team. we have an upbeat, high energy, safe and fun environment.
we are looking for someone who is a self-starter, works well with others across and is able to adapt to changes quickly in a high-pressure environment. the candidate for front end web developer/designer must be highly motivated, extremely organized, team-oriented, creative, and possess strong verbal and written communication skills.
job responsibilities:

customizing wordpress template themes to customer specifications
design custom website templates for our clients to use
improve usability and features of our dashboards and client facing reporting
make updates to client websites
work with marketing department to develop sales support materials

requirements to be considered for hire:

4+ years of front-end website development experience
4+ years of extensive up-to-date, in depth knowledge and proven development experience of modern html and css
3+ years of jquery/javascript experience
experience using responsive design techniques and progressive enhancement in a mobile-first approach
knowledge of how to optimize css/html/javascript for rapid page load
ability to work in a rapid development production environment on time-sensitive projects and tasks
ability to work from a task list and manage your work with little assistance
cross-browser compatibility including explorer 8, firefox, chrome, safari, and major tablet/mobile browsers
4+ years of working in and around wordpress themes and plugins not requiring ftp
4+ years experience with adobe photoshop
passion for user experience
strong verbal and written communication skills
basic understanding of php and mysql is a plus

bonus points:

mobile app development experience
landing page conversion/lead generation experience
email marketing design best practices
project management experience
as or bs in computer science or equivalent experience
experience in modifying wordpress page templates and plugins
video storyboarding
video production skills

benefits to working with biziq:

competitive salary based on experience
401k, medical and dental benefits
paid time off
paid holidays
fun, energetic atmosphere
espresso machine
open concept work environment
no weekends or travel required
relaxed and social company culture

we are scheduling interviews now for immediate hire!
no walk-ins, please. all interviews must be scheduled.
