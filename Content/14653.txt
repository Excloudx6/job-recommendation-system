associate software engineer - (remote position) - (1830.14)
×close this details all the information about the job posting.     











														
																	job description
																
															

support and work with a small team of web engineers using ruby on rails, react native, foundation, and other cool technologies. we're a division of monster.com and are responsible for a collection of platforms and websites designed to help millions of users advance their careers and education. we currently need an associate software engineer to help us round out the team and are hoping you're passionate about designing and coding well-crafted, high-performance software and enjoy working with a small, dedicated team. ideal candidates will have experience with frontend design and implementation and a desire to learn and contribute to backend technologies as well.




														
																	job requirements
																
															

design, develop, enhance, and maintain current and new ruby on rails applications using foundation as a frontend framework and react native applications for deployment on mobile devices
participate in all aspects of our agile development process including requirements analysis and design; work closely with technical, design, and product teams  
communicate regularly with team members and management in a clear, concise manner
provide input to execute on efforts to improve the efficiency and quality of our development projects    
conduct and assist in unit, integration, and production testing
stay current with latest technologies and best practices being used by the larger software community
 
required experience:
4-year bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent
1+ years of ruby on rails web application development experience
1+ years of react native or mobile apps development experience preferred
