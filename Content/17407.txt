the new republic is looking for a talented and creative front-end developer and designer to play a lead role in redesigning our website and reimagining our digital products.
we’re looking for someone who obsesses over the needs of users and who can craft elegant, polished designs that will improve their experience and expand our loyal base of readers and subscribers. candidates should be exploding with ideas, possessed of a sharp eye for design and a pixel-perfect attention to detail, and committed to collegiality and excellence in everything they do. the front-end developer will identify, design, and build editorial initiatives—from news projects and social campaigns to daily newsletters—as well as events, sales, and marketing products. this full-time position is based in our new york office, and reports to the director of digital operations.
 roles and responsibilities
 • collaborate closely with key staff to design and implement products for mobile, tablet, and desktop, from an extensive redesign to sales and marketing integrations. 
• work closely with the back-end developer to launch new and elegant products and to improve existing ones, while prioritizing multiple feature requests and product bugs.
• establish design guidelines and standards to ensure consistent user experiences across platforms.
• author css using bem methodologies with css preprocessing tools such as scss.
• define problems and goals, map user flows, prototype interactions, and oversee the building process.
• develop quality product mock-ups and prototypes.
• assist the back-end engineer with coding and troubleshooting. 
• collaborate with the back-end engineer to produce designs with clean, performant, maintainable code.
• keep tabs on best practices and trends in digital media to inform future projects to grow tnr’s audience.
• communicate regularly with key staff on project status, needs, and milestones.
• execute weekly experiments to improve conversion rates. 
• create and integrate services to improve editorial flow.
• train staff on new products.
 qualifications
 • 3 to 5 years experience with front-end development and visual design.
• expert knowledge of javascript (babel and bleeding edge es6+ features).
• demonstrated experience utilizing react.js.
• thorough understanding of mailchimp or ability to learn the marketing tool.
• experience optimizing mobile web applications. 
• familiarity with analytical and diagnostic tools for web performance.
• experience with wordpress.
• ability to communicate technical issues to a nontechnical staff. 
• ability to meet tight deadlines in a fast-paced editorial environment.
• ability to handle multiple short- and long-term projects simultaneously 
 apply
the position includes a competitive salary and excellent benefits. diversity is important to us in everything we do. we encourage applicants of all ages, genders, sexual orientations, races, and ethnicities. to apply, please submit your résumé, a portfolio that includes links to your work, and a cover letter detailing your qualifications for the position. the position is open until filled. no phone calls or snail mail, please.
