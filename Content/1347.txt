description


position overview:the climate corporation is looking for a predictive analytics, deep learning intern to join our seeds & placement team in the st. louis office. we are seeking a highly motivated graduate-level student who shares our passion for innovation in the area of predictive analytics. if you are interested in applying your analytical skills to help farmers, the climate corporation would love to talk to you!what you will do:support climate’s efforts in decision agriculture by developing novel methods and leveraging big data to solve tasks that will disentangle the factors of variation from product performance, weather, and field characteristics to enable the field level advisor and better farmer experience<spanlearn the decision-making process in climate’s product development pipeline to gain experience of implementing big data analytics models in the ag industry.basic qualifications:must be currently enrolled in a masters or phd degree program in statistics, mathematics, computer science, operations research, or a related discipline with a focus on machine learningdemonstrated programming skills with r or python or java, and related deep learning packagesdemonstrated experience of using certain open-source deep learning packages (e.g., tensorflow) to solve real-world problemspreferred qualifications:theoretical and practical experience of deep learning research:  autoencoders and restricted boltzmann machines for feature abstraction; general deep learning algorithms such as convolutional neural network (cnn) and recurrent neural network (rnn)familiarity with spatial predictive modeling is a plusfamiliarity with heterogeneous modeling is a plusstrong written and oral communication skillswhat we offer:our environment is extremely engaging and fast-paced, with a diverse set of top engineers, agronomists, scientists, and statisticians working together to provide the best possible products and experiences for our customers.we offer competitive pay and perks.we provide meals and a large assortment of snacks and drinks to get you through the day.you’ll get 1:1 mentoring from a talented and motivated employee on your team.you’ll have the opportunity to work on real projects with experienced employees and have a direct impact on the company. (our internships are not summer camp!)you’ll have the amazing opportunity to participate in one of our biotechnology tours where you get out of the office and into the field (literally) with agents and farmers.you’ll work in an award winning office with smart designs, large common areas and open floor plans that fuel collaboration.you’ll attend various company sponsored events, activities and off-sites - we make sure interns are having fun!we regularly host meet-up groups and tech-talks and encourage participation in relevant workshops and conferencesyou’ll have the opportunity to interaction with key executives and leadership.#li-dnp
