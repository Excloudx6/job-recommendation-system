data analyst 

        at whoop
      
(view all jobs)

        boston
      


at whoop we’re passionate about solving data challenges on the cutting edge of physiological thought. we’re seeking a talented data analyst, excited to answer never before asked questions, armed with our massive, growing biometric and performance data. your work will directly impact the performance optimization of whoop’s growing elite and professional athlete user base while supporting whoop’s thought leadership in performance analytics.
responsibilities

work across whoop teams to support a broad variety of data analysis initiatives. 
capable in sql & python with little technical assistance from others. 
communicate progress and results to both technical team members (data scientists) and non-technical team members (management and product).
ability to interpret results using statistical techniques
this position will occasionally require direct interactions with clients in order to understand their needs and communicate findings. 

desirable

2+ years of analyst experience, with expertise using the scientific python stack and sql (or similar language) 
strong communication skills
strong interpersonal skills
understanding of statistics 
basic understanding of exercise physiology
