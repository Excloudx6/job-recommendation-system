requisition id: 17023803primary location:united states-north carolina-raleighcategory:r&d, computer science & engineeringdescriptionconduent is the world's largest provider of diversified business process services with leading capabilities in transaction processing, automation, analytics and constituent experience. we work with both government and commercial customers in assisting them to deliver quality services to the people they serve.we manage interactions with patients and the insured for a significant portion of the u.s. healthcare industry. we are the customer interface for large segments of the technology industry and the operational and processing partner of choice for public transportation systems around the world.  whether it's digital payments, claims processing, benefit administration, automated tolling, customer care or distributed learning - conduent manages and modernizes these interactions to create value for both our clients and their constituents. learn more at www.conduent.com.position description: data scientist - business process modeling, optimization, and automationwe’re seeking experienced researchers interested in multidisciplinary business process modeling and optimization work. this area of research develops methods, algorithms and tools to support the design, orchestration, management and analysis of business processes and service operations. we work on real world processes and take pride in developing tools and technologies to drive data-driven process improvements. this includes redesigning processes and the infrastructure that supports the execution of these processes. individuals in this area will work in a team environment and be responsible for creating new state of the art business process modeling and optimization services and solutions for transportation, healthcare, financial and other industrial applications. we work closely with our engineering and operations departments to understand the current systems, develop and prototype technologies, and help deliver real world end-to-end solutions. our team consists of researchers and engineers with expertise in disciplines of computer science, operations research, ethnography, analytics, system modeling and simulation. skill sets requirement for this opening include operations research, management science and analytics applied to modeling and optimizing business processes.primary responsibilities include:research and analysis of bpm systems and process modeling for system behavior, modeling prediction, diagnostics and optimization. the individuals filling this position play a key role in the conceptualization, analysis, design, and optimization of business processes through analysis, simulations and pilot studies.communicating with research and business teams in order to develop partnerships through cross-functional collaboration. in addition to internal collaborations, the researchers would also collaborate with academic and research institutions across the country with projects at ms and phd level.journal and conference publications are also expected.qualifications:phd or post-doc in the fields of operations research, industrial engineering, computational and mathematical sciences with focus on modeling and optimization of business processes, workflow management and automationcandidates with bs/ms degree with
