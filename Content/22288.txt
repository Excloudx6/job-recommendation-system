        details:



                this role is responsible for developing advanced analytic solutions that are leveraged by usma (us medical affairs) for critical business decisions in applications such as medical engagement effectiveness, influence network measurement of external experts, resource allocation for medical resource deployment, product launch analyses, predictive modeling to solve strategic business questions, and medical voice of customer/patient to uncover insights for strategic decision making.  a primary focus will be consulting with the usma business to understand their critical needs and determine appropriate advanced analytic solutions aligned with operating procedures and policies that enable better customer focus and scientific engagement.  included within this responsibility will be thinking through approaches, developing advanced analytics solutions and timely insights for new product launches.  strong project management, communication, technical skills, presentation skills and innovative/flexible thinking are critical to be successful in this role.you will have a significant impact on the business and will create a gsk and usma competitive advantage by driving industry leading advanced analytics solutions through data synthesis/integration and maximizing use of analytics tools and frameworks to support pipeline and marketed products.  innovation will stem from advanced analytics and method application that uncovers new customer/patient insights, data visualization to visualize analytic insights and output in a simple yet powerful way to inspire decisions, and maximizing the use of voice of customer and unstructured data sources.  the analytics deliverables will inform and shape discussions in the most senior leadership teams and across franchises and functions in r&d and commercial.  about gsk medical affairs:our medical affairs physicians and scientists are the experts on our medicines/vaccines and an extremely valuable part of our global business. in all countries where our medicines and vaccines are marketed, these professionals help to ensure that healthcare professionals can understand the appropriate use of our products, and also ensure that the patient remains at the heart of our decision making. our global medical organization is at the forefront of the industry-leading changes to improve patient treatment options. we are committed to making gsk’s medical affairs team the best in our industry, and a trusted valued partner in the delivery of healthcare to patients.*li-gsk
