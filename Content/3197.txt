software engineer
job descriptiondescription:come and join network platform group as a software engineer.these are the main responsibilities for this job:design, coding, and integration of 4g/5g lte base station software on system on a chip soc. ensure the base station software meets all production quality requirements through validation testing of functionality and performance. work with partners, customers and other internal teams to deliver highly optimized next generation wireless technology software for widely deployed products.utilize the latest technology and programming techniques to realize the full capacity of silicon.code, document, design, and release according to latest industry proven software processes.help design scalable software architectures that run in a virtualized environments.create software that is modular, well documented, and with fully integrated diagnostics to minimize resolution time for reported defects and maximize code reuse.qualificationsyou must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. relevant experience can be obtained through school work, classes and project work, internships, military training, and/or work experience. this is an entry level position and will be compensated accordingly.minimum requirements:- candidate most have an ms degree in computer science engineering, electrical engineering, or related field.- minimum 1 year of experience in:c/c++ and debugging skills.wireless technologies: lte and next gen 5g standards.rt (real time linux kernel.preferred requirements:  networking protocols.soc (cpu and dsp architecture).dma engine operation.cache and memory management.lte test equipment tm500, ixia, cue's, vsa, wavejudge, etcinside this business groupthe data center group (dcg) is at the heart of intel’s transformation from a pc company to a company that runs the cloud and billions of smart, connected computing devices. the data center is the underpinning for every data-driven service, from artificial intelligence to 5g to high-performance computing, and dcg delivers the products and technologies—spanning software, processors, storage, i/o, and networking solutions—that fuel cloud, communications, enterprise, and government data centers around the world.posting statement. intel prohibits discrimination based on race, color,
