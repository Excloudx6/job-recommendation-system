software engineer
job descriptioncome work for intel's automated driving group as a software engineer, in the automated driving solutions division. this is an opportunity to  work on automotive customer projects and research academic publications/implementations to understand state of the art deep learning methods being applied for adas/ad use cases.responsibilities include the following:work with cross division teams at intel to develop reference sensor fusion and autonomous driving algorithms for target architecture using hw optimized api's like opencl.work directly with key automotive oem/tier1 customers to develop proof-of-concept/benchmark. analyze and optimize selective algorithms developed for intel architecture based systems.  identify performance bottlenecks and recommend product/feature enhancements in hw/sw/libs/tools.provide training to customers and support use of intel sdk and tool chain for developing autonomous driving applications including deep learning networks.provide acceptance testing and recommend feature enhancements for sdk/tools/libraries to partner organizations based on developer experience from customer teams.work with customers to help transition their code and development environment to intel architecture and associated sw development tool chainthis is an entry level position and will be compensated accordingly.qualificationsyou must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your school work/classes/research and/or relevant previous job and/or internship experiences. minimum requirements:- must have a master's degree in electrical/computer engineering or computer science- minimum of 6months experience with:prior algorithm development and/or software development experience in embedded/automotive applications will be considered highly valuable.software development toolchain experience on [intel cpu or automotive asic/fpga ].software development: c/c++, python, shell scripting. preferred qualificationsos: linux ubuntu, yocto and/or embedded os/rtos qnx, ghs, other.experience with one or more computer vision programming tools and apis: matlab, opencv, openvx, openclmachine learning/deep learning concepts and experience working with one or more deep neural net frameworks : caffe, tensorflow, theano etc.coursework and/or research background in digital image processing or digital signal processing will be a plusinside this business groupautomated driving group : zero accidents. mobility for all. intel is collaborating with the world's leaders in automotive design and technology to turn visionary concepts for automated driving into reality.  the automated driving group (adg) is architecting transportation for a better life and a safer world.  we accelerate innovation and adoption of smart, connected, transformative, market leading automated driving solutions by delivering high performance soc’s, modules, software and reference designs.posting statement. intel prohibits discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, national origin, age, disability, veteran status,
