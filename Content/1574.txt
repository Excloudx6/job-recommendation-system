what you’ll do cisco’s collaboration technology group continues transforming how people collaborate and communicate globally. the spark and jabber communication platforms are diverse platforms, members of this team focus on services supporting instant messaging, presence, mobile push notifications and end to end encryption.  as a software engineer you will:    develop readable, maintainable, comprehensively-tested code collaborate within an agile environment delivering world-class software shape all aspects of cloud services including software development, continuous integration, multi-faceted testing, and continuous delivery contribute in code and architecture reviews  support, respond to and resolve service issues perform software performance analysis and system tuning   who you’ll work with join the diverse community of engineers innovating on the latest cloud-based software, including continuous integration and delivery.  our team develops cisco's cloud-based collaboration services enjoyed by millions of users at thousands of companies, including some of the world's largest corporations. who you are a successful candidate for this role possess fluency in coding and systems design, strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills, excellent communication skills, and a commitment to solve complex problems of running cloud services.  minimum qualifications  bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent experience  2 to 4 years experience in platform design and development  solid technical background using complex data structures and algorithms collaborate well in groups and cooperate with people from all disciplines and cultures    preferred skills  professional experience programming with c++ professional experience programming with java practical knowledge programming and debugging cloud, server-side environments experience with python and shell scripts ability to handle periodic on-call duty as well as out-of-band requests  practical knowledge of xml, http/rest, xmpp or sip proficiency with git, preferring github enterprise  why cisco we connect everything: people, processes, data, and things. we innovate everywhere, taking bold risks to shape the technologies that give us smart cities, connected cars, and handheld hospitals. and we do it in style with unique personalities who aren’t afraid to change the way the world works, lives, plays and learns.   we are thought leaders, tech geeks, pop culture aficionados, and we even have a few purple haired rock stars. we celebrate the creativity and diversity that
