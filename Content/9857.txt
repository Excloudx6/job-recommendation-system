description

sm energy is seeking an experienced data scientist to join a multidisciplinary advanced analytics team. on this team you will be charged with helping develop the fabric in which we solve complex scientific and engineering problems related to energy exploration and production.  we will leverage your experience and creativity within statistics, computational mathematics, machine learning and data analytics to help build the data science practice at sm.  as a key part of the newly developed team, the ideal candidate will have experience in developing theoretical models, participating in pocs, and implementing solutions at an enterprise scale. you will provide best practice recommendations, mentor others in the organization and have a desire to help build a top tier data driven analytics culture at sm energy.     key responsibilities: collaboratively design, develop, and implement innovative data and analytical solutions to solve high impact business problems across the organizationbring a combination of mathematical rigor and innovative algorithm design to create recipes that extract relevant insights from data to meaningfully improve business performance.work with key stakeholders and understand their needs to develop new or improve existing solutions.stay current on new developments in statistics, machine learning, big data, iot, related technology and innovative business processes.mentor others in the organization to help develop wide spread adoption and application of machine learning and analytic techniques    qualifications:   master’s degree in cs, ee, mathematics/statistics, with a minimum of five years' experience, phd preferredprofessional experience in data analytics, machine-learning, and/or computational physics.strong skills in statistical analyses with abilities in advanced data management and statistical programming using sas, r, stata, mathematica, scala, python, sql, hive, impala, or other programming and statistical or mathematical packages.experiences with one or more of the following is highly desirable:  hpc/parallelization, operationalizing ml models; cloud computing (e.g. google cloud, aws, etc.); familiarity with ml frameworks such as tensorflow, theano, mxnet, etc. strong fundamental understanding of various modern machine-learning methods strong oral and written communication skillsability to work independently and in a multidisciplinary team
