company description
as market leader in enterprise application software, sap helps companies of all sizes and industries innovate through simplification. from the back office to the boardroom, warehouse to storefront, on premise to cloud, desktop to mobile device – sap empowers people and organizations to work together more efficiently and use business insight more effectively to stay ahead of the competition. sap applications and services enable customers to operate profitably, adapt continuously, and grow sustainably.
the platform services team at sap successfactors is looking for a software architect to provide product and technology vision in the bigdata area to scale our capabilities while driving aggressive growth. this opportunity presents a significant intellectual, technical and operational challenge with an enormous impact. the software architect will drive the evolution of the product vision and work on translating that vision into implementation designed for simplicity and scale.
 
the software architect provides technical leadership at sap successfactors. they help establish technical standards and drive sap successfactors overall technical architecture, engineering practices, and methodologies. they work on our hardest problems, building high quality, architecturally sound systems that are aligned with our business needs. they think globally when building systems, ensuring sap successfactors builds high performing, scalable systems that works well together. software architects are pragmatic visionaries who can translate business needs into workable technology solutions. their expertise is deep and broad. they are hands on, producing both detailed technical work and high-level architectural designs.
 
responsibilities:
 

design and develop frameworks for highly scalable nosql/bigdata solutions
production/operational troubleshooting in bigdata solutions
research and resolve technical problems of the highest complexity
define and evangelize best-practices for cloud platform programming & usage
ability to make educated judgment calls on what pieces of technology should be created and further invested upon within the organization versus leveraging 3rd party or open-source components
exert technical influence over multiple teams, increasing their productivity and effectiveness by sharing deep knowledge and experience
	 


required qualifications:
 

bs/ms in computer science (ms preferred).
strong technical credentials, with at least 10 years of overall software development experience particularly in nosql/bigdata space. 
	
preferred experience in solutions such as hadoop/hdfs, elastic search, cassandra, etc


experience in working large scale deployment of bigdata (in pb size)
expert knowledge in core java concepts and principles and java ee web application technologies.
expert knowledge of ooa and ood
expert knowledge of one more of the market-leading application servers/web containers
solid knowledge of database concept and design
solid experience solving application performance and scalability issues
solid understanding of sql and familiar with data storage and management systems
solid understanding of internet protocols and practice
experience working in an agile development environment, preferably scrum
familiarity with javascript, ajax, and html
