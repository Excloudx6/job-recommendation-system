we have an immediate need for a frontend software developer in our boston office. 
humanyze helps companies make more informed people decisions. born out of the mit media lab, humanyze uncovers the hidden patterns of digital and face-to-face communication to quantify the people factor in the workplace. humanyze is committed to helping teams work better together and has the data and science to prove it.  humanyze’s world-leading people analytics platform delivers advanced insights and is used by top fortune 500 companies to answer specific business questions around diversity and inclusion, collaboration and delivery, space planning, workload, teamwork and engagement, and regulatory and compliance risk.

role description:the frontend developer will play a key role in building out our primary product—a user-facing web dashboard written in ember js— and will be expected to build new features, improve existing functionality, and contribute performant, tested code that will set the foundation for future growth of the frontend platform.
job requirements:

building and supporting our user-facing dashboard, written in ember js
writing quality, testable code
writing acceptance, integration, and unit-level tests for both new and existing functionality
developing and applying patterns for composable/modular architecture
communicating closely with backend, product, user experience design, and qa teams to define and meet expectations throughout the software development lifecycle
continually researching and exploring new frontend technologies, libraries, patterns, and practices to improve the product as well the development process

required skills:

2+ years experience in javascript
2+ years experience with one or more mv* frameworks (any language)
1+ years experience writing tests for an mv* application (any language)
proficiency with git and github


preferred skills:

experience with ember js
familiarity with semantic versioning (semver)
familiarity with git flow
experience working in an agile environment (bonus points for scrum)
understanding of modern best practices for the development of single page applications, such as web workers, virtual dom, es6/esnext
experience working with: qa engineers; product management, to balance business needs and technical specifications; engineers in other departments / areas of the stack, such as api/backend

what we offer:

competitive salary and bonus
stock option plan
flexible work environment
fully paid medical and dental insurance
simple ira matching contributions
3 weeks of vacation plus a holiday week
11 other company holidays
