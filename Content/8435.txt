business analyst

        at promoboxx
      
(view all jobs)

        boston hq
      


promoboxx is the only brand-to-retailer commerce platform that connects and aligns national manufacturing brands with independent, specialty retailers to increase local awareness and sales. we are proud to partner with leading brands, including nissan, ge appliances, new balance and the north face, to transform the way retailers and brands market together at a local level. promoboxx is rapidly expanding, and we are looking for a business analyst to join our product team!
 
we are looking for an experienced business analyst to contribute insights that will guide the future of the promoboxx platform. a key member of the product team, you will partner with product managers, product marketing, ux designers, and engineers. you will have a direct impact on the solution, product roadmap, and will enable the team to make data-driven decisions faster. our ideal candidate has the ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously, and use our internal and external data sources to provide insights to the team that help us grow in the multi-billion dollar digital advertising and co-op marketing space.
 
responsibilities:
 

work with product management to create and develop product backlog based on market data, product strategy, and product roadmap priorities
research and identify key business impact metrics that prove our value and support new business growth
support a data-driven approach to prioritizing the product backlog by creating business cases for new features, products and product lines
work with product managers, product marketers, ux designers and engineers to project manage the development process and go-to-market plans
lead planning and estimation discussions with engineering
organize and conduct regular stand-up meetings for the product team during implementation phases
serve as a resource to answer questions and clarify requirements for engineering during planning and implementation phases

 
qualifications:
 

detail oriented, but with the ability to see “the big picture”
great verbal, written and interpersonal communications skills
organized, with the ability to manage several projects at one time to successful and timely completion
confident contributor and collaborator in a team setting
ability to learn on the fly to understand a customer’s business requirements and goals within context of a specific use case
good common sense to help analyze specific customer requests and internal stakeholder needs


ideal background:
 

bachelor’s degree (b.a/b.s.), or equivalent combination of education and experience
minimum 3 years’ experience working as business analyst, product owner or related position developing a saas or enterprise software solution
solid skills with excel, sql, and experience working with different data sources. 
solid understanding of agile methodologies and iterative software development
experience working with jira and confluence is a plus
experience with b2b, marketing tech or ad tech is a plus
experience in working in a fast-paced environment, or startup, is a plus


what promoboxx can offer you:

competitive salary, health benefits, stock options, and 401k match program
unlimited pto for vacation, as well as sick days when you need them most
employee rewards program and other professional development opportunities  
an open, collaborative work environment with an amazing team and experienced leadership to help you succeed and grow
cold brew coffee tap, beer and cider fridge, and snacks to help fuel your day

 
promoboxx is an equal opportunity (eeo) employer. we hire without regard to age, color, disability, gender (including gender identity), marital status, national origin, race, religion, sex, sexual orientation, veteran status, or any other status protected by applicable law.
