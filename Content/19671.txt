we are looking for a web developer to support our worldwide marketing efforts as we continue our growth phase. the ideal candidate will have experience working with the latest front-end and back-end technologies coupled with excellent eye for design. this is a contractor position and will report to our director of marketing.
 
main responsibilities:

create, update, and optimize websites content templates using wordpress
set up and manage website environments (dev/test/live) and workflow in github/pantheon/wordpress
work with graphic and ui designers to implement brand style across multiple web and digital properties in a mobile-first design
find and implement website digital best-practices in terms of website speed, site search, seo, digital tracking code, etc.
assist with developing and improving marketo templates (email coding, landing page)

 
qualifications:

5 years of experience in wordpress theme development, html and css
fluent in php, javascript, bootstrap, jquery and front-end web development
experienced with web development management using github for collaboration
have delivered self-service wordpress themes previously that enable marketing teams to manage content and page design
ability to conduct qa according to web and design standards and test for usability, performance, and accessibility
experience building out dynamic pages in html/css/javascript from wireframes and mockups
highly collaborative to deliver high quality, effective solutions on-time and on-budget

 
preferred qualifications:

bacherlor’s degree in computer science or related degree
experience with “drag and drop” wordpress design tools
experience with inbound marketing, e-commerce and/or other integrated web marketing tools
