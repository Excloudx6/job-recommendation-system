scientific applications software engineer for nanotech startup

voxa
     -
seattle, wa


founded in 2013, voxa is engaging in development, design, production, sales, and services of revolutionary high-accessibility wireless nano-imaging instruments with potential to disrupt established industries. in addition, the company is providing cutting edge technology solutions to pioneers in the fields of neuroscience and dna sequencing.we are looking for a skilled and enthusiastic software engineer to develop technical software, build great products, delight customers, and collaborate effectively with teammates.we are an equal-opportunity employer and are committed to growing a diverse team - we welcome applications from people of all races, genders, sexual orientations, and other facets of identity.the position:  the software engineer (se) will report directly to the founder and ceo. the se will translate ideas and system requirement specifications into software design and physical code, documentation, and customer support. the se is a key member of an integrated team with multiple disciplines. the se will have opportunity grow into a management level software engineering position.general characteristics:  the successful candidate will have a customer-centric mindset, be inspired to build great products, work well in a team collaboratively and also independently, and be effective under tight schedule constraints. his/her collaborative mindset and social skills are just as important as his/her professional and technical skills.mindsets: enthusiasm: genuine enthusiasm for developing new scientific tools that catalyze new technologies and promote improvement of the human experienceintegrated design: integration across technical boundaries such as mechanical, electrical, software, and user experience.product presentation: able to rapid-prototype new ideas, and translate these into production-level presentation and user experience.communication: provide and accept constructive feedback to and from teammates to maximize team efficiency.documentation: efficiently document all projects, enabling others to continue and build upon work completedtechnical skillsets: technical foundation: firm grounding in software and firmware development, including system requirements, architecting, simulation, implementation, optimization, and testing of systems with a focus on the user and experience.maintenance and refactoring of dynamic production codebasesdevelopment and management of software powering complex electromechanical systems(integration of software across multiple domains using varied languages and toolsets - including, for example: python, objective c and swift (ios), c/c++/c#, javascript and related libraries - with ability to pick up new languages and frameworks quickly; software-hardware interfacing, such as arduino ide, machine vision, and data acquisition systems)related experience in it, cloud services, and big data are a plus, but not a strict requirement, especially with cross-platform experience including linux, windows, mac, and ios. other "plusses" include physics and robotics.additional keywordsnanotechnology, microscopy, memsjob type: full-time
