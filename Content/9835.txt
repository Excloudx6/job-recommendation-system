job descriptionthe pricing analyst is responsible for conducting pricing analysis and implementing data-driven tools to support strategic pricing initiatives across thermo fisher scientific business units.key responsibilities:retrieve, consolidate, analyze and summarize data from very large data sets using sqlprovide analysis and technical support for pricing initiatives that include setting list prices, determine customer specific discounts and identify product conversion opportunities.develop tools in sql and excel to help businesses set strategic prices in the market, implement pricing improvement programs, and track financial impact.identify, validate, and execute opportunities for sales performance improvements.support the development and refinement of tools, policies, and procedures that enhance existing performance.monitor price-volume tradeoffs of deals and provide reporting to managementassists team in determining optimized customer pricing.perform analysis and prepare executive-level reports of findings.perform ad hoc data analysis for business units, customer, products and vendors.perform other duties as assigned.minimum requirements/qualifications:excellent analytical capabilities including sql, excel and relational database skills.experience with in-depth financial modeling as well as demonstrated analytical and statistical problem solving skills, including data handling and relational database programmingminimum 1 year experience working with large data sets from multiple sources. sql experience preferred.candidates must have a bachelor’s degree in business, operations, mathematics, economics or a scientific field.strategic thinker and problem solverdetail oriented, organized and analytical. has the ability to handle multiple projects at the same timestrong commercial orientation with a balance of pricing and strategic business management capabilities. a passion for understanding pricing strategies and execution methodologies in a manufacturing and/or distribution businessability to work cross-functionally and effectively influence and partner. must relate well to all kinds of people, listens and builds constructive relationships, uses diplomacy and tact. shows creativity and innovation in presenting ideasability to drive projects and activities to successful conclusion, operating with a high sense of urgencydemonstrated ability to operate independently with broad general guidance against a framework of defined business objectivesprior experience working in large corporate environment or a diversified, complex organization is preferred. candidates should be comfortable operating within a matrix structure.strong intellect, drive, and communication skillsability to travel about thermo fisher scientific

					thermo fisher scientific inc. (nyse: tmo) is the world leader in serving science, with revenues of more than $20 billion and approximately 65,000 employees globally. our mission is to enable our customers to make the world healthier, cleaner and safer. we help our customers accelerate life sciences research, solve complex analytical challenges, improve patient diagnostics, deliver medicines to market and increase laboratory productivity. through our premier brands - thermo scientific, applied biosystems, invitrogen, fisher scientific and unity lab services - we offer an unmatched combination of innovative technologies, purchasing convenience and comprehensive services.
