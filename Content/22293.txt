        details:



                our medical affairs physicians and scientists are the experts on our medicines/vaccines and an extremely valuable part of our global business. in all countries where our medicines and vaccines are marketed, these professionals help to ensure that healthcare professionals can understand the appropriate use of our products, and also ensure that the patient remains at the heart of our decision making. our global medical organization is at the forefront of the industry-leading changes to improve patient treatment options. we are committed to making gsk’s medical affairs team the best in our industry, and a trusted valued partner in the delivery of healthcare to patients.the director of medical advanced analytics enablement position is a new role within u.s. medical affairs (usma).  you will be responsible for the design, creation and implementation of a comprehensive medical affairs analytics enablement program which fuels efficient advanced analytics delivery and effective data driven decision making. you will have a significant impact on the business and will create a gsk and usma competitive advantage by driving industry leading business intelligence and advanced analytics delivery solutions through efficient data synthesis and maximizing use of analytics tools, frameworks, and technology infrastructure in support of pipeline and north america marketed products.  innovation will stem from data visualization, streamlined storyboards and maximizing the use of voice of customer and unstructured data sources.  the analytics deliverables will inform and shape discussions in the most senior leadership teams and across franchises and functions in r&d and commercial.  key responsibilities:• design and implement an efficient and easily consumable business intelligence solution that provides intuitive and impactful visualizations of advanced analytics outcomes inspiring action and decision in delivering the gsk r&d pipeline, commercial product launches and long-term support of our medicines and vaccines.  • ensure efficient and strategic delivery of advanced analytics methods (response modeling, predictive modeling, multivariate analytics and methods, experimental design, demand/utility models, propensity and interaction models, and other advanced statistical applications) in partnership with the advanced analytics team within usma to support strategic planning for customers and products• in alignment with medical leadership, other gsk analytics functions and external partners, identify and develop new data driven insights that can be understood and implemented to drive effective medical strategies, medical voice of the customer, and usma operations.• identify and mitigate usma and corporate risk by ensuring compliance with policies, sops and guidelines including analytics programs and reporting associated with usma cia requirements.• leverage information from usma’s most critical medical/business systems which are used by usma functions and teams to execute on their accountabilities, delivering meaningful, timely, accurate reports that drive business performance excellence. signal detection programs are a critical component of the analytics delivery to anticipate needs and drive timely action.• develop, maintain and deliver key performance indicators (kpis), dashboards, launch excellence reports and metrics for usma and embed their usage throughout the organization.  • determine best in class analytical platform including master data management, reporting schemes and tools, visualization approaches and tools, and predictive modeling, while embracing innovative and agile delivery methods with a focus on driving rapid business results.  • ensure an “outside-in” lens for external benchmarking to deliver value aligned with best in class capabilities.• based on complex analytics of business problems, develop integrated and innovative insights from diverse, complex data.  ensure analytic reporting capabilities & expertise within us pharma and r&d organizations are optimized and analytic strategies are enterprise aligned.  be a credible partner with other gsk
