tti success insights is looking for a talented, design oriented website developer to join our team. you'll work with a variety of technologies determined by the needs of the project. the fast-paced environment, believes in doing things the right way, producing high quality code, while still producing at a speed the business needs to stay ahead of the competition.

responsibilities

work on different projects ranging from marketing websites, ui/ux for web applications, stand alone prototype product sites.
be prepared to address and improve current systems as well as design from the beginning.
take accountability and ownership for all projects through creation, testing, deployment and support.
collaborate with developers, creative, marketing and management to design and create effective internal and external sites.

requirements

ui/ux design
html, css mastery
bootstrap/responsive design
wordpress proficient
graphic design ability preferred
some java preferred
ability to work alone or in a group
great ability to present solutions to complex problems
constantly seeking new growth and new opportunities to learn
excellent communicator with both technical and non-technical audiences

compensation

salary depending on experience
generous pto, personal and sick time
100% employer paid health insurance for employee
100% employer paid vision, dental, disability and life insurance for employee
50% contribution for dependent health insurance
401k
bonus structure

about tti success insights
