senior data scientist



location: austin, tx		

department: data science		

type: full time		

min. experience: senior manager/supervisor		





about us:
we’re an innovation lab, operating within the home depot, finding competitive edges with predictive analytics.  we get to have a massive impact on a 100 billion dollar company, given that we are impacting product pricing, assortment, margins and share.   we are the tiger team, focused on the ceo’s agenda, and we work on the high impact projects across the company, not in one functional area.   we're a polyglot team combining science with engineering, and we are lean and experimental.  we're engineers, analysts and scientists. we look for high leverage problems and attack them with the freedom to take risks. our team is small, with little hierarchy so everyone has an opportunity to make an impact.
about the role:
the primary role of this position is to provide leadership in developing data science solutions to optimize operations and revenue for the business, and to ensure that the techniques and methods are appropriate, effective and leading-edge. specific responsibilities of the position include:

optimizing product matching, classification, and assortment
developing price/demand models and optimizing business revenue
building pricing solutions and local pricing strategies
working closely with the engineering team and building a competitive advantage with innovative technology/methodology
developing research agendas and implementation plans
conducting research into new or alternative methods and techniques

 
requirements:
the primary requirement for this role is demonstrated expertise in operations research and statistics. in addition, senior scientists will possess the ability to solve analytic and software problems, think creatively and work effectively with teams of professionals engaged in tackling real-world business problems.

ph.d. or masters degree with 5 years industrial experience in operations research, industrial engineering, statistics, machine learning, data mining, or related discipline
at least 3 years performing substantive design and analysis work in demand modeling, revenue management, or pricing optimization
solid understanding in big data technologies and applications (hadoop, mapreduce, hive, hbase, etc.)
solid experience in database management (ms sql, mysql, postgres, etc) and developing analytic solutions using statistical tools (e.g. r, sas, or similar)
strong programming skills with a solid understanding of object oriented languages and principles
solid hands on coding data science solutions in python, java, c++, or similar
sound presentation skills and visualizing complicated data science results in tableau, microstrategy, or similar
strong team player
strong communication and interpersonal skills
curious and interested in learning about the core challenges facing the home depot, then crafting hypotheses to test.  
willing to travel as needed

 
our benefits

competitive base and bonus package
restricted stock awards and employee stock purchase program
very competitive benefit package that covers you and your family on day one.
open and flexible pto
401(k), eligible for matching company contribution after one year
stocked kitchen with frequent breakfast tacos and a catered weekly lunch
work life balance
located in the heart of downtown austin.
start up culture.  big company benefits.
