at ntt data services, we know that with the right people on board, anything is possible. the quality, integrity, and commitment of our employees are key factors in our company’s growth, market presence and our ability to help our clients stay a step ahead of the competition. by hiring the best people and helping them grow both professionally and personally, we ensure a bright future for ntt data services and for the people who work here.
 
ntt data services currently seeks a peoplesoft enterprise learning management (elm) lead to join our team in austin, texas (us-tx), united states (us).
 
 
<span style="color: black; font-family: "verdana", "sans-serif"; font-size: 9pt;"> 
in this role, you will have the opportunity to:  

troubleshoot functional and business process issues related to the elm module.
translate the elm module configuration to respective peoplesoft users, it staff and it management
configure the elm module to support key initiatives including qualification requirements
report for training, requirements gap analysis, training audit process, certification management process and supplemental learning management process
work with other functional analysts and developers to determine the best technical approach and fit-gap of user requirements and elm
provide it support to map existing internal technical, professional development and leadership courses to new job requirements
work with peoplesoft enterprise learning management (elm) it applications and production support team to provide functional and business expertise to address
re-configuration of elm module v9.2 to ensure accurate training, re-certification, and professional certification records are created, maintained and readily accessible to appropriate managers and employees

 
the ideal candidate will have the following qualifications:

3+ years of peoplesoft elm functional experience must include module configuration, developing training materials, requirements gathering, and writing reports.
recent experience with large scale implementation and production support for peoplesoft elm modules with at least three separate companies
last assignment must be directly involved with elm implementation, upgrade, or production support.
capable of configuring modules independently to align business processes. ability to develop test plans, create test scripts and perform all phases of testing from unit to uat for front and back end functionality
experience must include strong capability to write complex sql scripts to resolve data issues
must be familiar with elm table structure
experience must include ability to independently configure integration between hcm and elm modules

minimum education and certifications:
	• undergraduate degree or equivalent combination of education and work experience.
