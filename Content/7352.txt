the john marshall law school


5 reviews

chicago, il


the john marshall law school is accepting applications for a data and reporting analyst within information technology services (its). the analyst provides consultation, analytical support and data reporting services for its and other departments. the role requires analyzing data, overseeing all data reporting and analytic tools, producing reports, and presenting findings to administrators and end users in a clear and concise manner. the analyst will work collaboratively with all academic departments and support various business units to translate data findings into actionable recommendations and improvements. the analyst will also be tasked with assessing the current state of ssrs data reporting at the law school, developing recommendations and plans to improve upon the current state, including the possible use of new data reporting and visualization tools, and working closely with departments across the law school to implement changes approved by the administration.the john marshall law school, finding any invidious discrimination inconsistent with the mission of free academic inquiry, does not discriminate in admission, services, or employment on the basis of race, color, sex, religion, national origin, ancestry, age, disability, veteran status, marital status, sexual orientation, gender identity, gender expression, genetic characteristics, or any other characteristic protected by applicable law.duties and responsibilities: compiles complex data and develops institutional reports for internal and external reporting requirements. internal reporting includes compiling data from the school’s sql based student information system (ellucian colleague). external reporting includes state and federal requirements, accreditation data reports, and publishing companies that are submitted at various intervals throughout the year (i.e., aba, hlc, princeton review, ipeds).produces standardized reports that are distributed to the administration.plans, develops, and conducts special research and data management projects using computer information systems and specialized software packages.develops and enhances self-service data retrieval and reporting services to benefit end users and administrators.coordinates the data retrieval process utilizing available databases.identifies various data resources to enhance and expand institutional reporting capabilities.works closely with end users in functional departments to ensure the integrity of institutional data.develops, maintains, and evaluates databases and information systems, troubleshooting data problems.determines sample/data to be used in analyses, downloads data, oversees data storage and security, and monitors data quality and data integrity.creates and updates documentation on a regular basis.other duties as assigned.minimum job requirements: bachelor’s degree from an accredited institutionat least three years of progressive experience in information technology or related statistical roleknowledge of database design and structureexperience with sql server management studio, and sql programming techniquesworking knowledge of sqlsql query writingmicrosoft excel and all microsoft office productsideal candidate will have: experience in a higher education institution preferred.experience working with higher education erp software products, specifically ellucian banner, ellucian colleague, peoplesoft or related productsexperience with ellucian colleague and sql server is preferredknowledge of identity management products like forefront/microsoft identity manager (fim/mim)project management experiencethree years of experience generating custom reports using sql tools or other bi tools like cognos, business objects, power bi, tableau, ssrs, etc.high level of integrity and confidentialityhigh degree of accuracyability to work with a wide range of individual who have varying degrees of technological sophisticationwillingness to work flexible hours as needed, especially at the start and end of semesters and during heavy accreditation report preparation periodsjob type: full-timerequired
