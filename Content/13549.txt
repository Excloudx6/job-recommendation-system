						job description
					

mckean defense is an employee owned naval life cycle management, engineering, enterprise transformation and program management business headquartered in philadelphia, pa. mckean’s engineers, developers, technical staff, programmers, analysts, and program managers identify and deploy new shipboard technologies, integrate information technology across shipboard platforms, and develop strategies to support the warfighter. mckean’s employees create strategic solutions to help customers reach new levels of mission support and transform their organizations.
mckean defense group is seeking a software engineer to support naval surface warfare center, philadelphia division in philadelphia, pa.
 


						required skills
					

the candidate must have:
- bachelor's degree in computer science, computer engineering or a related technical discipline, or the equivalent combination of education
- experience with ada, c++, microsoft sql server 2005 and above, windows 7, redhat or other linux shell administration and programming
- versa module eurocard (vme) bus, programmable logic controller, vmware administration experience
- strong programming skills and experience with matlab and simulink; specifically: real-time workshop, simpowersystems, stateflow, stateflow coder (a plus)
- candidate must be clearance eligible, active clearance preferred
 
 
 
 


						required experience
					

the candidate must have experience with:
- cmmi, level 3 technical practices
- strong c, c++ and/or c# programming skills
- strong experience with version control software such as sourcesafe, subversion
- experience designing, developing, deploying and maintaining software based simulations
- proven ability to initiate, investigate and solve technical issues with minimized technical oversight
- strong problem solving and debugging skills towards the identification and resolution of software issues
- desire, interest and proven capabilities to operate in a fast-paced team environment with distributed areas of expertise
- strong written and verbal communication skills 
 
equal opportunity employer–minorities/females/veterans/individuals with disabilities/sexual orientation/gender identity.
mckean defense group is an e-verify company.
