sr. data scientist/data integration
req id 86569br job category operations countries (state/region) colorado location united states - colorado - denver
bp.com #tag #lower48req
apply now

role synopsis
data and analytics organization at bp lower 48the data and analytics organization are teams of highly experienced data scientists, data engineers, data management and quality experts, reporting and visualization professionals, and strategy and integration analysts. with core capabilities in data, analytics, solution implementation and strategy execution, we create data, analytical solutions, and actionable insights to drive towards the overall objective of intelligent operations and the path to premier at bp lower 48.description of the position:overviewsenior data scientist/data integration at bp l48 is the highest level technical lead for data management and data quality in the company. job consists of improving current data structures and systems while moving towards incorporating unstructured and real time data sets. responsible for being the technical liaison between it and operations, especially the data science group. the position gives the individual the ability to set the foundation for company transforming from a process driven to information driven enterprise.  
key accountabilities
· plan and implement projects to improve the reliability and performance of data structures of bp lower 48. monitor performance and implement solutions that change or optimize the infrastructure of company databases.· work with the analytics manager to define and implement data retention policies.· work with the analytics manager to create guidelines and systems to properly control end users utilization of databases.· study data sources by interviewing end users and understanding use cases. define, analyze, and validate data objects in the company.· create data structure that optimizes the net benefit of data creation and movement against the overall cost to the business, especially in a cloud environment.· work with both the it and analytics groups to ensure that both the it architecture and the analytics models are designed holistically.  
essential education
· master’s degree in computer science, information science, or an equivalent field. phd preferred.· 5+ years industry experience in database management with a proven track record of improving database performance in an environment where both data magnitude and user requirements are constantly increasing. 
essential experience and job requirements
· ability to create, monitor, and quickly action metrics for data quality and data availability.· expert understanding of sql databases.· ability to work cross-functionally in a highly matrix driven organization, at times under ambiguous circumstances.· experience with data migrations, especially to cloud solutions, is a plus.· experience in both improving existing data structures and implementing greenfield projects. 
other requirements (e.g. travel, location)
n/a
desirable criteria & qualifications
foster an environment of safety first operations demonstrate ability to achieve high performance goals and meet deadlines in fast paced environmentpossess the grit necessary to tackle any challenge and a growth mindset to be on constant lookout for new solutionsdemonstrated use of impact principles: i -    innovated: learns from new ideas and applies solutions to add value.m - motivated: overcomes obstacles with an intense desire to succeed.p -   performance driven: makes value-based decisions involving measured risk to deliver business objectives.a -   accountable: takes responsibility and ownership of business performance. c -   collaborative: shares knowledge and works together for the good of l48.t -           trustworthy: keeps commitments, listens to others and authentically supports change necessary to achieve our path to premier.

about bp
bp's us lower 48 (l48) onshore business operates across a vast us geography, from texas north through the rocky mountains. the business manages a diverse portfolio which includes an extensive unconventional resource base of about 7.5 billion barrels of oil equivalent across 5.5 million gross acres in some of the largest and most well-known basins in the us. headquartered in houston (texas), l48 employs about 1,700 people across six states, operates more than 9,600 producing wells and has 70,000 royalty owners. our vision is to be the most respected and admired oil and gas company in the lower 48 us states.our wyoming operations are anchored on the giant wamsutter tight gas field in the south central part of the state. in the san juan area of colorado and new mexico we produce from tight gas sands and operate the largest coal-bed methane field in the us. our mid-continent operations cover the prolific anadarko, and is home to the famed east texas basin, along with the woodford shale gas play and arkoma basin. we also have non-operating interests in over 10,000 wells across the us with substantial positions in both the eagle ford and fayetteville shale basins.in 2015, the l48 onshore is being established as a separate business within bp’s upstream to manage its onshore oil and gas assets across the us onshore. this effort is being undertaken to improve competitiveness and help l48 remain at the forefront of innovation and development of technologies for unconventional resources.*li-nowrap
