general purpose 
responsible for the design, implementation, maintenance, and monitoring of database technologies and related software. conduct analysis, creates system specifications, develop, test, and implement engineering, scientific and business applications, operating systems, and file/database servers. utilize existing or new technology in the automation of processes. evaluate software packages and provide recommendations to management. provide actionable recommendations for improvements in application performance after analyzing underlying database-related infrastructure components. provide assessment and recommendations for security issues. write and maintain comprehensive documentation, including database dependencies, database inventory, basic database troubleshooting, and daily database operations documents. foster a team environment with a leadership role in cross-training on relevant services.
responsibilities

design, code, test , implement, document, and maintain database utilities


perform complex database analysis and design reviews.


research and fact-finding to develop or modify database structures.


work with vendors to review quality, adaptability and compatibility of their products.  


provide support for vendor-developed database implementation and periodic maintenance releases as required.  


provide technical input to assist in development of project planning.  


maintain rdbms software at appropriate vendor-supported levels.  


team or project leadership role as required 


act as a subject matter expert/lead for level 1-2 analysts


requirements
bachelor’s degree in information systems, computer science, or related technical field; or equivalent work experience. (typically 4 years of additional related, progressive work experience would be needed for candidates applying for this position who do not possess a bachelor’s degree.  a minimum of 2 years additional directly related technical experience is required.)
seven years of related experience in database administration/analysis/design with an emphasis on oracle (11g, 12c). additional experience with microsoft sql server (2012 and later) and db2 luw (rel 9.7 and later) preferred.
experience with installation and use of oracle enterprise management for database management, performance tuning, monitoring, corrective actions and job scheduling.
experience with installation and use of oracle universal directory for access management.
an understanding of database related security issues and experience with implementation of database auditing and other security related enhancements
effective performance tuning of enterprise level applications.
effective oral and written communication skills utilizing your ability to work with other sr. dba’s to agree on future direction and standards and work as a team.
effective analytical and problem-solving skills.
ability to prioritize and handle multiple tasks and projects concurrently.

preferences
ten years of related experience in database administration/analysis/design with an emphasis on oracle (11g, 12c). additional experience with microsoft sql server (2012 and later) and db2 luw (rel 9.7 and later) preferred.
expertise with architecture, installation and use of oracle enterprise management for database management, performance tuning, monitoring, corrective actions and job scheduling.


unix shell scripting, vi.
sql scripting skills.
idera sql monitoriung.
sql server management studio.
rman backup/restore/cloning.
oracle experience with cdp/pdb containers, rac, dataguard, golden gate.
oracle certified associate or oracle certified professional certification a plus.
