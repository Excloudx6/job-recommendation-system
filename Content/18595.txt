senior front end developer

        at green bits
      
(view all jobs)

        portland, or 
      


the role: 
we are looking for engineers who want to help us define an industry that could grow to over $20b in 4 years or less. our product team is pioneering a new way for canna-businesses to run and our goal is to provide the most efficient and profitable way to run a canna-business while simplifying legal compliance.
automation is at the core of our engineering team, and we do not believe that we can effectively deliver product and grow as a team without it.
you will: 
constantly be solving tough and interesting problems -- like how to provide a simplified experience for customers in different states, keeping them compliant with all the local laws, and interacting with each state’s traceability systems.
help us run software that is the backbone of our customer's business. we do not believe downtime is acceptable and will constantly remain focused on how to minimize this possibility.
leverage your love for the challenge of continuously delivering new features while maintaining an extremely high level of reliability to our customers.
our ideal candidate has: 

4+ years of professional software engineering experience
solid experience with modern front end frameworks like (react.js, react native, angularjs, or ember.js)
solid experience with web frameworks in ruby, python, or node.js
strong computer science fundamentals, demonstrated by the ability to learn new technologies quickly and to choose the right technology for the problem being solved
excellent communication skills
a willingness to speak up if you believe we are working on the wrong things
startup experience or equivalent (fast-paced, fluid, and highly accountable)

bonus points: 

bachelor’s degree or greater in cs, stem, or comparable
