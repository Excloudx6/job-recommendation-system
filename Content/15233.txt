please note: you must be currently authorized to work full-time in the united states on a permanent basis. iodine does not sponsor for visa status.
iodine software equips healthcare teams with the most innovative tools to solve some of their toughest problems in clinical documentation. by leveraging groundbreaking technologies like natural language processing, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, our platform drives significant improvements in how hospitals document patient health conditions, in turn positively influencing team performance and patient outcomes.
our client base is growing rapidly, and we need talented, dedicated individuals to join our engineering team. our software developer will be responsible for building and maintaining the web applications and services in the iodine platform. if you are passionate about problem solving, focused on driving results, thrive on improving productivity, and love development, this position may be right for you.
what you’ll do

design and implement highly performing and highly scalable applications
analyze user requirements based on input from technical services and support team, and propose potential design solutions
research, design, and develop software systems
develop level-of-effort estimates for enhancements and new features
work with other team members to develop a complete and integrated solution; and deliver quality software and required documentation on time
understand and comply with development standards to ensure consistency across the larger development team
conduct in-depth technical and performance analyses in support of production issue troubleshooting
monitor and maintain production systems


 what you’ll need

bs in computer science or related area, with a strong record of academic achievement
5+ years of professional experience writing java code; experience with spring, postgresql, and/or vaadin a plus
comfort working in a linux environment
demonstrated expertise in successful large project/build management and execution
demonstrated ability to manage multiple work streams simultaneously and efficiently
team player dna with a desire to solve for the interests of our internal clients and of our business
positive, self-starter attitude with desire to exceed expectations and ability to work extended hours when necessary
attention to detail, highly organized, with an absolute focus on quality of work

what you’ll get
this is a unique opportunity to join a close-knit, rapidly growing team and help us improve a key piece of the organization. you will be learning the latest technologies that are pushing the boundaries of our industry and impacting the way medical teams document patient health conditions. you will be creating custom solutions, solving challenging business problems, and directly influencing client support. you will join a passionate and ambitious team, with a proven record of success building multiple companies. learn more about us at www.iodinesoftware.com.
