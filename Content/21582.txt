about resmed:
resmed (nyse:rmd) changes lives with award-winning medical devices and cutting-edge cloud-based software applications that better diagnose, treat and manage sleep apnea, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (copd) and other chronic diseases. resmed is a global leader in connected care, with more than 1 million patients remotely monitored every day. our 5,000-strong team is committed to creating the world’s best tech-driven medical device company – improving quality of life, reducing the impact of chronic disease, and saving healthcare costs in more than 100 countries.

 digital marketing web developer  the web technology specialist will be joining and contributing to the evolution of the americas digital marketing team at a growing medical device company that provides true value in the lives of patients and the businesses that distribute our products. the digital team is a dynamic group of digital veterans that is partnering with marketing experts and creative talent to improve how we reach and engage with our customers. the candidate we are looking for has a strong background in web development but also experienced and passionate about software and working with marketing technology platforms. the role will be a balance of managing our marketing technology platforms to deliver marketing campaigns and jumping in to do hands on web development from scratch when needed for email creation, microsite development and daily troubleshooting.  primary objectives

support the web technology team with marketing programs and campaigns that have digital marketing requirements including email, website content, microsite development, form creation, newsletters and webinars.
author content in the resmed content management system, adobe experience manager. leveraging existing and new components and templates to publish new pages, assets or content across us, latin america and canada websites.
hands on coding for web applications, microsites, and database integrations using html, css, jquery, php and general front-end web development skills. application of these core web development skills to troubleshoot and maintain the functionality and performance of the website. 
build and execute email campaigns in marketo and silverpop including new email template development, web form development, landing page creation and testing/deliverability.
manage core marketo campaign set-up including distribution list logic, campaign flow and sfdc sync settings and managing the automated assignment of program membership statuses like opened and converted.
daily technical troubleshooting and root cause analysis of technical issues, along with managing testing and bug tracking/resolution for new functionality. use of webmaster tools for debugging and optimization.

 responsibilities and accountabilities 

collaborate on cross-platform digital marketing projects including email marketing, website management, landing page creation, webcast support and marketing automation
support development of landing pages, or microsites in wordpress, marketo and adobe experience manager.
locally maintain multi-language websites for www.resmed.com
support development of lead scoring and nurturing models in marketo and silverpop
develop landing page forms that map via api to database like salesforce.com and marketo or local mysqls.
understand current cookie tracking methods and applying hidden field values in forms for improved data collection
identify opportunities to evolve the use of web and digital marketing technologies and best practices.
accountable for meeting project deadlines and collaborating with creative services and marketing teams on project definition, scoping and delivery.

 experience  

fully proficient and able to build from scratch in a range of web technologies. e.g sql, php, javascript, json, ajax, xml, html, css, adaptive & responsive design
experience using content management systems: desirable to be familiar with adobe experience manager, sitecore, ektron, fatwire, or similar programs
experience with daily management of an enterprise email marketing platform-preferably a marketing automation platform (silverpop, exact target, pardot, eloqua, marketo)
experience working with google analytics – setting up campaign tagging, producing reports and generating insights on information and/or ecommerce websites
experience implementing analytics tracking scripts and familiarity with adobe tag manager or other tag management systems.
ability to work well in a team environment, active contribution to team issues, support of team members and sharing of relevant information.
excellent english language skills and written communication and interpersonal skills, including demonstrated liaison, consultation and negotiation.
experience optimizing websites for seo from a technical perspective for things like site structure, canonicals, metatags. (versus content optimization). 
experience monitoring changes in google algorithms and updates. also experience using google web master tools for monitoring seo performance and spotting any website issues that are impacting site indexing.
experience implementing and supporting websites and/or web applications leveraging a variety of core online technologies such as http/https, ssl, dns, server side logic/sessions, databases.

 education & qualifications 

solid foundation in the principles of user experience design and user interface design.
diploma or degree in computer science, web software development or related disciplines or 5-7 years experience of applied web development and demonstrated expertise in building websites, managing enterprise cms systems or marketing automation platforms’3+ years working with enterprise content management systems such as adobe experience manager or drupal
