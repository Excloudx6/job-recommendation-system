are you great at html, css and expressing an organization’s brand via the web? do you have an appetite for creating websites of beauty and perfection that work in elegant ways? do you enjoy working collaboratively with an energetic team to turn ideas into reality? does the idea of working for a software company that helps leading non-profits make a difference in the world inspire you?
personify is hiring for a web designer/developer, who will “own” front-end design for the small world community product line. the role consists of two parts 1) creating and updating the design of online communities and 2) working actively with ui product designer to keep the saas product on a path of constant improvement. this is a great role for a skilled designer who also wants to guide the big-picture strategy for design processes, standards, and evolution of a highly-configurable saas platform.
job responsibilities
create designs that thoughtfully integrate clients' existing style, audience types, main ctas/features, and goals of the community
decipher existing sites to understand and effectively update the style sheets and javascript currently in play
create “pixel-perfect” designs with high attention to detail, rock-solid code and elegant implementation across multiple browsers and devices
collaborate with our community consultants to understand client requirements, requests and community mission to create top-notch designs
work with our product team to optimize the platform and help define requirements for new features
the ideal candidate has the following characteristics:
great mix of technical and visual design skills
self-starter, independent learner, quick study
extremely fluent in css, html and can understand and manipulate javascript
extensive working knowledge of user interface, layout, typeface, web, accessibility, and responsive design
great eye for design – ability to implement branding guidelines and create communities that mirror organization’s existing style
strong ability to “reverse engineer” legacy code to make updates & improvements
experience working in production environments with 10+ simultaneous projects
ability to manage projects, meet deadlines, and stay on track
ability to efficiently complete punch list work
can reliably estimate amount of time needed for day-to-day projects
communicates well with community consulting team – is a great project partner
willingness to go the “extra mile” to make sure work is completed on time
attention to display detail across platform and browsers. willingness to "own" and "sign-off" on double-checked, high-quality design work
ability to think strategically and creatively about how to increase design efficiency and sophistication
ability to independently problem solve and continue growing both platform and design knowledge
desire to collaborate cross-functionally to improve productized elements of design
experience working with nonprofit industry is a plus, but not required
the ideal candidate is strong in these technical areas:
html5, css3
responsive design
accessible design
javascript, including jquery libraries
experience working with larger css frameworks
saas experience
graphic design, using tools like adobe photoshop
experience with content management systems, such as wordpress, joomla, drupal
