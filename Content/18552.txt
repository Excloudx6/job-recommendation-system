software engineer
job descriptionyou will develop software as the primary job function.  you will design, develop, debug & test complex software in any field e.g., systems software, software development tools, applications, drivers, numerical methods, ui's, web. may work directly with companies developing and optimizing compilers, operating systems, and other software technologies. qualificationsyou must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates. experience listed below would be obtained through relevant previous school, industry jobs and/or research experience.minimum qualifications: should possess a master of science degree or a ph.d. in computer science, mathematics, or electrical engineering, or a background in areas such as software design and software engineering, scientific computation, and numerical analysis.candidate should have 6+ months of work or educational experience with the following:good mathematical background, in particular in the area of numerical analysis and understanding of accuracy in transcendental function designgood understanding of intel architecture processors, their features, and operationfamiliarity with standards for floating-point computations including the ieee standard 754-2008 for floating-point arithmeticknowledge of intel architecture ia assembly languagefamiliarity with software system debug techniques using intel software toolstechnical skills in microsoft windows, linux or other os sw development environments, c and/or c++, and pc architecturefirm grasp of programming tools such as compilers, linkers, and debuggers, static and dynamic code analyzers, understanding of fusa preferred qualifications:experience with software systems such as maple, mathematica, matlab, or similar onesinside this business groupthe intel software and services
