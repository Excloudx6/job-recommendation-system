get to know grab:
grab is more than just the leading ride-hailing and mobile payments platform in southeast asia.
we use data and technology to improve everything from transportation to payments across a region of more than 620 million people. working with governments, drivers, passengers, and the community, we aim to unlock the true potential of the region by solving problems that hinder progress.
grab began as a taxi-hailing app in 2012, but has extended its product platform to include private car services (grabcar & grabshare), motorcycle taxis (grabbike), social carpooling (grabhitch), last mile delivery (grabexpress & grabfood), bus & shuttle services (grabcoach & grabshuttle) as well as a mobile payments services (grabpay). we are focused on pioneering new commuting and payment alternatives for drivers and passengers with an emphasis on speed, safety, and reliability. currently we offer services in singapore, indonesia, philippines, malaysia, thailand, vietnam, and myanmar; however, we also have r&d offices in singapore, seattle, beijing, bangalore, jakarta, & vietnam.
if you share our vision of driving south east asia forward, apply to join our team today.

this data protection notice / privacy policy describes how grabtaxi holdings pte ltd (“grab”) and its respective subsidiaries, associate companies and jointly controlled entities (“grab group”) uses your personal data.

when the personal data requested through this tool is mandatory, you must provide and agree to the processing of this personal data by grab group. however, if you do not provide grab group your personal data, grab group will not be able to process your personal data for the purposes outlined below and your application may be rejected.
the information provided by you in your employment application will be stored in the grab candidate application database. the purposes for which grab group processes your personal data are as follows:

to verify your identity;
to assess and/or verify your suitability and employability;
to contact you in respect of your application;
to perform other related activities (including surveying and research activities to improve grab's recruiting processes);
to consider you for future potential opportunities and may include a link to your public social media profile(s) which you have authorized grab to access by applying with a social media widget/button.

your information will be accessed by grab’s internal recruiters, hiring managers, potential managers in the hiring organization and the associated technical and human resources supporting staff. if you decide not to provide us with your personal data in any of the mandatory fields, we will not be able to continue with your employment application using this tool. if you are applying for a particular opening, the data controller/holder of your application information is your potential employer in the grab group and your information will be protected in accordance with local law applicable to your potential employer. if you are submitting your application for any open current or future positions, the data holder of your information will be grab group until your information is submitted to your potential employer for a vacancy.
please note that grab is a singapore corporation and while being the holder of your information it will be protected in accordance with singapore laws. by registering, you acknowledge and approve that your information may be processed by any member of the grab group for the purpose of evaluating your application for employment in the respective countries and performing any related activities. as such, your personal data may be transferred to, stored, used and processed in a jurisdiction other than your home nation or otherwise in the country, state and city in which you are present, to companies under grab group which are located outside of your home nation and/or where grab group’s servers are located outside of your home nation. you understand and consent to the transfer of your personal data out of your home nation as described herein. the supplied information will also be protected in accordance with grab internal policies regarding employee privacy and data security.
you may at any time withdraw from a particular vacancy for which you may be applying by clicking on the withdraw button in the "my applications" view.
you may view, update or change certain sections of your application. if you wish to obtain additional contact details regarding your data controller/holder, or a copy of your personal information that we keep, or if you become aware that the non-editable information is incorrect and you would like us to correct it or delete it, contact the grab’s pops team.
