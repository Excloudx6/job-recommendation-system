dev ops software engineer 2
apply now
job id r21590 date posted 11/17/2017 location draper,  utah
why work at dell?endless challenges and rewards. opportunities on six continents. a team of colleagues fueled by collaboration. all this, and a company deeply committed to integrity and responsibility.we are looking for a hands-on dev ops engineer 2 that will be setting up and maintain a continuous integration/continuous deployment environment where code will be deployed through the pipeline starting from qa and moving code to production while executing pre-defined automated tests.skills and background we are looking for:-bs in a technical field plus 2+ years applicable experience (development or qa) or ms or equivalent experience-strong coding skills in python or ruby-experience with kubernetes and/or docker-experience working in a continuous integration/continuous deployment environment-experience working with cloud foundry or openstack-linux experience-exceptional communication skills (verbal and written); excellent documentation skills are required-must be detail oriented, analytical, methodical, a systematic thinker, tactful and diplomaticknowledge of, or experience with, any of the following highly desired:-aws or gcp-jenkins pipeline-golang, sonar, git, rabbitmq, redis, elasticsearch and/or kibana a huge plus-metrics tools such as graphite, influxdb, prometheus, grafana-understanding of cloud terminology iaas, paas and saasdell is committed to the principle of equal employment opportunity for all employees and to providing employees with a work environment free of discrimination and harassment. all employment decisions at dell are based on business needs, job requirements and individual qualifications, without regard to race, color, religion or belief, national, social or ethnic origin, sex (including pregnancy), age, physical, mental or sensory disability, hiv status, sexual orientation, gender identity and/or expression, marital, civil union or domestic partnership status, past or present military service, family medical history or genetic information, family or parental status, or any other status protected by the laws or regulations in the locations where we operate. dell will not tolerate discrimination or harassment based on any of these characteristics.  learn more about diversity and inclusion at dell here
