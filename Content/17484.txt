slalom is a purpose-driven consulting firm that helps companies solve business problems and build for the future, with solutions spanning business advisory, customer experience, technology, and analytics. we partner with companies to push the boundaries of what’s possible—together.founded in 2001 and headquartered in seattle, wa, slalom has organically grown to nearly 4,500 employees. we were named one of fortune’s 100 best companies to work for in 2016 and are regularly recognized by our employees as a best place to work. you can find us in 25 cities across the u.s., u.k., and canada.job title:full stack developerwe are slalom it:·        we whiteboard. best way to come up with a good idea is eliminate a few bad ones. ·        we pick each other up. there aren't any breadsticks, but when you're here you're family.·        we play. there's something in the air here… oh, it's a nerf dart. you have to watch out for those!·        we build. when we aren’t creating amazing solutions, you can find us inventing something new on our lego wall.·        we have a purpose. we help our people, clients, and communities be amazing. ·        oh, and we were recently voted fortune 100 best companies to work for! but enough about us…  you may be a candidate for this role if:·        you are excited to get out of bed every day and see what you can tackle·        you are a self-starter with a creative energy and passion for problem solving·        you have a thorough understanding of modern front-end technologies and want to use the force for good      on a typical day you would:·        develop and maintain software applications written in a range of languages (e.g. jquery, css, angularjs, angular 4, c#, .net) on a range of platforms (e.g. .net webapps, salesforce, sharepoint, windows, etc.)·        troubleshoot/resolve technical problems to ensure minimal disruption to the business·        support existing applications and identify/recommend enhancement opportunities, as warranted·        work with project management, the business units and technical resources to achieve the appropriate and desired solution for the business·        evaluate new technology offerings to see how they could improve our portfolio and processes·        design and execute automated test scripts and test scenarios. create and configure full stack build pipelines      it would be great if you have:·        a bachelor's degree in an it-related field or equivalent industry experience·        at least 4 years of full time working experience with modern front-end technologies and build pipelines·        intermediate familiarity with c# and .net web applications·        working experience with the git version control system, and related workflows ·        passing knowledge with relational databases·        a positive attitude when solutioning feature requirements with other developers and it team members·        strong technical and analytical troubleshooting skills·        ability to work independently and take ownership of issues, tasks, and problems·        demonstrated ability to research and resolve ·        prior experience working in a fast-paced environment  bonus point if you have any:·        experience building custom pages and components in salesforce·        involvement in projects doing design and ux ·        familiarity with azure cloud servicesif this sounds like you, let’s talk. c'mon, what are you waiting for? love your future! slalom is an equal opportunity employer and all qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability status, protected veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.
