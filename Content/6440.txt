our goal: 
to design practical computational imaging algorithms that drive new camera features
to bring these algorithms to life by proto-typing them and then implementing them on snapdragon
to improve algorithms in the camera pipeline and finding new way of doing old things that improves performance
take advantage of machine learning techniques and cnn when it is warranted
