																	job description
																
															

about bdp international: 
bdp international is a privately held, family-owned non-asset based global logistics provider with annual sales exceeding $2.1 billion usd. founded in 1966 by richard bolte sr. and based in philadelphia, pennsylvania, bdp has grown to be one of the largest logistics providers in the world. bdp employs more than 4,000 people worldwide, and operates freight logistics centers in 270 cities across the world through a global network of wholly owned offices, joint ventures and affiliates in 135 countries.  
the company serves more than 4,000 customers worldwide. clients include bayer, dow, dupont, heineken usa, honeywell, johnson & johnson, revlon, trek bicycle, wacker, and others. 
 
job summary:

the risk analyst position is responsible for working with the legal team on examining sources of loss and exposure in connection with the organization’s activities by recommending and implementing risk mitigation strategies through the analysis of existing business processes, loss history and industry research in order to deliver efficient and effective solutions that support both business and customer needs. 
 
candidates must have strong analytical, communication and problem solving skills along with the ability to work both independently and collaborate with members of the legal team as well as other departments within the organization when needed.
  essential functions: 

lead, administer and maintain basic departmental functions and programs


collect claims data for submission, address third parties and customers on-going questions and issues through claim’s resolution


liaison with the company’s insurance broker and insurance companies when needed on claims, policy questions, company needs and customer requests


perform business monitoring and identify areas of emerging risk using key risk indicators and dynamic thinking


lead projects requiring the design of new processes, procedures and controls including the development of documentation


collect and validate risk information from the business providing constructive analysis and interpretation as appropriate


conduct research and case study analysis on industry trends and report results to the legal team and other departments 


provide online and in person training on both department and company related topics






														
																	exemption type
																
															

								exempt (salaried)
															



														
																	job requirements
																
															

a college degree is required - preferably in the business or risk management fields.
must be able to work in a fast paced environment. 
good communication and organization skills and a strong attention to detail are required.   
the ability to work on a team and leadership experience is strongly desired. 
candidate should have the ability to learn various computer programs.
 
please note: there is no relocation or immigration assistance associated with this opening.  
compensation based on experience. we offer a very competitive salary, full benefits, matching 401(k), tuition reimbursement, and casual dress environment.
