company description
as market leader in enterprise application software, sap helps companies of all sizes and industries innovate through simplification. from the back office to the boardroom, warehouse to storefront, on premise to cloud, desktop to mobile device – sap empowers people and organizations to work together more efficiently and use business insight more effectively to stay ahead of the competition. sap applications and services enable customers to operate profitably, adapt continuously, and grow sustainably.
the concur data science team is looking for a bright and creative data science engineer to help build new services within the company by leveraging the power of machine learning. the data science team is a python shop, and we’ve been so successful building services that we now need some help managing it all.  this position offers an opportunity to work with all the latest python packages.  also: docker, hadoop, jenkins, artifactory, quay, vault.  all are technologies that we use today.  the team has build out a cutting edge continuous integration (ci) pipeline. we’re an end to end team and engage in all steps from idea, data exploration, model building and deployment.
 
in the first 12 months you will deliver:

deployments of machine learning services written in python and running in docker containers on premise or in the cloud. collaborate with the data scientists on the team to bring models into the real world.
an understanding of the data sources available within concur, and contribute with ideas of how to turn these data sources into services that enhance the end user experience. some of your time will be spent digging into the expense reporting and data streams to understand the data available and what the challenges are.
api design: we’re building new services that bring ml to previously manual tasks. you will spend parts of your time working on interface design, and understanding requirements from other teams less skilled in machine learning.
new skills to the team: every few months you’ll have the opportunity to present an idea/algorithm or tool to the team and we have a small discussion about it and eat some cookies.  we love that each new team member brings additional skills and has past experience we can all learn from.

 
in order to accomplish this, you will need to:

have passion for python! we do almost all work in python, so python skills are required.
have some experience with linux.  this will really help.  our dev machines are macs, but our machines in prod are linux boxes, occasionally there are task such as mounting a drive where linux experience could be helpful.
learn our stack.  you will need to be a good learner, and enjoy diving into an area where you have technology gaps. work with people throughout the company to understand technologies and next steps for your project.
be a bit of a hacker.  we love to get our hands dirty and dig in. hopefully you love to do the same.
continuously update your skills and progress.

 
so who are we?
concur, an sap company, takes companies of all sizes beyond automation to a completely connected spend management solution encompassing travel, expense, invoice, compliance and risk. for more than 20 years, our global expertise and industry-leading innovation has kept customers a step ahead with time-saving tools, leading-edge technology and connected data.
your career at concur is what you make of it. you can forge your own path, work in different offices, switch business units or solve a problem that no one has before. we drive innovation forward until it becomes the new normal, but we’ll never accept business as usual if there’s an opportunity for business to be better. want to be a part of something amazing? check us out at: concur.com/careers
what do you need to bring?

bachelor’s degree, or higher in computer science, math, or just come with the skills and interest needed for the job!
5+ years of professional experience with software development.
strong data discovery and analysis skills
experience with big data using technologies like hadoop, spark, nosql and/or traditional relational databases
experience with statistical software packages & tools (r, matlab, spss, sas, scipy, mahout, torch)
experience with a variety of relevant languages (python, java, scala, pig, sql) preferably on linux

 
exceptional candidates will also have the following qualifications:

industry experience with common machine learning models (decision trees, linear models, naïve bayes, neural nets)
industry experience with data visualization (ggplot2, d3, tableau)
familiarity with agile/scrum
familiarity with software as a service, api development
familiarity with standard software development practices (source control, unit testing, code reviews, automation, performance monitoring)
