description
objectives:
the people at industrial scientific are committed to ending death on the job by the year 2050, and we need more help.  we hire smart, motivated people, give them world-class tools in a supportive environment, and we let them do their job.  our team is highly engaged, creates quality solutions and delivers outstanding customer service.  our leaders understand the critical elements of breakthrough performance: bringing out the best in people; creating a climate of trust, openness and high performance; fostering candor; and treating people fairly and with dignity.
the software developer, internal applications role will design, develop, maintain and support the internal applications software systems under the direction of senior software development staff.
essential duties and responsibilities:
• develops high-quality software on schedule, under budget, and meeting all stakeholder requirements
• participates in requirements-gathering meetings with project stakeholders
• creates and modifies project documentation
• delivers software to sqa and collaborate with sqa to complete testing
• collaborates with other developers and support personnel as needs arise
• participates in project coordination meetings, design reviews, code reviews and other business related meetings
• adheres to the process guidelines specified in the company’s product-development process (spear) and to the sdlc process of the software products department
qualifications required (non-negotiable):
• bachelor’s degree in computer science or related program
• three or more years professional software development experience in a c#.net environment
• sql and relational database skills, including an understanding of er diagrams
• strong object oriented analysis and design skills
• understanding of core internet technologies such as http, xml, webservices, etc.
• ability to work in a team environment, communicate and collaborate effectively
• energy, initiative, a drive to succeed, and a willingness to learn
 qualifications preferred (negotiable):
• three years professional software development experience in a c#.net environment
• understanding of sdlc best practices
• uml familiarity
• experience developing pc-based applications
• oracle, informix or sql server database experience
• experience using microsoft team foundation server
• .net certification
physical demands:
note: the physical demands described herein are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.  reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. 
while performing the duties of this job, the employee is required to sit and talk and hear.  the employee is required to sit and type on a keyboard for extended periods of time. the employee is required frequently to use hands and digits to handle or feel work material.  the employee is regularly required to stand, walk, and reach with hands and arms.  lifting/carry up to 50 lbs occasionally, 20 lbs frequently, and 5 to 10 lbs daily.  frequent assent/decent of stairs/steps, especially at field locations.
