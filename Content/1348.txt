a career with milliporesigma is an ongoing journey of discovery: our 50,000 people are shaping how the world lives, works and plays through next generation advancements in healthcare, life science and performance materials. for 350 years and across the world we have passionately pursued our curiosity to find novel and vibrant ways of enhancing the lives of others. milliporesigma is a business of merck kgaa, darmstadt, germany.
 
 
what we do
we do our work in three fields: healthcare, life science, and performance materials.  across these three areas, we bring specialist and high-quality products to the world.  we work on everything from groundbreaking prescription medicines that treat conditions such as cancer and infertility to over-the-counter products for colds and pain.  we provide scientists and engineers with best-in-class lab materials, technologies, and services, and are dedicated to making research and biotech production simpler, faster, and more successful.  we develop specialty chemicals for demanding applications, innovative liquid crystals for displays, effect pigments for coatings and color cosmetics, and high-tech materials for the production of integrated circuits.
 
who you are
-rising undergraduate junior or older pursuing a bs/ms/phd in stem or economics
-natural leader
-self-motivated worker who is hungry to learn, act and innovate
-bold and audacious individual who has the confidence to believe that you, as an intern, can change the industry
-sharp communicator and talented presenter with the ability to translate technical content and ideas effectively into business settings
 
what you’ll do
-spend the summer living in and exploring st. louis, one of america’s hidden gems
-venture to international headquarters with a weeklong business trip to darmstadt, germany
-take advantage of a large dose of high exposure to key players in the company, including members of the c-suite
-work on projects that move at a high speed and produce tangible results
-take charge of what projects you want to work on and learn what you want to learn
-meet and present regularly to high-level executives, forming relationships and expanding your business network
-enjoy a competitive salary
-benefit from a relocation package and housing stipend for those who qualify
 
 
desired skills
-sql proficiency
-knowledge of at least one programming language (java, python, etc.)
-strength in statistics, data analytics, and machine learning fundamentals (preferred, not required)
-experience in analyzing complex data and using results to implement change
-familiarity with unix/linux
-firm grasp on data analysis using r or pandas/scikit-learn
-ability to use distributed computing, e.g. apache spark
 
project possibilities
-research and implement novel statistical and machine learning techniques to solve strategic problems
-establish scalable, efficient automated processes for large-scale data analyses, model development, model validation, and model implementation
-work closely with software engineering teams to put machine-learning models into production
-find new ways to leverage data and information to produce higher conversions
 
 
what we offer: with us, there are always opportunities to break new ground. we empower you to fulfil your ambitions, and our diverse businesses offer various career moves to seek new horizons. we trust you with responsibility early on and support you to draw your own career map that is responsive to your aspirations and priorities in life. join us and bring your curiosity to life!
curious? apply and find more information at come2emd.com
