director of data sciencesalt lake citya compelling opportunity has arisen to join a game changing, ai first technology business as a director of data science building out a new data science offering, focusing on nlp and computer vision.based in salt lake city, reporting to the ceo, you will be responsible for growing and developing a high performing data science team, charged with changing the way we perceive and react to events as they happen.this is a pivotal role in a growing business with an infectious company culture and a high level of responsibility and visibility. your role as director of data science: you will bring creative and innovative solutions from inception through to production, working on some of the hardest problems in the industrydevelop world class machine learning and deep learning systems that will drive understanding of complex data sets across speech, text, image and video.build, nurture and grow a team of data scientists at varying levels across computer vision and nlp skills and experience: phd in a quantitative discipline such as: statistics, maths, computer science or engineering (master's degree considered)significant experience of leading and building data science teamsunderstanding and knowledge of deep learning toolkits and methodologiesentrepreneurial leader with executive presence.strong track record of leveraging data analytic methods within the technology sector and experience with large data-sets to create data-driven insights the benefits:a competitive base salary, as well as a personal related annual bonus and generous employee benefits. how to apply:please register your interest by sending your résumé via the' apply' link on this page.
