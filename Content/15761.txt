job description : 
this position involves software development for the ammm program (c-130j/c-5). this involves both the development of airborne applications for c-130j/c-5 aircraft as well as ground based communication and maintenance systems. in addition, this position will be involved in supporting and maintaining software tools used to generate data files loaded into c-130j/c-5 aircraft systems. this co-op / internship is for summer 2018.basic qualificationsmust be a us citizen or legally authorized to work in us as a permanent resident. this position is located at a facility that requires special access.pursuing a bachelor’s degree in computer engineering, electrical engineering, or computer science.desired skillsexcellent communication (written and verbal) and analytical skills required. fluency in pc based ms office application.experience with at least one of the following software programming languages desired: c/c++, c#, java, or ada.as a leading technology innovation company, lockheed martin’s team of 113,000 people works with partners around the world to bring proven performance to our customers’ toughest challenges. lockheed martin has employees based in all 50 states and more than 570 facilities that span 70 countries.join us at lockheed martin, where we’re engineering a better tomorrow.lockheed martin is an equal opportunity/affirmative action employer. all qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, protected veteran status, or disability status.job location(s): marietta georgia
