basic qulaifications:
-3+ years business analyst- requirements elicitation & collaboration
-3+ years functional consultant, financial management, accouting & finance-design configuration
-3+ years business analyst & data analysis
 
at ntt data we know that with the right people on board, anything is possible. the quality, integrity, and commitment of our employees have been key factors in our company’s growth and market presence. by hiring the best people and helping them grow both professionally and personally, we ensure a bright future for ntt data services and for the people who work here.
req id: 8688  


 
job description: the person in this position will act as a liaison between business and technical organization by planning, conducting, and directing the analysis of highly complex sor’s and the products and processing therein.  the position requires a balance of strong business/finance skills along with a solid understanding of databases and systems and the ability to communicate effectively across groups. responsibilities include:•support the wholesale profitability platform by researching, analyzing, and responding to user tickets in a timely fashion. this process includes understanding the business issue to be solved, utilizing front end systems as well as databases to conduct research, and communicating findings to end users.•integration, communication with other finance and accounting teams.•writing use cases, user stories and data mapping.•proven ability to elicit, analyze, and formalize business and data requirements with a strong understanding of sdlc methodology•understand the needs and help manage expectations of profitability and cross-sell platform business needs. serve as sme to validate the profitability and cross-sell project data requirements and design will meet the overall business needs•work with end-user groups and system of record sme’s (business, operational and technology) to facilitate working sessions to understand the product portfolio, contract structures, business process and transactional analysis, gl accounting process and data feeds•support technical design discussions with profitability systems partners•function in a highly interdependent atmosphere and collaborate with people in different locations while working independently and proactively basic qualifications: 5+ years of experience in business systems analysis and/or design and in data analysis minimum qualifications excellent written and oral communication skillsmust have (red color skill set)•capital markets functional and data expertise (collateral, derivatives, trading experience)•subject matter expertise on managing and reporting securities and derivatives data – preferably from a regulatory or financial perspective•very proficient in writing use cases, user stories and data mapping.•familiarity and understanding of wholesale banking organization and products•strong analysis and problem-solving skills•demonstrated relationship management skills•experience working with system development life cycle processes•solid understanding of relational database concepts•intermediate sql query abilities using tools such as toad data point, ms sql server, etc•demonstrated ability to work independently person willing to travel is preferrable 
this position is only available to those interested in direct staff employment opportunities with ntt data, inc. or its subsidiaries.  please note, 1099 or corp-2-corp contractors or the equivalent will not be considered. we offer a full comprehensive benefits package that starts from your first day of employment.                                                                                
                      
about ntt data services
 
ntt data services partners with clients to navigate and simplify the modern complexities of business and technology, delivering the insights, solutions and outcomes that matter most. we deliver tangible business results by combining deep industry expertise with applied innovations in digital, cloud and automation across a comprehensive portfolio of consulting, applications, infrastructure and business process services.
