business system analyst – samsung pay data analytics team


mountain view, ca                    

full time                

mobile payments and security                    

mid level                


















this is a great opportunity for a talented and hard-working business/system analyst to join and build data solutions for millions of users of the world’s best-selling mobile devices. the data analytics team focuses on the rapid development of data platform, algorithms, and frameworks for samsung pay, the world’s most widely accepted mobile payment service. this is an exciting opportunity for anyone who are interested in envisioning, prototyping and implementing cutting-edge products with a growing team.
come join the samsung pay data analytics team and help us define and develop the future role of mobile payments!
responsibilities may include, but are not limited to:

elicit, analyze and document requirements for technical solutions
assist the product management team in refining business requirements
design and perform complex analyses to determine resolutions that clarify and address operational and business concerns
translate business requirements into technical/project deliverables
consider multiple scenarios and potential outcomes for recommending actions based on analysis
generate, analyze and review reports for and communicate with key stakeholders
prepare and process data upon request
conduct data-driven analysis for business processes
generate and deliver technical presentations for both technical and non-technical audiences
identify business opportunities and recommend creative solutions to complex problems

minimum qualifications:

ba/bs degree in computer science, engineering or related technical field or equivalent practical experience
excellent professional, organizational, and interpersonal skills
strong communication and presentation skills, comfortable with engaging with technical and business stakeholders at all levels
excellent conceptual, problem solving, and analytical skills
proficient in sql and database systems (e.g., postgresql, mysql, redshift)
experience of analyzing data and creating reports with spreadsheets and powerpoint or other technologies
experience of using data analytics to drive business outcomes
basic programming skills with python/java

preferred qualifications:

ms or phd degree in engineering, mba or related technical field
3 years of experience in sales operations, strategy or marketing
basic knowledge of data visualization tools (e.g., qlik, tableau)
