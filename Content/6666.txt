sr. technical data analyst

date: 
dec 11, 2017
                


location: 
houston, tx, us


company: 
nrg


nrg is the leading integrated power company in the u.s., built on the strength of our diverse competitive electric generation portfolio and leading retail electricity platform. a fortune 500 company, nrg creates value through best-in-class operations, reliable and efficient electric generation, and a retail platform serving residential and commercial businesses. working with electricity customers large and small, we implement sustainable solutions for producing and managing energy, developing smarter energy choices and delivering exceptional service as our retail electricity providers serve almost three million residential and commercial customers throughout the country.. more information is available at www.nrg.com. connect with nrg energy on facebook and follow us on twitter @nrgenergy.
 
summary:  
 
this position is responsible for addressing highly challenging operational and scientific problems using data analytics, machine learning, modeling and statistics for the nrg home solar, nrg retail, commodity and non-commodity areas.   the individual should have robust knowledge in data architecture, data stewardship, data warehousing, statistics, process automation, data visualization, predictive analytics, and machine learning (deep neural networks, supervised, unsupervised) to create software applications/tools that reduce or eliminate operational costs, optimize workflow efficiency, satisfy contractual obligations to customers/partners, and provide actionable insight to stakeholders ranging from agents up to and including executive leadership.
 
essential duties/responsibilities:

drive innovation through data analytics.  brainstorm, theory craft, and identify opportunities utilizing data that streamline workflow, eliminate redundancies, and reduce cost.
drive decisions through data utilization.  create simulations / visualizations and communicate the sophisticated concepts (machine learning / monte carlo simulations) to key stakeholders and explain the story the output tells, in a meaningful and understandable way.
write algorithms and programs that focus on value creation by automating rote tasks / reporting.
act as a liaison between home solar and it.  educate system users on best practices and make recommendations for data architecture additions and modifications.
serve as the resident big data expert for nrg home solar.
serve as the main point of contact for home solar reporting.
support o&m, lease operations, legal, and accounting teams by fielding and answering outlier questions and providing requested data / recommendations.
serve as the main point of contact for home solar data activities.  including external reporting for financial partners.
serve as the main point of contact for our external partners that house our solar generation data.  
maintain, leverage, and troubleshoot our current code base.
responsible for maintaining and monitoring our internal data warehouse.
responsible for quality assurance, ensuring our current tools are operating as expected.
ensure contractual obligations are being satisfied by the existing code base.
drive efforts for data cleansing/scrubbing.
perform ad-hoc tasks as required.
availability for remote support when required.
train personnel on how to use custom systems and tools.

 
position requirements:
 
education: 
bachelor’s degree in quantitative field such as engineering, statistics, mathematics, computer science, or equivalent education and/or work experience.
 
experience:

a minimum of 3-5 years’ experience serving as a system and solutions architect in development, reporting, and data analytics. 
spotfire knowledge for custom report creation
salesforce knowledge
strong knowledge in cloud computing (amazon web services)
proficient in use of version control software (github, gitlab)
proficient in writing and leveraging api’s used for data aggregation from 3rd party data sources
substantial r or python programming experience is expected, previous experience with sas can also be considered
proficient in writing sql scripts
knowledge of relational databases
ability to write batch files, schedule tasks / cron jobs
 


additional knowledge, skills and abilities:

position requires a self-starter who will operate autonomously with little direction.
ability to develop recommendations/action plans and manage multiple projects simultaneously.
clearly communicates complex technical concepts both in a written and oral manner.
excellent problem solving skills and ability to use good judgment to make recommendations and decisions.
ability to import, export, and manipulate data files between various it applications and databases. (e.g., oracle, sap, sql server, salesforce, 3rd party platforms
intelligence, flexibility, patience, and tenacity are quintessential to success in this role. 
 


physical requirements:

occasionally requires lifting as appropriate to perform duties and responsibilities.

 
working conditions

open office environment.
some overtime required as special projects arise
