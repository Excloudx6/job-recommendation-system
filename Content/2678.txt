job description
godaddy’s productivity suite of applications and services help millions of small businesses run and grow their ventures. we are looking for strong c# software engineers who are passionate about scalability and growth.godaddy’s productivity suite of products help millions of small businesses run and grow their ventures. we are looking for software engineers who are passionate about scalable development practices to help accelerate our growth. if you’re into backend c# and love to build efficient and seamlessly integrated products, this is the team for you! you will have an active role in creating and supporting the application responsible for administering all office 365 accounts offered by godaddy, including account creation, data migration, setup, and help. this product group is an important contributor to the company’s revenue, customer growth and technical innovation.responsibilities:•write and maintain c# code•participate in all aspects of the software life-cycle•apply agile approaches to software development•be accountable for code quality and writing unit tests•be expected to contribute to architectural discussions•perform code review and participate in suggesting and supporting coding best practicesrequirements:•3+ years of professional full-time software engineering experience coding in c#•familiarity with agile methodologies, scrum preferred•strong communication skills•familiarity with relational databases (e.g. mysql, sql server)•experience with designing and implementing restful apis•experience with microsoft exchange server or office 365 is preferred•experience with git•experience working in a highly dynamic, fast-paced environmentgodaddy is a proud equal opportunity employer.#li-cd1about godaddygodaddy powers the world's largest cloud
