company: bscc corporatelocation: us-tx-houstonzip code: 77077minimum level of education: bachelor's degreeminimum years of experience: 1position type: exempttravel percentage: 0 more information about this job: overview:at sysco, we offer our associates the opportunity to grow personally and professionally, to contribute to the success of a dynamic organization, and to serve others in a manner that exceeds their expectations. we are looking for talented, hard-working individuals to join our team. come grow with us and let us show you why good things really do come from sysco. the sourcing analyst will be responsible for analysis and reporting required to support sysco’s sourcing activities. this role will require exceptional analytic skills along with a working knowledge of database design and management in microsoft access and/or sql server. this position will work directly with the sourcing team leads, category operations, supply chain and business centers. the sourcing analyst will be responsible for gathering and validating all necessary data. responsibilities:·         conduct analytics to build the fact base in support of negotiations·         perform sourcing data analysis using data template defined in category management process·         lead analysis for visibility in key cost drivers, pricing trends, emerging market regions, supplier capabilities and capacities etc.·         develop ideas leading to continual improvement to the sourcing process at sysco·         provide data support to supplier relations and category management team·         develop any adhoc sourcing related reports needed by sourcing managers, category managers, ssr managers etc.·         provide the analytical support to ssr manager during supplier business reviews·         ▪ work with the insights team to develop surveys and interview guidelines for the key suppliers  qualifications:required minimum education:bachelor’s degree required. required minimum experience:“analytics expert with solid sourcing experience” intrinsic capabilities·         ability to pull disparate pieces of data together to form a coherent story·         ability to manage multiple projects in a fast paced environment, adapt to shifting priorities, and be very detail oriented·         self-driven individual with intellectual curiosity and pushes to understand underlying issues·         collaborative team player who can work across functions (e.g., analytics, category management, ssr) experience·         minimum 1-3 years of business analytics experience, preferably in a sourcing capacity.·         highly skilled in data structures and database access/reporting·         excellent knowledge of microsoft excel, access and sql server·         theoretical knowledge of statistics and quantitative analysis techniques (e.g. spreadsheet modeling, regression analysis) physical demands·         the physical demands described here are representative of those that must
