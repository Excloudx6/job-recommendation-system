 senior data engineer - minecraft









minecraft is one of the most popular video games of all time, with more than 122 million copies sold. minecraft inspires players to create together and has created one of the most active and passionate player communities in the world.as a senior data engineer, you will be responsible for leading the engineering function for the data solutions both the minecraft data science team and the studio depend on. working closely with the embedded data science function you will be play a leading role defining both our data development roadmap and aligning with our data architecture strategy. this is a central role and essential to ensuring we have sufficient technologies in place to enable data informed decisions.an appropriate candidate will have deep technical knowledge of bi systems design, big data architecture and technology landscape, service architecture, etl/elt orchestration, business intelligence tools such as power bi as well as large scale event telemetry ingestion and aggregation systems. the candidate should have excellent organizational and communication skills and feel comfortable in a fast-paced environment.so, if you are you an expert in designing and building business intelligence solutions, big data and are fascinated by how analytics driven organizations can transform data into actionable insights, we have a dream job for you!key responsibilities:• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp design, implement data solutions• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp define and execute the data engineering roadmap• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp provide technical leadership to the minecraft data science team• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp lead the data engineering functionoperational responsibilities:• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp contribute to the continual improvement of our data architecture• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp conduct code reviews ensuring development follows best practice• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp establish and maintain regular updates detailing, current issues, cost, and development progress• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp direct research on potential technology solutions and implementations in support of new initiatives and opportunities.• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp effectively manage vendor budgetsminimum qualifications:• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp bachelor’s degree or 5 years software engineering experiencepreferred qualifications:• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp we are looking for a skilled and experienced data engineer with an extensive background in developing large scale data solutions• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp proven background and strong hands on experience with one or more of the following big data technology stacks: hadoop in the cloud, mpp sql (hive preferred), etl orchestration systems• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp the engineer will be highly experienced with relational databases (sql server or similar)• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp the engineer will be highly experienced with mpp sql such as hive (or similar sql on hadoop implementations) • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp in-cloud development and linux experience a plus• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp strong software engineering background and understanding of the software development lifecycle• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a fast-paced environment• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp ability to express complex technical concepts effectively, both verbally and in writing• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp highly self‐motivated, self‐directed, and attentive to detail• &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp strategic thinker, combined with strong ability to execute • &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp solid verbal and written communication•
