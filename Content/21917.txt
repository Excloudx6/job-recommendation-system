company:

web application and services developer
 
bentley systems has exciting new opportunities for talented individuals enthused about developing cloud-based solutions for the world’s engineers and infrastructure professionals. you will join our team of 3,000 colleagues worldwide who have been providing the leading software solutions for the architecture, engineering, and construction industries since 1984.  
 
at bentley, we are passionate about helping our users for a very good reason. our users are responsible for designing, building, and operating the world’s infrastructure to improve and sustain the quality of life for all of us. with over a million users in over 150 countries worldwide, bentley is making a difference. our products are used in infrastructure projects such as highways, bridges, fiber optic networks, power generation and distribution, commercial buildings, airports, manufacturing facilities, production plants, oil production facilities, railways, tunnels, potable water and waste water systems.
 
job responsibilities:
•        working with a team in an agile software development process
•        understanding and defining software requirements
•        designing and developing new features, products, or web solutions
•        upgrading and maintaining existing software and web services
•        assisting the testing team to create automatic and manual tests of your software
 
requirements:
•        minimum 2 years of professional software development experience
•        university degree in computer science or software engineering
•        good verbal and written communication skills in english language
•        ability to learn and adapt quickly to maximize productivity
•        self-motivated person with the ability to work individually and as part of a team
•        knowledge of agile scrum development
 
desirable skills:

experience in the implementation and consumption of cloud services and rest interfaces
angular2, angularjs, react or similar framework
javascript/typescript
net / c#
html5, css
working in microsoft azure or consuming azure services
knowledge of test driven development and devops best practices
proficiency in full stack (front-end, back-end) web development in multiple languages

 



nearest major market: pittsburgh
                    
                

job segment: 
engineer, wastewater, water treatment, test engineer, software engineer, engineering
