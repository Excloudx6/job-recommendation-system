software is eating the world — and as atlassian’s largest global provider of award-winning commercial apps, appfire is uniquely prepared for the feast! our trusted product brands like “bob swift” and “wittified” are loved by over ten thousand business teams worldwide, amassing over 1 million downloads. come, be a part of our appfire family for this amazing journey!
appfire is looking for an exceptional graphic designer/web developer to join our fast growing team. you are a creative machine, with a passion for outstanding graphic design and pixel-perfect web development, extremely high standards for your own work, and a strong desire for continuous improvement. your design skills and attention to detail are only matched by your love of a well crafted style guide and your desire to work within a fast-paced, “startup like” environment.
what you’ll be responsible for


creating and maintaining style guides for our multi-brand product portfolio with guidance from our director of marketing and executive leadership.
using design elements such as color, typography, illustration, and photography to create striking and compelling layouts.
designing jaw-dropping graphics for email marketing campaigns, digital advertising campaigns, videos, product documentation pages, marketing landing pages, marketplace product listings, and more — including product screenshots, vector graphics, and animated illustrations.
designing and producing amazing responsive websites and landing pages in our marketing platforms (webflow.io and unbounce) and customizing these assets with html5, css3, javascript, and other languages and libraries, as needed.
working closely with our email marketing specialist to ensure that outgoing campaigns and other assets meet our brand guidelines and represent our brands at the highest standard.
collaborating with product marketing managers to prioritize projects and manage workflow to ensure projects meet production deadlines.
proofreading and editing comprehensive layouts to produce accurate and high-quality work.
working directly with the marketing team and external vendors as needed to ensure successful delivery of press ready files for print and optimized files for mobile and web.
staying current on the latest tools, trends, styles, methodologies, thought leaders, libraries and more to ensure your web development work makes use of the most modern coding technologies and best practices.
maintaining a thorough working knowledge of print production, color requirements, preflight packaging, and common print pitfalls to avoid.
optimizing all graphics and website code for desktop and mobile devices.
making recommendations for stock imagery purchases as needed.
creating and maintaining a digital archive of all creative assets — organized and accessible in a collaborative design platform.
participating in regular team meetings and company meetings.
other responsibilities as assigned by the director of marketing.

about your skills and experience


self-motivated and enthusiastic.
incredibly high standards for your own creative work.
expert level proficiency in adobe photoshop, illustrator, indesign, and acrobat; experience with premier pro a plus.
appreciation for core aesthetic principles to ensure deployment of messaging campaigns align with our core design themes and high standards for visual output.
experience working as a designer, developer or animator in a professional design/advertising agency, major tech startup, or fortune 500 brand.
experience coding responsive websites with html5, css3, javascript, jquery, and other modern languages and libraries.
experience creating engaging illustrated animations for web and video.
experience with webflow.io, unbounce and optimizely platforms.
impeccable spelling and grammar, and thorough proofreading skills with a consistent eye for detail.
efficient designer with the ability to manage several tight deadlines at once.
ability to effectively prioritize, self-manage, and work independently.
ability to efficiently shift between different brands, products and project types frequently.
ability to confidently present your designs and articulate your vision concisely.
experience with atlassian tooling, such as trello, jira, and confluence, a plus.
experience coding email templates and troubleshooting inbox rendering issues a plus.
enduring passion for continuous improvement is highly valued on this team.
can maximize his/her output and excel in a work-from-home setting.
ability to travel to tradeshows and team meetings.
bachelor degree in graphic design or related field required.
minimum three years of professional design and web development experience required.
candidates based in metro atlanta are preferred. exceptional remote candidates will be considered.
all applications must include a link to your current personal design portfolio in the “website, blog or portfolio” form field. submissions without an outstanding graphic and web development portfolio will not be considered.

perks
as part of the appfire team, in addition to a competitive full time salary, you’ll also have the flexibility to work from your home office and enjoy benefits such as full company-paid health insurance.
more about us
for more than 12 years, appfire has driven atlassian products and services into the enterprise, enabling countless team to quickly innovate, develop, and bring their products to market. with over 1 million downloads, and more than 60 of the most popular app on the atlassian marketplace, appfire's award-winning "bob swift" and "wittified" product brands have helped over 10,000 customers across 90 countries to creatively enhance their atlassian tools.
