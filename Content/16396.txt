        provo
      


qualtrics is a single system of record for all experience data, also called x-data™, allowing organizations to manage the four core experiences of business—customer, product, employee and brand experiences—on one platform. over 8,500 enterprises worldwide, including more than 75 percent of the fortune 100 and 99 of the top 100 u.s. business schools, rely on qualtrics. to learn more, and for a free account, please visit www.qualtrics.com.
our team is a collection of extremely passionate engineers who are riding the cutting edge of cloud computing and web scalability. we organize and sort terabytes of data and develop solutions used by millions of users every day. we design tools that make sophisticated research simple. our environment is primarily java, scala, c++, go, javascript, angular.js, d3/c3, node.js, mysql nosql (mongodb), aws, python, php, and docker. in our collaborative culture, you find plenty of opportunities to impact our full-stack product; from web & mobile ui to data visualization & analysis to microservices apis to data storage & processing.
responsibilities 

design and execute test plans and cases for our full stack web application
convert manual tests cases into automated cases
design and develop test frameworks, test tools, and automation for complex, distributed saas systems for stes, sdet, and sde to use.
ensure code is tested at the appropriate level (unit vs functional, or api vs ui), reducing unnecessary test cases, intermittent failures, and increased execution time.
define, implement, and analyze metrics to determine test effectiveness and the overall quality of software.
research, share, and recommend new technologies, best practices, and trends
help your team evolve by actively participating in the code review process, design discussions, planning, and post-mortems.
develop and mentoring other team members.

 
requirements 

bachelor's degree in computer science or related field
4+ years professional coding experience in a programming language like java, c#, or c++, knowledge of javascript and python are a bonus
1+ years professional experience creating test automation frameworks and tools from scratch
proficiency with a broad range of data structures, algorithms, and design patterns; knowing when it is appropriate to use them (and when it is not).
design and execute test plans and cases for our full stack web application
convert manual tests cases into automated cases
experience applying different software testing techniques, such as equivalence class partitioning, boundary value testing, pairwise testing, etc.
experience with service-oriented architecture and distributed systems. experience testing webservices is a plus. experience with performance / scalability / reliability testing is also preferred.
