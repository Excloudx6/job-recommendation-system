market intelligence analyst
job descriptioncpmi market sizing arm is seeking market intelligence analyst. you will work closely with the dcg segments telco, cloud, enterprise, technical computing, planning, finance, as well as smg, ssg and peg to 1 collect, explore, and extract insights from internal experts & external data sets, 2 find patterns and relationships in data, 3 perform scenario experimentation that help answer strategic questions from key stakeholders, and 4 help define the engagement model for sharing the insights with customers and strategic partners. key responsibilities include: sizing key dcg markets and new strategic imperativesanalyzing key competitor & customersdelivering ad hoc projects and analysis to support day-to-day business decision-makingproviding insights and recommendations to inform strategic ranging decisions reporting on key market trends and changing market conditions to highlight opportunities and threats identifying and developing relationships with key internal stakeholders
