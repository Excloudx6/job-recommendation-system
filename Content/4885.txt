job description : 
as a software engineer, lockheed martin invites you to create “new realities,” enabling solutions that have never been seen before. you’ll work with fast-paced teams on diverse challenges, from creating out-of-the-box web apps to designing software for legendary advanced projects. whatever the project, you can rely on the backing of a global technology leader with a culture that values innovative thinking and the resources that make it happen. stand on the shoulders of a rich heritage of achievement as you write our next chapter.so take on what’s never been done before. become a lockheed martin software engineer. you are meant to solve the hardest challenges.• this position is for a software engineer to participate in the development and integration of software in a fast-paced, multi-disciplined research and development environment under the mentorship of experts in the dsp field.• the responsibilities for this position include (1) algorithm development involving detection, signal formation and geolocation, (2) mission performance and data analysis, (3) technical writing and presentation to customers, (4) scientific software development.lockheed martin space systems offers a total compensation package from competitive wages, on-site medical care, 25 three days weekends, and healthy living incentives just to name a few reasons to join lockheed martin space systems.basic qualifications(1) strong math and/or physics background with experience in the intelligence community, (2) experience in digital signal processing (dsp) and/or signal formation and geolocation processing, (3) full life-cycle experience with c++ in unix development environments, (4) proficient in python or other prototyping language (e.g., matlab), (5) self-motivated individual seeking a learning environment and challenging assignments developing state of the art signal processing algorithms and applications.desired skills(1) strong statistics background, (2) understanding of both the conventional and modern signals intelligence (sigint) environment and experience analyzing signal processing results to determine quality of algorithms and software developed, (3) experience leading projects - providing budget and schedule oversight, (4) experience presenting to customers at formal program reviews, (5) familiarity with machine learning, (6) familiarity with git, jira and bitbucket.as a leading technology innovation company, lockheed martin’s vast team works with partners around the world to bring proven performance to our customers’ toughest challenges. lockheed martin has employees based in many states throughout the u.s., and internationally, with business locations in many nations and territories.join us at lockheed martin, where we’re engineering a better tomorrow.lockheed martin is an equal opportunity/affirmative action employer. all qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, pregnancy, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, protected veteran status, or disability status.job location(s): littleton colorado
