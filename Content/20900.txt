zaact is now looking for a highly motivated and driven javascript full stack developer to join our growing team of consultants along the wasatch front. 


candidates must have solid understanding and proven production experience in developing for web based technologies. this includes a strong technology focus on responsive development utilizing javascript, especially react.js, node.js, typescript.   


while some exposure to sharepoint and the microsoft o365 stack is desired, zaact is willing to train the right individual.  he or she must demonstrate a passion for learning microsoft-related technologies and will work alongside experts to expand their current skillset. candidates will design and develop using the latest technologies within sharepoint 2013, office 365, and azure. 


you will play a key role in helping zaact to continue to grow by staying abreast of current and breaking technologies and market trends. as contributors of the organization, zaact consultants are often working with client’s and team members, both remotely and onsite, to complete assigned projects on time. candidates must be self-motivated and self-disciplined enough to work in an environment with minimal management.   



zaact is the only sharepoint gold certified / managed microsoft partner in salt lake city and we offer full benefits and paid training along with an unlimited pto policy. 


requirements: • 5+ years responsive web development experience with javascript.  • excellent communication skills; both verbally and written. • demonstrated high degree of initiative and self-guided work. • must be willing to work diligently to meet deliverable deadlines. • demonstrate strong desire to develop new technical knowledge and professional skills on a continual basis. • bachelor’s degree in information technology, computer science, or equivalent.
