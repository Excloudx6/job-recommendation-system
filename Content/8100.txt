description


position overview:the climate corporation seeks motivated masters-level student to manage and execute customer analytics projects to support the company’s customer engagement team in marketing. a successful candidate should be an effective customer analyst and marketer with a passion for delivering data-driven, excellent work.this position will report to the senior global customer engagement manager and will have the opportunity for collaboration across many different departments and functions; including data analytics, marketing insights, product management and product marketing management. if you are passionate about customer analytics and how insights can be used to develop data-driven marketing strategies and plans, and working for an innovative organization that is making a difference in the world, the climate corporation would love to talk to you!what you will do:understand business landscape and translate customer data into insights to inform actionable marketing plans and solutionsanalyze marketing automation campaign performance tracking user behavior and provide recommendations on how to improve future campaigns through better:segmentationmessagingtactics analyze promotions and engagement behaviors to understand what drives upgrades and renewals:collaborating with data analytics and sales operations teams to understand which promotions performed the best with which customer segments understand what engagement behaviors drive upgrade and renewal partner with data analytics team to build dashboards to showcase campaign results partner with global brand and acquisition team to analyze advertising effectiveness and make recommendations for more impactful spendbasic qualifications:candidates must be currently enrolled in a master’s program in marketing or customer analytics, or business (mba)excellent analytical, organizational and communication skills with the ability to be solutions-oriented and confidently bring new ideas to the organizationability to seek and join data, proficient with sql. familiarity with statistical software, such as spss, stat or r.preferred qualifications:previous marketing and/or analytics internship or work experience a plusinterest in marketing and customer analytics and making insights actionable exposure to tech, data-science driven or agricultural companies a plus time management experience and ability to multi-task and manage multiple projectsstrong attention to detail with the ability to work independently as well as part of a teamwhat we offer:our environment is extremely engaging and fast-paced, with a diverse set of top engineers, agronomists, scientists, and statisticians working together to provide the best possible products and experiences for our customers.we offer competitive pay and perks.we provide meals and a large assortment of snacks and drinks to get you through the day.you’ll get 1:1 mentoring from a talented and motivated employee on your team.you’ll have the opportunity to work on real projects with experienced employees and have a direct impact on the company. (our internships are not summer camp!)you’ll have the amazing opportunity to participate in one of our biotechnology tours where you get out of the office and into the field (literally) with agents and farmers.you’ll work in an award winning office with smart designs, large common areas and open floor plans that fuel collaboration.you’ll attend various company sponsored events, activities and off-sites - we make sure interns are having fun!we regularly host meet-up groups and tech-talks and encourage participation in relevant workshops and conferencesyou’ll have the opportunity to interaction with key executives and leadership.​#li-dnp
