overview
delphic, a hero digital company is looking for a forward-thinking full stack engineer with deep technical expertise to lead our php & front end practices. if you can’t stop chasing down the technology of tomorrow and are eager to push the boundaries of what is possible, keep reading.
in this role you’ll join a team of passionate engineers, working along side front- and back-end developers to integrate code into a beautiful web experiences for our clients.  you’ll collaborate with our internal teams to design, develop, test, and troubleshoot advanced data-driven web applications using drupal, wordpress, magento, and other leading cms’s.  you'll share your passion and expertise with our growing team, helping our junior developers elevate their skills and integrating the latest technologies for continued advancement.
who you are

you’re an experienced website developer with 5+ years of development experience, with at least 2 years in an agency environment.
a natural team player who loves to collaborate with digital marketers, designers, and information architects.
the go-to expert resource for all things javascript and php.
a wiz with modern frameworks like jquery, angular, react, node, etc.
lover of logic and problem solving.

what you’ll do

build modern, interactive websites using html5, css3, javascript and ui frameworks, including integration with existing client systems.
drive the efforts of our php team on complex website development initiatives.
fully test and troubleshoot work, ensuring consistently error free, high quality results.
apply industry best practices, such as, specification design/review, object oriented development, in-line comments and other typical documentation specifications.
stay current on the latest technologies and inspire your team to do the same.
mentor junior team members by helping them develop skills and grow their careers.

our culture
we're anything but typical. call us quirky, if you will, but we believe in the power of the individual. it's the diverse and unique talents each person has that makes us the company we are.
to be a fit at delphic, you should be aligned to our values:         

be intellectually curious for all things digital
deliver value by being accountable and impactful
leave your ego at the door and elbows off the table
make the complex feel simple
bring positive energy every day
