listed on 10/10/2017 
kellymitchell matches the best it and business talent with premier organizations nationwide. our clients, ranging from fortune 500 corporations to rapidly growing high-tech companies, are exceptionally served by our 1500+ it and business consultants. our industry is growing rapidly, and now is a great time to launch your career with the kellymitchell team.
job summary:  solr developer
duties:

developing scalable, high-performance applications. 
build smart search applications to make information retrieval a very intuitive and seamless experience

you must have: 

4-5 years of strong hands on skills in solr querying and indexing supporting web applications 
expertise in solr cloud technologies – collection sharding/replication, failover, fault tolerance, high availability along with java. 
expertise in configuring solr schema and understanding of advanced schema fields 
expertise in tuning the relevancy of search results 
expertise in fine tuning solr caches and cache warming and segment merging 
exposure to the internal implementations of solr/lucene and understanding of information retrieval techniques 
strong knowledge and understanding of data structures, object oriented design and systems / applications programming 
strong knowledge of data cleansing and enrichment experience 3-5 years of t-sql experience , including writing and troubleshooting stored procedures 

nice to have: 

experience with machine learning techniques (especially recommender and nlp systems) 
experience with ui technologies such as reactjs, polymer or javascript in general is desired 
good communication, presentation and collaboration skills 
proven analytical, problem solving and time management and organizational skills 
familiarity with the elk (elastic, logstash, kibana and beats) stack is a plus 
interest in cyber security is a plus
