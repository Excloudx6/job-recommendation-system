job title: horizontal directional drilling engineer (engineer iii)
 
the engineering services organization develops best-in-class capabilities in managing some of the most exciting engineering and construction projects in the united states – setting the standard for our industry.our organization is seeking an energetic engineer to join our talented workforce and focus on challenging trenchless pipeline installations, including horizontal directional drilling (hdd).   this position will be a part of the engineering services team based in pittsburgh, pa.  the primary responsibility of this role is to lead trenchless pipeline planning, feasibility, design and construction efforts and also provide engineering support for project teams on capital pipeline projects.    
 
responsibilities:

lead engineering design, scope refinement, cost estimating, development of project design specifications, and support construction for trenchless pipeline installations.
oversee project activities of third party project managers, consulting engineers, construction inspectors, and contractors.
lead and oversee geotechnical investigations and provide engineering review of geotechnical findings
lead trenchless feasibility evaluations and risk discussions with project stakeholders
translate project plans into coherent, credible, realistic project schedules and budgets
provide technical support to all functional departments, as part of early design, regulatory compliance, front end planning, and project execution, throughout the project life cycle.
interpret trenchless design reports and utilize problem-solving to identify solutions to complex problems
lead efforts and project to improve operability, maintainability, and reliability of existing assets.
participate in front end engineering and planning, execution, commissioning, and start-up of new and modified facilities.   
utilize computer modeling and data analysis tools to aid in the evaluation, justification, and development of solutions to problems and/or opportunities.
utilize systems & processes, and support the subject matter expert to aid in decision making and potential project development.
prepare and provide schedule and budget progress, risk reports, trending charts, and schedule analysis.
	 


functional/technical competencies:

have computer experience with microsoft office, microsoft projects, powerpoint, and excel work products.
possess the ability to manage multiple complex projects with a high level of customer satisfaction.
possess the ability to mentor others through technical training and guidance on planning, scheduling, and engineering subjects.
have the motivation and ability to improve existing processes.
understand complex systems and be able to identify potential areas of improvement.  
	 


leadership competencies:

integrity, values, and trust – instills confidence of one’s intentions and positively represents the organization by being responsible and trustworthy.
drives for results – is motivated and determined to achieve objectives while working in compliance.
customer/stakeholder focus – makes customer and stakeholder needs a primary focus of one’s actions; develops and sustains effective relationships with customers and stakeholders.   
communication – exchanges thoughts and information clearly and concisely to ensure understanding of intended messages.
self knowledge – personally explores and seeks feedback from others to understand talents, motivations, values, and developmental needs; utilizes understanding to improve performance and build relationships.
	 


qualifications:
a successful candidate will:

possess a bachelor of science degree in engineering from an accredited university.
have 5+ years of engineering experience in the oil and gas industry, but still acquiring higher level knowledge and capabilities.
have experience in pipeline operations.
have experience with hdd and other trenchless installation methods
have experience with geotechnical investigations and how they apply to trenchless pipeline installations
have experience with project engineering, construction and commissioning of natural gas pipelines, processing facilities, or related equipment.
have the ability to collaborate with operations technicians, safety specialists, and other frontline employees to identify and improve operational conditions.
excellent oral and written skills, the ability to organize, coordinate and negotiate solutions as well as skills in computer applications.
pe license preferred
