socialcare is currently seeking a principal data engineer to join our growing team. as a key member of our engineering team, working closely with our product development team, the principal data engineer at socialcare must be able to articulate complex architecture ideas into simple business use cases; employing their advanced understanding of programming to create intuitive and efficient models that scale with the organization’s needs.
the socialcare mission is to provide a secure, interoperable platform that streamlines the development of innovative applications that deliver value across clinical and business aspects of healthcare. connecting previously separate channels of information opens new and unseen possibilities.  the socialcare platform provides solutions to help healthcare providers achieve better outcomes in patient care and optimize revenue streams. this platform enables healthcare organizations to unlock access to existing data and empowers developers to accelerate innovative applications for faster time-to-market. 
socialcare is excited about the future of healthcare technology in austin, and we’re looking for the right additions to our team. below are more details on this role. 
what you’ll do:

proactively seek opportunities to come up with innovative solutions to cater challenging business problems through creative application of modeling, simulation and small-scale experimentation
proactively demonstrate leadership by advising and sharing knowledge and expert opinions with all levels of engineering and business staff. mentors and trains functional colleagues and assesses current and emerging business challenges enabling functional goal achievement
provide deeper understanding of process/product performance using appropriate modeling and simulation tools that are otherwise not amenable through simple experimentation
build and integrate mathematical models for a variety of analytics processes
ensure development of technologies that drive engineering excellence and scientific innovation that improve the future capabilities and productivity of the greater organization
play a strong technical leader in a cross-functional, collaborative environment
mentor less experienced engineers in the application of engineering and process development
source and identify emerging scientific trends from multiple internal and external sources and assess relevance. integrates trends into functional short-term objectives
advance cross-discipline technology and direction through generation of data

what you have:

demonstrated proficiency in taking analytics products from ideation to full-scale production environments
bachelors, masters, or phd in engineering or related field
proficiency in java, jenkins (or similar), mapreduce
understanding of large scale cluster environments, streaming and data virtualization.
proficiency in data encryption and masking
proficiency in batch processing of analytics aggregation
demonstrated leadership of technical teams and effective cross-functional communication
“big data” experience in the form of large, dimensional analytic data sets and transactional collection services
etl experience with extensive data validation
experience with dimensional models (facts and dimension organization)
experience with phi or pii data regulations and applicable technology
the ability to apply machine learning to solve analytics problems programmatically
strong communication and visual presentation skills for sharing ideas and vision with stakeholders

bonus points:

experience with emr platforms and various healthcare data integrations
experience with the fhir data spec
ability to communication ideas effectively and take direction as necessary
experience with hadoop, spark, kafka, elastic search, aws or other big data technologies
experience with phi de-identification/anonymization or ability to learn quickly
experience working with data science teams to productionize models and workflows

what you’ll get:

opportunity to make a meaningful industry and organizational impact, solving challenging real-world problems
work with a close-knit, world-class team that works together to solve tough problems and overcome obstacles
comprehensive compensation and full benefits package (health, dental and vision) with large employer contribution
flexible working hours/schedule options and four weeks of paid time off
generous stock incentive compensation plan 

**unfortunately, we do not offer visa sponsorship - please do not apply unless you are authorized to work in usa unencumbered.
