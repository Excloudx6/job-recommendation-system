additional information


purpose


                    this position is responsible for creating, maintaining, and updating standard reports as well as fulfilling ad hoc reporting reporting requests in collaboration with the reporting and analytics team.
                



essential functions


                    
                        develop and deliver reporting and analytics solutions designed to translate data into actionable business intelligence. consult with stakeholders to determine reporting needs and define reporting solutions to meet requirements. identify, retrieve, query, and append data from internal databases and external data sources in support of reporting and analytics operations. design and build dashboards to track kpis. cultivate and maintain expertise on alumni and donor data. conduct quantitative and qualitative analyses on a broad array of issues to inform strategic decision making. serve as a primary point of contact for support questions from the development user community. provide training and support to enable stakeholders to use reporting and analytics tools in effective and efficient ways.
                    
                



marginal/incidental functions


                    
                        other related functions as assigned.
                    
                



required qualifications


                    
                        bachelor's degree in computer or information science, mathematics, social and political sciences, or a related field. minimum of three years of directly related experience. advanced knowledge and experience in extracting data from relational databases. strong working knowledge of sql. experience in one or more data management, visualization, or analytics software tools such as tableau, r, spss, or sas.  desire and ability to learn new reporting technologies. strong communication and collaboration skills. ability to build and maintain cross functional relationships. experience working with confidential information. strong independent work ethic. excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills.
                        equivalent combination of relevant education and experience may be substituted as appropriate.
                    
                



preferred qualifications


                    experience writing reports or delivering data using sql. experience with programming for statistics through a software package such as sas, spss, or r (with a preference for r). a strong understanding of general linear modeling and other advanced predictive modeling techniques (random forest classification, monte carlo simulations, etc.). experience with dashboard design and deployment using tableau. previous experience or understanding of higher education development.
