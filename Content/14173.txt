qualities
every promethean embodies and strives for a certain set of qualities that makes us successful. we work as a team, we understand that there are no excuses, and we approach everything with a can-do attitude.  we seek to solve problems and simplify the work of our customers and each other.  we reject complacency, plan strategically, and execute with urgency. we do all of this while treating each other as friends and having fun!
impact
work with a small team to provide ongoing development support to the core products of prometheus group's business. this is a dynamic entry-level role that is a combination of reactive work (bug fixes, feature requests from customers) and roadmap work based on a planned release cycle. associate software developers have a chance to work on a wide variety of projects and with a diverse array of technologies and languages. the ideal candidate for this role will have excellent communication skills and the drive to tackle new challenges daily. associate software developers will have the opportunity for immediate growth within the team. 
responsibilities 

learning skills and techniques relevant to working within a sap environment
make enhancements to the software products as guided by their supervisor
maintain the outcome of their work in the development tracking system (jira)
support the professional services team when engaged, including providing direct development-level support for customers
supervising development interns when necessary

education

b.s. in computer science or equivalent degree

required

object-oriented programming (java, c#, c++)
experience with sql database development and interaction
ability and willingness to pick up new languages/technologies quickly

career paths
 the associate developer is an introductory position, the paths this position can lead to are pretty straight-forward, and at best are described as furthering your knowledge of an area. the indicated skills and the growth thereof should be the main focus of periodic reviews with your manager, whether bi-weekly or semi-annually.
development -developer

proficiency in object-oriented languages
white-box learning how the full-stack of your team’s software works
basic sap usage in core modules
time management and prioritization of tasks

qa -associate qa engineer, qa engineer

software engineering practices, including agile-like testing modules
black-box learning how the full-stack of a team’s software works
basic sap usage in core modules
technical writing

 why prometheus group?prometheus group consists of a great group of fun-loving, hard-working people that create and support enterprise software. our suite of software products simplifies the usability of sap plant maintenance by replacing manual and time-consuming processes. we listen to our customers and develop our tools alongside them to make sure we have products that make their lives better.  we help them achieve significant roi through cost savings, increased productivity and reporting!
