you might work on:development of tools to help automate and monitor the environmenttroubleshooting of problems in the teams own tools as well as provide second level supportprojects with other teams in market data what we're looking fora bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, mathematics or a related discipline or its foreign equivalent. relevant technical experience may be
