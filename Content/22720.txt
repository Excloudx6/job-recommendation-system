about ihs markit
ihs markit harnesses deep sources of information, analytics and expertise to forge solutions for the industries and markets that drive global economies. our company partners with clients in business, finance and government to provide the unrivaled insights and perspectives that lead to well-informed, confident decisions. we call this the new intelligence. ihs markit serves more than 50,000 key customers in more than 140 countries, including 80 percent of the fortune global 500.  we help decision makers apply higher-level thinking to daily tasks and strategic issues across a host of industries and disciplines including energy, finance, automotive, engineering, technology, maritime and trade, aerospace and defense, chemical, and economics and country risk. 

headquartered in london, ihs markit (nasdaq: info) is committed to sustainable, profitable growth.
_______________________________________________________________
 
 
internship opportunity
technical research/ data transformation
 
as an intern with ihs markit, you will undertake work on a real business project intended to add value to your team as well as provide you with a great learning opportunity. in this role, you will specifically support our latin america – frontier north america (latam – fna) team with data quality projects.
the latam – fna team is responsible for data sourcing, extraction, analysis, and processing for database entry. you will be working with regional managers, field researchers, well analysts, and contract analysts on project(s) intended to increase the completeness, correctness, consistency, and currency of the edin database.
the focus of this position will be to work closely with field researchers to review data which will be used to updated / create fields. the candidate will analyze the data from public domain as well as ihs markit in order to assess the fields. this will allow our group to continue to improve the data and provide the customers with a stronger data set. 
 
overview of the technical researcher function:
applies and integrates geological data from a variety of sources (both public and internal) in order to assist wells, fields and basin researchers in enhancing existing product offerings. the candidate will work closely with the african regional team receiving exposure to wells, fields, basin researcher and editorial workflows. in addition the candidate may partner with regional experts on a bespoke research topic for presentation at an industry conference.        
 
role requirements:
to be considered for this opportunity, candidates should demonstrate the following education, skills and experience

currently pursuing a degree in the geosciences
good communication skills to include writing, interpersonal and listening.
good organizational skills with the ability to handle multiple tasks and effectively prioritize.
ability to analyze, assess and interpret information.
demonstrates flexibility and the ability to creatively problem-solve.
strong database skills.
enthusiastic team player willing to learn and manage challenges.
work as a technical resource for management on special projects and quality improvement initiatives.
understand and learn the quality processes of the team
excel skills and proficiency with other ms applications (word and powerpoint)
gis application skills
the ability to work to deadlines and prioritize a complex workload
strong analytical and quality control skills
strong attention to detail

 
other skills:
 

ability to work well in a team or individually
good presentation skills
ability to present solutions to complex problems in simple understandable terms
some understanding of spanish or portuguese.
proficient in python, r, vba, sql or a similar programing language
proficient in data analysis software such as tableau, spotfire, power bi, etc.

 
2018 summer intern requirements:
to be eligible to apply for a place on our intern program, you must be able to answer ‘yes’ to the following questions:

are you legally eligible to work for the duration of the intern program in the country for which you have applied?
will you have completed your studies in either 2019 or 2020?  this program is open to current students only.
if you do not reside in the ihs markit office location for which you are applying, are you able to self-relocate in time for the program start date?

 
program dates:
participation for the full duration of the 10-week program is expected. please ensure you are available as per the dates below before submitting your application:

north america – june 04 to august 10
apac – may 21 to july 27
emea – june 4 to august 10
india – may 21 to july 27
