role description
as an associate software engineer, you’ll collaborate on cross-functional project teams to solve some of today’s most pressing business problems for fortune 500 companies. you’ll own the full stack technical implementation of large scale innovative solutions, and you’ll help build our intellectual property portfolio by being a key contributor to our cloud-based big data analytics platform.
who you are

excel in a highly collaborative team environment with a diverse set of teams and perspectives
enjoy interacting directly with clients
committed to finding creative solutions to complex business problems
possess strong computer science fundamentals in data structures, algorithm design, problem solving, and complexity analysis
in-depth knowledge of at least one modern programming language such as java, c#, r, python
demonstrated interest in data science, etl/elt, machine learning, cloud computing
understanding of the importance of unit tests and a desire to implement them
knowledge of modern open source tools and frameworks
a strong distaste for performing repetitive tasks and seeing others do the same

why join revenue analytics?
because you have exceptional talents in business strategy, math and statistics, or software engineering and you want to be part of a fun and highly collaborative team that creates analytical solutions to eliminate the unknowns in business problems for world-renowned companies.
outstanding benefits & perks

competitive market based salary + incentive bonus
paid medical insurance, dental and vision coverage for both you and your family
401k plan with immediate vesting and generous employer match
paid time off includes 3 weeks’ vacation, 5-6 national holidays, 5 days for winter holiday break and paid sick time
company issued cell phone and laptop to employee
flexible office hours
defined career path with a coach to foster your growth
life insurance, long and short term disability coverage
paid maternity leave for new mothers and paternity leave for new fathers
paid immigration and sponsorship processing for foreign nationals
