description

the role
tesla is looking for an exceptional data analyst to impact the reliability engineering team. this is a great opportunity to get on the ground level of data infrastructure development for tesla!
requirements

strong sql, comfortable writing queries, data processing scripts, and understanding rdbms data structures
strong python, especially in a data analytics/science capacity where pandas/numpy are used heavily
able to visualize data effectively
	
python (matplotlib/seaborn/plotly/etc)
tableau (or, similar bi tools)
any open source/freeware visualization tools (e.g., d3, ggplot)


sound understanding of basic statistics and the ability to apply statistical reasoning to analyses
mathematical maturity
ability to quickly learn new technologies
desire to produce clean, pretty, reusable code, and compelling visualizations and analyses

plus

exposure to "big data" technologies, particularly any part of a spark, hbase, hadoop stack
experience with statistical and numeric methods, especially in python (numpy/scipy/sklearn)
