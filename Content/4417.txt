intern - print supplies big data
apply
job id 3018666
location
    vancouver, washington
date posted 10/27/2017
"big data" is more than a buzzword, it's a growing industry made up of data analysts and data scientists that work through complexity to drive insight and action.  at hp, we're embracing big data, harnessing the opportunities it affords to better understand our customers, and leveraging the insights big data provides to profitably grow our business. our data science and analytics teams focus on a variety of projects, from core big data science to more complex architecture and advance machine learning.  if you like to work on challenging projects and cutting-edge technology, this is the right place for you. your role will help us build prototype machine learning capabilities to enable us to better understand our data, to make informed product decisions, and to better understand our customers and their interests.  the ideal candidate is a team player, but also an independent thinker. this role is ideal for someone who has a thirst to understand what is happening in our data and how to extract insights from it.  we want someone who is as excited to learn new things as he/she is to work on a team and help teach others.education and experience required: must have completed a bachelor's degreecurrently enrolled in master's degree program (preferably in computer science, software engineering, mathematics, analytics, or a closely related field)proficiency with ms excel and sqlstrong knowledge of relational databasesaptitude in programming (python, scala, r, or java)strong attention to detail; good organization skillsability to solve problems independently
