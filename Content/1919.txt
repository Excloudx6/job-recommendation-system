data analytics engineer

        at nutonomy

        boston, ma or santa monica, ca
      


nutonomy aims to be the first company in the world to launch an autonomous taxi system, and we are building up an awesome team to make this goal a reality. we are developing the first-of-its-kind complete solution for providing point-to-point mobility via large fleets of autonomous vehicles. this includes software for autonomous vehicles, smartphone-based ride hailing, fleet management, and teleoperation. the company's software has been tested extensively on public roads in the u.s. and singapore. we offer a unique opportunity to work closely with experts from a wide array of backgrounds, to create ground-breaking technology with potential for huge impact. as a member of a fast-growing start-up, you will be able to make a large contribution to the final product. for more information about nutonomy, visit: www.nutonomy.com
 

we are seeking a highly talented data/analytics engineer in santa monica, ca or boston, ma to support a variety of teams and projects, including the following:

help define useful metrics to quantify performances of core vehicle software systems
provide estimators of such metrics based on data
provide insights and bounds on the amount of data required to be reasonably confident about the estimation
mine for “outlier” cases
leverage metrics to identify regressions and validate improvements in our software

things you’ll work on:

design machine learning algorithms from scratch
translate generated algorithms into production-level code
define system documentation, metrics, measurements
collaborate with various teams to achieve objectives in improving the system with more data


education and skills you’ll likely have:  

practical experience in data science, statistics, and analysis of large data sets
fluency in in a modern programming language, such as c++, python, r, or julia (and their related data processing, machine learning, and visualization libraries)
ma in data science or similar preferred

bonus points:

experience using data modeling tools
familiarity with autonomous robotics and vehicle systems
