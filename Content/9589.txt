data quality analyst intern, supply chain
date: 
dec 5, 2017
                


location: 
charlotte, nc, us, 28601

req #: 
13683



corning is one of the world’s leading innovators in materials science. for more than 160 years, corning has applied its unparalleled expertise in specialty glass, ceramics, and optical physics to develop products that have created new industries and transformed people’s lives.

corning succeeds through sustained investment in r&d, a unique combination of material and process innovation, and close collaboration with customers to solve tough technology challenges.

our optical communications segment has recently evolved from being a manufacturer of optical fiber and cable, hardware and equipment to being a comprehensive provider of industry-leading optical solutions across the broader communications industry.this segment is classified into two main product groupings – carrier network and enterprise network. the carrier network product group consists primarily of products and solutions for optical-based communications infrastructure for services such as video, data and voice communications. the enterprise network product group consists primarily of optical-based communication networks sold to businesses, governments and individuals for their own use.
scope of position: 
 
the responsibility of a supply chain data governance summer intern is to learn and apply scm best practices and processes to a given project as it relates to data in the erp system. on the-job training on overall data governance and quality processes and tools to improve supply chain efficiencies.
 
day to day responsibilities:

to apply supply chain skills and knowledge to their assigned project or projects.
ability to apply basic data analysis skills to help improve data quality
able to communicate with other departments to address process/data issues
the candidate should be able to utilize and build on solid supply chain fundamentals to complete his/her tasks.

 
required education:

pursuing a degree in supply chain management, business, information systems or equivalent.
prefer candidates pursuing a degree in supply chain management

 
required skills: 

advanced excel skills (i.e. vlookups, macros, charts, graphs, etc.)
data analysis skills
communication skills  – verbal and written
demonstrates drive and commitment
ability to comprehend, analyze, and solve complex problems
ability to work independently in a dynamic, business/manufacturing environment
computer literate in ms office

 
desired skills:

sap knowledge & experience (or other erp system)
project management
ability to prioritize and complete multiple tasks
adaptable and flexible
able to work in a team environment

 
soft skills:

fluent in english 
adaptable and flexible
able to work in a team environment
