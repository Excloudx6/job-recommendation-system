requisition id: bus002416merck & co., inc. kenilworth, n.j., u.s.a. known as merck in the united states and canada, is a global health care leader with a diversified portfolio of prescription medicines, vaccines and animal health products. the difference between potential and achievement lies in the spark that fuels innovation and inventiveness; this is the space where merck has codified its legacy for over a century. merck’s success is backed by ethical integrity, forward momentum, and an inspiring mission to achieve new milestones in global healthcare.
merck’s information technology organization partners with colleagues across the business to help serve our patients and customers around the world. ours is a high energy team of dynamic, innovative individuals dedicated to advancing merck’s contribution to global medical innovation by leveraging information and technology to efficiently advance the business by driving revenue and productivity.  
 
the business/data analyst will work as a member of the mmd it analytics team. the business/data analyst will possess/acquire sound understanding of key processes in the plan, source, make, deliver functions of manufacturing. under the direction of the account manager, the incumbent will be responsible for working with business stakeholders to facilitate discussions that elicit analytical requirements supporting business processes. (s)he will focus on transforming data/information into insights and actions to help seize business opportunities and solve pressing business challenges. this role also involves working with platform and client service leaders to help develop solutions satisfying these needs. 
 
major responsibilities may include but are not limited to: 


gain deep subject-matter expertise with the key mmd business processes, systems, and data. 

organize and participate in scoping discussions for projects in the mmd-it analytics portfolio. 

elicit business, functional, and user requirements for analytical solutions using agile/iterative methods. 

develop prototypes in spotfire, qlik sense and other technologies to expose & confirm requirements. 

collaborate with various it teams to help ensure alignment between business needs and solutions. 

serve as primary contact point for questions on business requirements and related questions. 

contribute to development and execution of standard sdlc deliverables such as requirements, system testing, user acceptance testing, and change management deliverables. 
the incumbent must work closely with various stakeholders across mmd, mmd-it, and the broader it community to understand key business requirements and align solutions to business and it strategy.qualificationseducation: 


ba/bs in business management, software engineering, computer science or related field,
required: 


strong experience working with data, developing analyses, and communicating results. 

demonstrated ability to analyze and define business problems, develop business cases, and identify appropriate process and technology solutions.

ability to work in an ambiguous, ever-changing environment where flexibility and adaptability are highly valued. 

experience with developing and defining business processes, key performance indicators, and business capabilities. 

excellent communication and interpersonal skills, including collaboration, facilitation and negotiation. 

strong continuous improvement focus and mindset. 
preferred: 


experience in merck manufacturing processes and systems. 

experience with rapid prototyping or working with agile methodologies such as scrum. 

an appreciation for advanced analytical techniques such as machine learning, simulation, statistical modeling, etc. 

knowledge of merck sigma
