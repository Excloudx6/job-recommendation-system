job description




operating as an internal startup, de&p’s technology and product development (t&pd) team is looking for self-starting and tech-savvy data scientist to join us at our bellevue, wa office. your role will be to help support the design and development of descriptive and predictive models, machine learning and optimization algorithms to provide actionable insight of big data for the de&p in order to create commercial value and competitive advantage through data and information. you will be a key member of t&pd team and will have a significant role in building and maintaining world class advanced data analytics and modelling solutions for de&p. 

processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of data used for analytics
enhancing data collection procedures to include information that is relevant for building analytic systems
extending company’s data with third party sources of information when needed
data mining using state-of-the art methods and produce actionable insight
selecting features, building and optimizing classifiers using machine learning techniques
design and develop predictive models and machine learning algorithms using advanced methodologies
provide detailed analytics reports, presentations, and documentations





requirements





strong background in advanced statistical modeling and machine learning techniques
strong background in time-series forecasting methodologies
excellent background in linear and non-linear programming
demonstrated ability to conduct data mining, design of experiments, and develop predictive models and machine learning algorithms
strong programming skills, at least being efficient with one of low level languages, c++/java, and one of scripting languages, python or r
excellent working knowledge of sql, hive, and hadoop environment
great written and oral communication skills
ability to adapt and learn quickly in a changing and fast-paced environment
knowledge of power and energy systems, such as wholesale power markets (pjm, iso-ne, miso, caiso, etc.), power systems analysis and economics, demand response, distributed generation is advantageous
2-3 years of professional experience or academic equivalent
experience in energy industry is desired but not essential
experience of agile software development is desired
proven ability to complete projects and achieve results in an ambiguous work environment
experience using visualization techniques for presenting data and analysis
proven track record of delivering high quality analytics insights and solutions
experience communicating with diverse teams including data scientists, engineers, and product managers is desired
require master’s degree in a quantitative field such as, computer science, statistics, applied mathematics, econometrics, operation research, or similar
ph.d. is preferred





responsibilities





processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of data used for analytics

data mining using state-of-the art methods and produce actionable insight

design and develop predictive models and machine learning algorithms using advanced methodologies
provide detailed analytics reports, presentations, and documentations
